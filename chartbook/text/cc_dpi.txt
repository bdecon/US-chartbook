Consumer credit totals \$4.32 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in June 2021. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 4.2 percent, while after-tax income increased by 0.8 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 0.8 percentage point. In June 2021, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.1 percent of annualized June 2021 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
