In the monthly measure, consumer credit totals \$4.44 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in January 2022. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 6.1 percent, while after-tax income decreased by 4.4 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 2.4 percentage points. In January 2022, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.3 percent of annualized January 2022 disposable income (see {\color{green!60!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
