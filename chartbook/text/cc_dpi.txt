In the monthly measure, consumer credit totals \$4.78 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in December 2022. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 7.8 percent, while after-tax income increased by 3.2 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 1.1 percentage points. In December 2022, total consumer credit is equivalent to 25.2 percent of annualized December 2022 disposable income (see {\color{green!60!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
