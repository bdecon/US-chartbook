Consumer credit totals \$4.16 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in October 2020. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 0.3 percent, while after-tax income increased by 6.2 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 1.4 percentage points. In October 2020, total consumer credit is equivalent to 23.8 percent of annualized October 2020 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
