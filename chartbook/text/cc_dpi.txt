In the monthly measure, consumer credit totals \$5.06 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in May 2024. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by two percent, while after-tax income increased by 3.6 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 0.3 percentage point. In May 2024, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.2 percent of annualized May 2024 disposable income (see {\color{blue!50!black}\textbf{---}}).
