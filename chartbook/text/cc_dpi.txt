In the monthly measure, consumer credit totals \$4.99 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in October 2023. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 3.1 percent, while after-tax income increased by seven percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 0.9 percentage point. In October 2023, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.4 percent of annualized October 2023 disposable income (see {\color{blue!50!black}\textbf{---}}).
