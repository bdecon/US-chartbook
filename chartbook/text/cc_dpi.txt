Consumer credit totals \$4.24 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in April 2021. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 2.4 percent, while after-tax income decreased by 0.9 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 0.7 percentage point. In April 2021, total consumer credit is equivalent to 22.5 percent of annualized April 2021 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
