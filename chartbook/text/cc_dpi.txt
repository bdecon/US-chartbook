In the monthly measure, consumer credit totals \$4.85 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in March 2023. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 7.3 percent, while after-tax income increased by 8.3 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 0.2 percentage point. In March 2023, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.5 percent of annualized March 2023 disposable income (see {\color{green!60!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
