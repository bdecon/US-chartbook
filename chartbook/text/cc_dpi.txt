Consumer credit totals \$4.14 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in August 2020. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 0.4 percent, while after-tax income increased by 5.4 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 1.2 percentage points. In August 2020, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.0 percent of annualized August 2020 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
