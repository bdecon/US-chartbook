Consumer credit totals \$4.12 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in June 2020. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 0.9 percent, while after-tax income increased by 9.5 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 2.0 percentage points. In June 2020, total consumer credit is equivalent to 23.1 percent of annualized June 2020 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
