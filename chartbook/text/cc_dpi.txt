In the monthly measure, consumer credit totals \$4.76 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in November 2022. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 7.9 percent, while after-tax income increased by 3.2 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 1.1 percentage points. In November 2022, total consumer credit is equivalent to 25.1 percent of annualized November 2022 disposable income (see {\color{green!60!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
