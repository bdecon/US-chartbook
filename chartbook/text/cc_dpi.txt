Consumer credit totals \$4.28 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in May 2021. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 3.7 percent, while after-tax income increased by 1.5 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 0.5 percentage point. In May 2021, total consumer credit is equivalent to 23.2 percent of annualized May 2021 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
