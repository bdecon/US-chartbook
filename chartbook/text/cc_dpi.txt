Consumer credit totals \$4.16 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in September 2020. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 0.6 percent, while after-tax income increased by 6.9 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income decreased by a total of 1.5 percentage points. In September 2020, total consumer credit is equivalent to 23.7 percent of annualized September 2020 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
