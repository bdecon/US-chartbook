Consumer credit totals \$4.38 trillion US dollars on a seasonally-adjusted and annualized basis in October 2021. Over the past year, consumer credit increased by 5.2 percent, while after-tax income increased by 4.1 percent. As a result, the ratio of consumer credit to disposable income increased by a total of 0.3 percentage point. In October 2021, total consumer credit is equivalent to 24.2 percent of annualized October 2021 disposable income (see {\color{green!70!blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}).
