Housing prices increased 7.8 percent over the year ending August 2022, far above the pre-COVID rate of 2.9 percent (the average monthly rate during 2019). Medical care prices increased 5.4 percent, these prices grew at an average rate of 2.8 percent during 2019. In contrast, prices of food consumed at home (groceries) increased 13.5 percent in the year ending August 2022 compared to 0.9 percent during 2019.

Transportation prices increased 13.4 percent over the year ending August 2022, far above the pre-COVID 0.3 percent decrease. Energy prices increased 23.8 percent over the year, compared to an average 2.1 percent decrease on average in 2019. Energy prices are historically more volatile than other categories.
