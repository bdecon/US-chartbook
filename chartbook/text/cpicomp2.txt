Housing prices increased 4.8 percent over the year ending December 2023, substantially above the pre-COVID rate of 2.9 percent (the average monthly rate during 2019). Medical care prices increased 0.5 percent, these prices grew at an average rate of 2.8 percent during 2019. In contrast, prices of food consumed at home (groceries) increased 1.3 percent in the year ending December 2023 compared to 0.9 percent during 2019.

Transportation prices increased 2.9 percent over the year ending December 2023, far above the pre-COVID 0.3 percent decrease. Energy prices decreased two percent over the year, compared to an average 2.1 percent decrease on average in 2019. Energy prices are historically more volatile than other categories.
