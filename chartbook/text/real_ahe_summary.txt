Hourly wages for production and non-supervisory workers in private goods-producing sectors average \$30.82 in April 2024 (see {\color{cyan!80!green!92!blue}\textbf{---}}). In April 2019, the average hourly wage for the sector is \$30.15, after adjusting for inflation.

Private service-providing industry wages average \$29.65 for production and non-supervisory workers in April 2024. The inflation-adjusted equivalent is \$28.30 in April 2019 (see {\color{blue!70!violet}\textbf{---}}).
