Hourly wages for production and non-supervisory workers in private goods-producing sectors average \$29.97 in October 2023 (see {\color{cyan!80!green!92!blue}\textbf{---}}). In October 2019, the average hourly wage for the sector is \$29.84, after adjusting for inflation.

Private service-providing industry wages average \$29.02 for production and non-supervisory workers in October 2023. The inflation-adjusted equivalent is \$28.10 in October 2019 (see {\color{blue!70!violet}\textbf{---}}).
