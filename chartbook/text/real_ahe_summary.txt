Hourly wages for production and non-supervisory workers in private goods-producing sectors average \$29.86 in September 2023 (see {\color{cyan!80!green!92!blue}\textbf{---}}). In September 2019, the average hourly wage for the sector is \$29.82, after adjusting for inflation.

Private service-providing industry wages average \$28.90 for production and non-supervisory workers in September 2023. The inflation-adjusted equivalent is \$28.07 in September 2019 (see {\color{blue!70!violet}\textbf{---}}).
