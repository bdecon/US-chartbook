Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$27,340 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2023 Q3 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to an inflation-adjusted \$27,177 in 2023 Q2, and \$26,122 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 4.7 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$13,092 per person in 2023 Q3 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$12,987 in 2023 Q2, and \$12,818 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$14,773 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
