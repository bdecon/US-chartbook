Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$20,364 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2020 Q3 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$18,198 in 2020 Q2, and \$22,994 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 11.4 percent since 2019 Q4. Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$11,203 per person in 2020 Q3 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$10,889 in 2020 Q2, and \$10,997 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$12,124 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
