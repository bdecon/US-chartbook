Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$26,850 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2023 Q2 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$26,745 in 2023 Q1, and \$25,625 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 4.8 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$12,605 per person in 2023 Q2 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$12,586 in 2023 Q1, and \$12,652 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$14,601 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
