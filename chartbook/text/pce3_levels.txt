Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$17,887 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2020 Q2 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$21,980 in 2020 Q1, and \$22,793 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 21.5 percent since 2019 Q4. Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$10,778 per person in 2020 Q2 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$10,997 in 2020 Q1, and \$10,903 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$11,997 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
