Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$25,994 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$25,848 in 2022 Q3, and \$25,075 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 3.7 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$12,384 per person in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$12,614 in 2022 Q3, and \$12,424 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$14,410 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
