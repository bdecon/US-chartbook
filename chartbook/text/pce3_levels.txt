Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$23,098 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2021 Q3 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$22,571 in 2021 Q2, and \$23,943 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 3.5 percent since 2019 Q4. Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$11,681 per person in 2021 Q3 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$11,739 in 2021 Q2, and \$11,229 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$12,864 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
