Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$25,922 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$25,870 in 2022 Q3, and \$25,096 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 3.3 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$12,400 per person in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$12,603 in 2022 Q3, and \$12,412 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$14,386 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
