Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$22,353 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2021 Q2 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$21,529 in 2021 Q1, and \$23,691 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 5.6 percent since 2019 Q4. Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$11,568 per person in 2021 Q2 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$11,643 in 2021 Q1, and \$11,045 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$12,619 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
