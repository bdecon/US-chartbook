Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$23,143 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2021 Q3 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$22,578 in 2021 Q2, and \$23,950 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 3.4 percent since 2019 Q4. Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$11,700 per person in 2021 Q3 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$11,743 in 2021 Q2, and \$11,232 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$12,869 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
