Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$26,447 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2023 Q1 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$26,251 in 2022 Q4, and \$25,414 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 4.1 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$12,502 per person in 2023 Q1 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$12,569 in 2022 Q4, and \$12,576 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$14,536 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
