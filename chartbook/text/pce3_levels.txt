Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$28,329 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2024 Q1 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to an inflation-adjusted \$28,026 in 2023 Q4, and \$26,576 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 6.6 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$13,560 per person in 2024 Q1 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$13,421 in 2023 Q4, and \$13,127 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$15,099 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
