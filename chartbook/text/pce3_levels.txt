Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$20,772 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2020 Q4 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$20,505 in 2020 Q3, and \$23,123 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 10.2 percent since 2019 Q4. Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$11,475 per person in 2020 Q4 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$11,292 in 2020 Q3, and \$11,084 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$12,232 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
