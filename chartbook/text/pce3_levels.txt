Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$28,665 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2024 Q2 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to an inflation-adjusted \$28,521 in 2024 Q1, and \$26,802 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased seven percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$13,700 per person in 2024 Q2 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$13,689 in 2024 Q1, and \$13,257 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$15,242 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
