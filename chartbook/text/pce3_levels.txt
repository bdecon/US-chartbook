Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$25,990 per person, on an annualized basis, in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$25,863 in 2022 Q3, and \$25,089 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has increased 3.6 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$12,396 per person in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$12,615 in 2022 Q3, and \$12,425 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$14,406 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
