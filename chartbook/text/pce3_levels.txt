Consumer spending on services other than housing and utilities totals \$23,696 per person, on an annaulized basis, in 2021 Q4 (see {\color{blue!75!white}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$23,351 in 2021 Q3, and \$24,166 in 2019 Q4. Spending on non-housing services has decreased 1.9 percent since 2019 Q4. 

Shelter costs, which combine housing, utilities, and residential fixed investment, are \$11,889 per person in 2021 Q4 (see {\color{green!85!blue}\textbf{---}}), \$11,901 in 2021 Q3, and \$11,424 in 2019 Q4. Shelter spending peaked at \$13,099 per person in the third quarter of 2005, during the housing bubble.
