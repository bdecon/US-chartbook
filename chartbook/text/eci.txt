In the first quarter of 2023, private industry wage and salary costs increased by 5.1 percent (one-year percent change, see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}), following an increase of 5.1 percent in 2022 Q4, and an increase of 5.2 percent in 2022 Q3. In 2019, private wages and salaries costs increased by three percent, on average.

The cost of private sector benefits increased by 4.3 percent (see {\color{cyan!40!blue}\textbf{---}}) over the year ending 2023 Q1, following an increase of 4.8 percent in 2022 Q4. In 2019, private-sector benefits costs increased by two percent, on average.
