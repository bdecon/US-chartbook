In the fourth quarter of 2022, private industry wage and salary costs increased by 5.1 percent (one-year percent change, see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}), following an increase of 5.2 percent in 2022 Q3, and an increase of 5.7 percent in 2022 Q2. In 2019, private wages and salaries costs increased by three percent, on average.

The cost of private sector benefits increased by 4.8 percent (see {\color{cyan!40!blue}\textbf{---}}) over the year ending 2022 Q4, following an increase of five percent in 2022 Q3. In 2019, private-sector benefits costs increased by two percent, on average.
