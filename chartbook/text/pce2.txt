In the the first quarter of 2020, household spending on goods subtracted 0.27 percentage point from GDP growth, household spending on services other than housing and utilities subtracted 5.70 percentage points, and shelter spending and investment contributed 0.82 percentage point. Spending on health care services subtracted 2.25 percentage points from GDP growth in 2020 Q1 and has contributed 0.12 percentage point, on average, over the past three years.
