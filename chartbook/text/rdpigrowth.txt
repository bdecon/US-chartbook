The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of 3.4 percent in August 2020, 7.7 percent in July 2020, and 1.3 percent in August 2019. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 4.1 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.4 percent.
