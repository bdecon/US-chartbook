The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person of -3.2 percent in September 2022, -4.6 percent in August 2022, and -1.5 percent in September 2021 (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, the measure has averaged -5.4 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.6 percent.
