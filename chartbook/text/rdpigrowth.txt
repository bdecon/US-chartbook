The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of -10.1 percent in January 2022, -0.3 percent in December 2021, and 13.3 percent in January 2021. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 1.4 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.2 percent.
