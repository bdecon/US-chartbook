The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person of 0.5 percent in April 2024, 0.8 percent in March 2024, and 4.0 percent in April 2023 (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, the measure has averaged 2.9 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.7 percent.
