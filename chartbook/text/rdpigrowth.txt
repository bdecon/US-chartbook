The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person of 2.8 percent in February 2023, 2.5 percent in January 2023, and -3.7 percent in February 2022 (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, the measure has averaged -4.3 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.6 percent.
