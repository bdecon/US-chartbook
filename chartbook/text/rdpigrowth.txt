The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person of -2.8 percent in November 2022, -3.3 percent in October 2022, and -0.0 percent in November 2021 (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, the measure has averaged -5.8 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.6 percent.
