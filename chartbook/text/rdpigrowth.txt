The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of -4.8 percent in April 2021, 29.6 percent in March 2021, and 16.1 percent in April 2020. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 8.1 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.4 percent.
