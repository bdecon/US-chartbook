The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of 4.5 percent in October 2020, 5.3 percent in September 2020, and 1.3 percent in October 2019. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 5.0 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.4 percent.
