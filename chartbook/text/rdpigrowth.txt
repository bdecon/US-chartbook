The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of -3.9 percent in July 2021, -4.0 percent in June 2021, and 9.3 percent in July 2020. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 4.1 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.3 percent.
