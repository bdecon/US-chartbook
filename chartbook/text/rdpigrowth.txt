The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of 3.0 percent in February 2021, 12.8 percent in January 2021, and 1.5 percent in February 2020. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 6.0 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.4 percent.
