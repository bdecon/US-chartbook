The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/income-saving/personal-income}{report} an inflation-adjusted one-year change in after-tax income per person (see {\color{blue!80!cyan}\textbf{---}}) of -2.3 percent in September 2021, -0.1 percent in August 2021, and 5.1 percent in September 2020. Over the past year, the measure has averaged 2.9 percent. During the three years before the COVID-19 pandemic, per capita after-tax income grew at an average annual rate of 2.3 percent.
