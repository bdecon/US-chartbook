In December 2021, 7,461,000 people are working more than one job, equivalent to 4.8 percent of workers (see {\color{cyan!50!blue}\textbf{---}}), the highest level since February 2020 and slightly below the February 2020 rate of 5.2 percent. The multiple jobholder share of workers peaked at 6.6 percent in November 1996.
