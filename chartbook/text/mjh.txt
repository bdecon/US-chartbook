In October 2021, 7,166,000 people are working more than one job, equivalent to 4.6 percent of workers (see {\color{cyan!50!blue}\textbf{---}}), the highest level since May 2021 and substantially below the February 2020 rate of 5.2 percent. The multiple jobholder share of workers peaked at 6.6 percent in November 1996.
