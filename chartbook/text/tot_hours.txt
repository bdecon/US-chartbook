Total hours worked in nonfarm businesses increased at an annual rate of one percent in 2024 Q1, following increases of 0.2 percent in 2023 Q4 and 1.1 percent in 2023 Q3. From 2017 through 2019, total hours worked increased at an annual rate of 1.3 percent. Since 2019, hours worked have increased by 0.7 percent per year.
