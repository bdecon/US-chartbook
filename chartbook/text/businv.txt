Business fixed investment contributed 2.88 percentage points to GDP growth in 2020 Q3, far above the average contribution of 0.30 percentage point over the three years prior to the pandemic. In 2020 Q3, investment in structures subtracted 0.43 percentage point from GDP growth (see\cbox{yellow!50!orange}), investment in equipment contributed 3.34 percentage points (see\cbox{cyan!60!white}), and investment in intellectual property products subtracted 0.03 percentage point (see\cbox{violet}).
