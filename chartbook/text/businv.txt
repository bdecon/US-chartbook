Business fixed investment contributed 1.29 percentage points to GDP growth in 2021 Q1, substantially above the average contribution of 0.44 percentage point over the three years prior to the pandemic. In 2021 Q1, investment in structures subtracted 0.12 percentage point from GDP growth (see\cbox{yellow!50!orange}), investment in equipment contributed 0.93 percentage point (see\cbox{cyan!60!white}), and investment in intellectual property products contributed 0.48 percentage point (see\cbox{violet}).
