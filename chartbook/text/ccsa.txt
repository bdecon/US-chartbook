For the week ending May 22, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,504,163 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 22,900. One year prior, during the week of May 23, 2020, there were an average of 19,118,900 insured unemployed.
