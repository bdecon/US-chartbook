For the week ending July 10, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,249,777 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 106,100. One year prior, during the week of July 11, 2020, there were an average of 16,396,700 insured unemployed.
