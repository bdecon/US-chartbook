For the week ending February 13, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 4,828,027 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 143,300.
