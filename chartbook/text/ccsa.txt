For the week ending October 8, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 1,201,137 \textbf{continued claims} for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), virtually unchanged from the previous week. One year prior, during the week of October 9, 2021, there were an average of 2,168,200 insured unemployed.
