For the week ending December 26, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 5,382,459 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 145,400. One year prior, during the week of December 28, 2019, there were an average of 2,147,200 insured unemployed.
