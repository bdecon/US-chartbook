For the week ending April 17, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,790,527 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 49,900.
