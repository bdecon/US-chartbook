For the week ending September 5, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 12,321,395 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 1,034,100. One year prior, during the week of September 7, 2019, there were an average of 1,465,600 insured unemployed.
