For the week ending October 3, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 9,631,588 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 1,188,200.
