For the week ending November 12, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 1,341,269 \textbf{continued claims} for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 107,500. One year prior, during the week of November 13, 2021, there were an average of 1,820,600 insured unemployed.
