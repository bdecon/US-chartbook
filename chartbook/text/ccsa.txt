For the week ending March 6, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 4,486,389 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 95,500. One year prior, during the week of March 7, 2020, there were an average of 1,977,300 insured unemployed.
