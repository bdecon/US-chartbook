For the week ending June 19, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,270,789 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 37,500. One year prior, during the week of June 20, 2020, there were an average of 17,518,700 insured unemployed.
