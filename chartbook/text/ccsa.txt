For the week ending June 12, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,210,285 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 94,100. One year prior, during the week of June 13, 2020, there were an average of 17,823,800 insured unemployed.
