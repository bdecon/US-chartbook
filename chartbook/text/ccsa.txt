For the week ending October 1, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 1,205,534 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 27,500. One year prior, during the week of October 2, 2021, there were an average of 2,262,800 insured unemployed.
