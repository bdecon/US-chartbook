For the week ending December 19, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 5,258,073 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 171,000. One year prior, during the week of December 21, 2019, there were an average of 1,776,300 insured unemployed.
