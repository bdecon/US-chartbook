For the week ending February 12, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 1,912,532 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 57,700.
