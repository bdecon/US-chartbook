For the week ending July 3, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,119,473 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 144,500. One year prior, during the week of July 4, 2020, there were an average of 17,393,800 insured unemployed.
