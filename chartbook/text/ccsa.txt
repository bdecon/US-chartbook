For the week ending July 4, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 17,355,176 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed; see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 838,300. One year prior, during the week of July 6, 2019, there were an average of 1,702,700 insured unemployed.
