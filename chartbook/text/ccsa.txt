For the week ending August 22, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 13,104,366 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed; see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 764,700. One year prior, during the week of August 24, 2019, there were an average of 1,566,900 insured unemployed.
