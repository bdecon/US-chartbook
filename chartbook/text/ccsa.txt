For the week ending December 31, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 1,873,668 \textbf{continued claims} for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 163,100. One year prior, during the week of January 1, 2022, there were an average of 2,053,300 insured unemployed.
