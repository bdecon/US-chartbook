For the week ending October 22, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 1,238,290 \textbf{continued claims} for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 11,400. One year prior, during the week of October 23, 2021, there were an average of 1,885,000 insured unemployed.
