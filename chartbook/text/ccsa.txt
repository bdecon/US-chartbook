For the week ending August 7, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 2,763,782 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 86,600. One year prior, during the week of August 8, 2020, there were an average of 14,155,600 insured unemployed.
