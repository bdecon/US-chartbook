For the week ending November 28, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 5,780,893 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 533,300. One year prior, during the week of November 30, 2019, there were an average of 1,754,600 insured unemployed.
