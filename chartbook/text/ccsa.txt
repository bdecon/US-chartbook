For the week ending October 29, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 1,263,154 \textbf{continued claims} for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 23,200. One year prior, during the week of October 30, 2021, there were an average of 1,952,900 insured unemployed.
