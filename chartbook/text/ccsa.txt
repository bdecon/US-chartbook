For the week ending April 10, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,862,890 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 50,800. One year prior, during the week of April 11, 2020, there were an average of 16,257,200 insured unemployed.
