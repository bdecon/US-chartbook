For the week ending February 20, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 4,806,269 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 22,400. One year prior, during the week of February 22, 2020, there were an average of 2,105,500 insured unemployed.
