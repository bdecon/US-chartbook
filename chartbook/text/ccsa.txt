For the week ending June 27, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 16,797,353 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed, see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 630,600. One year prior, during the week of June 29, 2019, there were an average of 1,610,600 insured unemployed.
