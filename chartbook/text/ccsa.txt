For the week ending January 1, 2022, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 2,056,818 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 187,100. One year prior, during the week of January 2, 2021, there were an average of 5,756,900 insured unemployed.
