For the week ending June 13, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 17,921,282 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed, see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 501,300. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 1,535,300 insured unemployed.
