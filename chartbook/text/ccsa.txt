For the week ending July 11, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 16,390,919 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed; see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 930,300. One year prior, during the week of July 13, 2019, there were an average of 1,697,500 insured unemployed.
