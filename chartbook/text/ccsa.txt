For the week ending May 8, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,684,082 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 10,300. One year prior, during the week of May 9, 2020, there were an average of 23,038,000 insured unemployed.
