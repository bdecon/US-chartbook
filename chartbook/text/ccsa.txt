For the week ending March 27, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 4,031,531 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 67,700. One year prior, during the week of March 28, 2020, there were an average of 8,158,200 insured unemployed.
