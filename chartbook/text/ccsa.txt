For the week ending April 3, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,936,696 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 88,000. One year prior, during the week of April 4, 2020, there were an average of 12,445,500 insured unemployed.
