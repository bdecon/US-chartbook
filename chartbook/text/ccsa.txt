For the week ending August 15, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 13,909,872 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed; see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 272,900. One year prior, during the week of August 17, 2019, there were an average of 1,615,800 insured unemployed.
