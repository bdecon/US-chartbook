For the week ending September 12, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 12,264,351 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 176,500. One year prior, during the week of September 14, 2019, there were an average of 1,399,600 insured unemployed.
