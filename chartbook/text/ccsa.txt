For the week ending July 25, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 15,849,488 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed; see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 965,700. One year prior, during the week of July 27, 2019, there were an average of 1,665,300 insured unemployed.
