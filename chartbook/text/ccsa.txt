For the week ending May 15, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,521,314 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 150,000. One year prior, during the week of May 16, 2020, there were an average of 19,037,700 insured unemployed.
