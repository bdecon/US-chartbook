For the week ending June 6, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 18,654,001 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed, see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 26,000. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 1,522,900 insured unemployed.
