For the week ending September 18, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 2,460,965 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 49,000. One year prior, during the week of September 19, 2020, there were an average of 11,037,700 insured unemployed.
