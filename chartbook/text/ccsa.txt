For the week ending September 25, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 2,391,433 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 79,000. One year prior, during the week of September 26, 2020, there were an average of 10,156,800 insured unemployed.
