For the week ending August 29, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 13,197,059 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 54,500. One year prior, during the week of August 31, 2019, there were an average of 1,476,200 insured unemployed.
