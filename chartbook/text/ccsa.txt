For the week ending August 1, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 15,207,671 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed; see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 624,700. One year prior, during the week of August 3, 2019, there were an average of 1,679,500 insured unemployed.
