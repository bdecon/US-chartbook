For the week ending December 18, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 1,636,508 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 191,100. One year prior, during the week of December 19, 2020, there were an average of 5,225,800 insured unemployed.
