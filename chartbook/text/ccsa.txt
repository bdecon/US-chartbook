For the week ending November 27, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 1,958,827 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 397,600. One year prior, during the week of November 28, 2020, there were an average of 5,790,600 insured unemployed.
