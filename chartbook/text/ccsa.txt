For the week ending November 7, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 6,081,402 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 419,700. One year prior, during the week of November 9, 2019, there were an average of 1,460,800 insured unemployed.
