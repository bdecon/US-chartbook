For the week ending October 17, 2020, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 7,422,454 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 662,400. One year prior, during the week of October 19, 2019, there were an average of 1,419,500 insured unemployed.
