For the week ending September 11, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 2,534,759 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 156,800. One year prior, during the week of September 12, 2020, there were an average of 12,391,900 insured unemployed.
