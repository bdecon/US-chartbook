For the week ending December 25, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 1,871,110 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 232,500.
