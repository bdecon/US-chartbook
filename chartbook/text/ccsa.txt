For the week ending June 26, 2021, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reports}} 3,236,197 continued claims for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week decrease of 49,100. One year prior, during the week of June 27, 2020, there were an average of 16,611,300 insured unemployed.
