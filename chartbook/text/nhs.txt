In October 2023, seasonally-adjusted \textbf{annualized sales} of new single-family homes total 679,000 (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}), as \href{https://www.census.gov/construction/nrs/pdf/newressales.pdf}{reported} by the Census Bureau. Over the past year, new home sales increased 0.2 percent. Pre-COVID, in February 2020, the annualized rate of single family new home sales was 699,000. Since February 2020, new home sales have decreased 2.9 percent.
