In 2024 Q1, wages increased 4.4 percent, following increases of 4.2 percent in 2023 Q4 and 4.3 percent in 2023 Q3 (see\cbox{cyan!50!lime!70!white}). Growth is currently below the 2021 highs and above the pre-COVID average.

Adjusted for inflation using the PCE deflator, growth is 1.2 percent in 2024 Q1, following increases of 2.5 percent in 2023 Q4 and 1.7 percent in 2023 Q3 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}). Real wage growth is above the long-term average.
