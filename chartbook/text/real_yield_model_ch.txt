Over the three months ending November 2022, nominal two-year treasury yields increased 1.29 percentage points, real yields increased 1.13 percentage points, and inflation expectations increased 0.17 percentage point. Ten-year treasury nominal yields increased 1.11 percentage points, real yields increased 0.76 percentage point, and inflation expectations increased 0.35 percentage point.

Over the three years ending November 2022, the nominal yield on two-year treasuries increased 2.93 percentage points, the real yield increased 1.72 percentage points, and inflation expectations increased 1.22 percentage points. For ten-year treasuries, the nominal yield increased 2.20 percentage points, the real yield increased 0.76 percentage point, and expected inflation increased 0.77 percentage point.
