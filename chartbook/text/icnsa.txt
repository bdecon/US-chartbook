The Department of Labor \href{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}{report} 213,425 actual \textbf{new claims for unemployment insurance} (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending March 18, 2023, a one-week decrease of 4,700. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 218,100 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 200,500 new claims per week.
