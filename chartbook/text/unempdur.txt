Among those who are unemployed in June 2024, the average (mean) \textbf{duration of unemployment} is 20.7 weeks (see {\color{blue!60!cyan}\textbf{---}}), and the typical (median) duration of unemployment is 9.8 weeks (see {\color{green!75!blue}\textbf{---}}). Over the year prior to COVID-19, ending February 2020, the average duration of unemployment was 21.7 weeks and the typical duration was 9.2 weeks.
