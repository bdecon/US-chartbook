In the first quarter of 2020, aggregate corporate profits were \$1.87 trillion, or 11.5 percent of net national income. Of this, \$1.38 trillion, equivalent to 8.5 percent of net national product, were paid out as dividends (see\cbox{blue!70!purple}), \$297 billion were retained (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), and \$196 billion went to corporate income tax (see\cbox{red!80!orange}).
