In the second quarter of 2020, aggregate corporate profits were \$1.81 trillion, or 12.8 percent of net national income. Of this, \$1.37 trillion, equivalent to 9.7 percent of net national product, were paid out as dividends (see\cbox{blue!70!purple}), \$198 billion were retained (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), and \$239 billion went to corporate income tax (see\cbox{red!80!orange}).
