In the first quarter of 2021, aggregate corporate profits were \$2.29 trillion, or 13.5 percent of net national income. Of this, \$1.34 trillion, equivalent to 7.9 percent of net national product, were paid out as dividends (see\cbox{blue!70!purple}), \$599 billion were retained (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), and \$358 billion went to corporate income tax (see\cbox{red!80!orange}).
