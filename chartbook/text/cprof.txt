In the third quarter of 2020, aggregate corporate profits were \$2.33 trillion, or 14.9 percent of net national income. Of this, \$1.35 trillion, equivalent to 8.6 percent of net national product, were paid out as dividends (see\cbox{blue!70!purple}), \$673 billion were retained (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), and \$307 billion went to corporate income tax (see\cbox{red!80!orange}).
