In the third quarter of 2019, aggregate corporate profits were \$2.08 trillion, or 12.7 percent of net national income. Of this, \$1.34 trillion, equivalent to 8.2 percent of net national product, were paid out as dividends (see\cbox{blue!70!purple}), \$529 billion were retained (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), and \$209 billion went to corporate income tax (see\cbox{red!80!orange}).
