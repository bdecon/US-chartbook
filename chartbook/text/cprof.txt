In the third quarter of 2021, aggregate corporate profits were \$2.92 trillion, or 16.1 percent of net national income. Of this, \$1.44 trillion, equivalent to 8.0 percent of net national product, were paid out as dividends (see\cbox{blue!70!purple}), \$1,084 billion were retained (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), and \$393 billion went to corporate income tax (see\cbox{red!80!orange}).
