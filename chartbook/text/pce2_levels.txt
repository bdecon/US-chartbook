Within consumer spending on services, housing and utilities spending totals \$8,414 on an annualized and per person basis in 2021 Q3 (see {\color{green!60!blue}\textbf{---}}) and \$8,323 in 2019 Q4. Construction or improvement of housing is considered residential fixed investment, not consumer spending, but can be combined with spending to analyze patterns in shelter costs. In 2021 Q3, residential investment totals \$3,267 per person (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$2,906 in the pre-COVID data covering 2019 Q4.
