Within consumer spending on services, housing and utilities spending totals \$9,287 on an annualized and per person basis in 2022 Q4 (see {\color{green!60!blue}\textbf{---}}) and \$9,080 in 2019 Q4. Construction or improvement of housing is considered residential fixed investment, not consumer spending, but can be combined with spending to analyze patterns in shelter costs. In 2022 Q4, residential investment totals \$3,113 per person (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$3,333 in the pre-COVID data covering 2019 Q4.
