Within consumer spending on services, housing and utilities spending totals \$8,324 on an annualized and per person basis in 2021 Q2 (see {\color{green!60!blue}\textbf{---}}) and \$8,239 in 2019 Q4. Construction or improvement of housing is considered residential fixed investment, not consumer spending, but can be combined with spending to analyze patterns in shelter costs. In 2021 Q2, residential investment totals \$3,244 per person (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to \$2,806 in the pre-COVID data covering 2019 Q4.
