In June 2024, seasonally-adjusted civilian employment increased by 116,000 (see\cbox{magenta}), substantially below the 2019 average increase of 169,000 jobs per month. The US added a net total of 206,000 nonfarm payroll jobs in June 2024 (see\cbox{blue!70!white}), compared to a monthly average of 165,667 in 2019. The average of both surveys over the past three months shows an increase of 44,200 employees per month.
