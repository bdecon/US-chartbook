In December 2022, seasonally-adjusted civilian employment increased by 717,000 (see \cbox{magenta}), far above the 2019 average increase of 168,083 jobs per month. The US added a net total of 223,000 nonfarm payroll jobs in December 2022 (see \cbox{blue!70!white}), compared to a monthly average of 164,000 in 2019. The average of both surveys over the past three months shows an increase of 189,300 employees per month.
