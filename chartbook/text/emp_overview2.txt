In October 2022, seasonally-adjusted civilian employment decreased by 328,000 (see \cbox{magenta}), far below the 2019 average increase of 167,083 jobs per month. The US added a net total of 261,000 nonfarm payroll jobs in October 2022 (see \cbox{blue!70!white}), compared to a monthly average of 164,000 in 2019. The average of both surveys over the past three months shows an increase of 197,700 employees per month.
