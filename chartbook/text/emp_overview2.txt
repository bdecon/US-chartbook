In February 2023, seasonally-adjusted civilian employment increased by 177,000 (see \cbox{magenta}), in line with the 2019 average increase of 168,083 jobs per month. The US added a net total of 311,000 nonfarm payroll jobs in February 2023 (see \cbox{blue!70!white}), compared to a monthly average of 163,250 in 2019. The average of both surveys over the past three months shows an increase of 473,700 employees per month.
