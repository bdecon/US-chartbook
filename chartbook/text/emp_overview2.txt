In June 2023, seasonally-adjusted civilian employment increased by 273,000 (see \cbox{magenta}), substantially above the 2019 average increase of 168,083 jobs per month. The US added a net total of 209,000 nonfarm payroll jobs in June 2023 (see \cbox{blue!70!white}), compared to a monthly average of 163,250 in 2019. The average of both surveys over the past three months shows an increase of 139,000 employees per month.
