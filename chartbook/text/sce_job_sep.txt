In May 2024, the perceived likelihood of leaving one's job voluntarily in the next 12 months averages 19.6 percent, compared to 21.0 percent in 2019 (see {\color{violet!50!blue}\textbf{---}}). In the latest month, the perceived probability losing one's job is 12.4 percent, compared to 14.3 percent in 2019 (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}).

During the pandemic, in April 2020, job loss expectations exceeded job leaving expectations. In May 2024, job leaving expectations exceed job loss expectations by 7.2 percentage points, compared to 6.8 percentage points in 2019.
