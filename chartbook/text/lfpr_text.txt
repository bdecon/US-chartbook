As of November 2023, 62.8 percent of those aged 16 and over are part of the labor force (see {\color{green!80!blue!60!black}\textbf{---}}), following 62.7 percent in October and 62.8 percent in September. Pre-pandemic, in February 2020, the rate stood at 63.3 percent.

In November 2023, 68.4 percent of men age 16 and older are in the labor force (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to 57.5 percent of women (see {\color{red!80!black}\textbf{---}}). Since February 2020, labor force participation has decreased 0.8 percentage point among men, and decreased 0.3 percentage point among women.
