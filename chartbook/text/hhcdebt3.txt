Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY) \href{https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/hhdc/background.html}{analysis} of Equifax data shows \$17.1 trillion in total consumer debt in the second quarter of 2023, which is equivalent to 85.8 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past four years, total consumer debt has increased by \$3.20 trillion, compared to an increase of \$3.61 trillion in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of total consumer debt to disposable personal income increased 0.6 percentage point over this period.
