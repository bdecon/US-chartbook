Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY) \href{https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/hhdc/background.html}{analysis} of Equifax data shows \$16.2 trillion in total consumer debt in the second quarter of 2022, which is equivalent to 87.7 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past three years, total consumer debt has increased by \$2.29 trillion, compared to an increase of \$2.14 trillion in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of total consumer debt to disposable personal income increased 2.5 percentage points over this period.
