Federal Reserve Bank of New York analysis of Equifax data shows \$13.86 trillion in total consumer debt in 2019 Q2, which is equivalent to 84.6 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past three years, total consumer debt has increased by \$1.57 trillion compared to an increase of \$2.37 trillion in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of total consumer debt to disposable personal income has fallen by 2.7 percentage points over this period.
