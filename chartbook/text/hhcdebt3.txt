Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY) \href{https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/hhdc/background.html}{data} show \$17.3 trillion in \textbf{household debt} in the third quarter of 2023, which translates to 85.1 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past four years, household debt increased by \$3.34 trillion, compared to a \$4.10 trillion increase in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of debt to income decreased 0.9 percentage point over this period.
