Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY) \href{https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/hhdc/background.html}{data} show \$17.1 trillion in household debt in the second quarter of 2023, which translates to 84.6 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past four years, household debt increased by \$3.20 trillion, compared to a \$4.06 trillion increase in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of debt to income dropped by 1.7 percentage points over this period.
