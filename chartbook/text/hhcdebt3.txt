Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY) \href{https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/hhdc/background.html}{data} show \$17.7 trillion in \textbf{household debt} in the first quarter of 2024, which translates to 85.5 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past four years, household debt increased by \$3.39 trillion, compared to a \$2.81 trillion increase in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of debt to income dropped by 1.1 percentage points over this period.
