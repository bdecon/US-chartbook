Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY) \href{https://www.newyorkfed.org/microeconomics/hhdc/background.html}{analysis} of Equifax data shows \$14.3 trillion in total consumer debt in 2020 Q2, which is equivalent to 78.2 percent of disposable personal income. Over the past three years, total consumer debt has increased by \$1.43 trillion compared to an increase of \$3.41 trillion in disposable personal income. As a result, the ratio of total consumer debt to disposable personal income has fallen by 8.4 percentage points over this period.
