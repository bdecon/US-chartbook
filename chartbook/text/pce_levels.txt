Total consumer spending is \$18.7 trillion in 2023 Q3, compared to a price-adjusted \$18.5 trillion in 2023 Q2 and \$17.0 trillion in 2019 Q4. On a per person basis, consumer spending is \$55,772 in 2023 Q3, of which \$18,566 are spent on goods (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}) and \$37,206 on services (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). In the fourth quarter of 2019, before the pandemic, consumer spending on goods was \$15,910 per person, and spending on services was \$35,527 per person, after adjusting for inflation.
