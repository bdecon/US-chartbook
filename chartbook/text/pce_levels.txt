On a per person basis, consumer spending is \$43,965 in 2020 Q4, of which \$14,692 are spent on goods (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}) and \$29,274 on services (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). In the fourth quarter of 2019, before the pandemic, consumer spending on goods was \$13,805 per person, and spending on services was \$31,579 per person, after adjusting for inflation.
