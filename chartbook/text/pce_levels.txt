Total consumer spending is \$15.7 trillion in 2021 Q2, compared to a price-adjusted \$15.2 trillion in 2021 Q1 and \$15.2 trillion in 2019 Q4. On a per person basis, consumer spending is \$47,319 in 2021 Q2, of which \$16,642 are spent on goods (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}) and \$30,677 on services (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). In the fourth quarter of 2019, before the pandemic, consumer spending on goods was \$14,239 per person, and spending on services was \$31,930 per person, after adjusting for inflation.
