Total consumer spending is \$14.4 trillion in 2020 Q3, compared to a price-adjusted \$13.2 trillion in 2020 Q2 and \$14.9 trillion in 2019 Q4. On a per person basis, consumer spending is \$43,572 in 2020 Q3, of which \$14,695 are spent on goods (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}) and \$28,877 on services (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). In the fourth quarter of 2019, before the pandemic, consumer spending on goods was \$13,815 per person, and spending on services was \$31,393 per person, after adjusting for inflation.
