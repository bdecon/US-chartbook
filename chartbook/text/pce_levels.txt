Total consumer spending is \$18.3 trillion in 2023 Q2, compared to a price-adjusted \$18.2 trillion in 2023 Q1 and \$16.8 trillion in 2019 Q4. On a per person basis, consumer spending is \$54,628 in 2023 Q2, of which \$18,191 are spent on goods (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}) and \$36,437 on services (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). In the fourth quarter of 2019, before the pandemic, consumer spending on goods was \$15,684 per person, and spending on services was \$34,976 per person, after adjusting for inflation.
