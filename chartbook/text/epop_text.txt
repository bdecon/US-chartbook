In October 2021, 78.3 percent of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 78.0 percent in September 2021. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 2.3 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 3.0 percentage points (equivalent to 3.7 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
