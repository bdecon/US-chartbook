In May 2021, 77.1 percent of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 76.9 percent in April 2021. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 5.8 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 4.2 percentage points (equivalent to 5.3 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
