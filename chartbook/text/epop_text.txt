In November 2019, 80.3\% of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 80.3 in October 2019. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 0.6 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 1.0 percentage points (equivalent to 1.2 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
