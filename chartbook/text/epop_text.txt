In December 2022, 80.1 percent of 25 to 54 years olds were employed, compared to 79.7 percent in November 2022. Over the past year, the age 25 to 54 employment rate increased one percentage point. The December 2022 rate was 1.3 percentage points (equivalent to 1.7 million workers) below the average rate of 81.4 during the tight labor market of 1999--2000.
