In September 2021, 78.0 percent of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 78.0 percent in August 2021. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 2.9 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 3.3 percentage points (equivalent to 4.1 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
