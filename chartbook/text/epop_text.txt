In December 2021, 79.0 percent of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 78.8 percent in November 2021. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 2.8 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 2.3 percentage points (equivalent to 2.9 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
