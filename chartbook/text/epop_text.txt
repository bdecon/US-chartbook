In May 2024, 80.8 percent of 25 to 54 years olds were employed (see {\color{blue!90!cyan}\textbf{---}}), the same level as April 2024. Over the past year, the age 25 to 54 employment rate was virtually unchanged. The May 2024 rate is 0.6 percentage point (equivalent to 820,000 workers) below the average rate of 81.4 during the tight labor market of 1999--2000.
