In January 2023, 80.2 percent of 25 to 54 years olds were employed, compared to 80.1 percent in December 2022. Over the past year, the age 25 to 54 employment rate increased one percentage point. The January 2023 rate is 1.2 percentage points (equivalent to 1.6 million workers) below the average rate of 81.4 during the tight labor market of 1999--2000.
