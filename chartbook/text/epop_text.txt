In April 2020, 69.7 percent of 25-54 years olds were employed, the lowest level in the data April 2020. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has fallen by 10.0 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 11.6 percentage points (equivalent to 14.6 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
