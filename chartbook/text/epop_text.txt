In July 2021, 77.8 percent of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 77.2 percent in June 2021. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 3.9 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 3.5 percentage points (equivalent to 4.4 million workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
