In February 2020, 80.5\% of 25-54 years olds were employed, compared to 80.6 in January 2020. Over the past year, the age 25-54 employment rate has increased by 0.6 percentage points. The current age 25-54 employment rate is 0.8 percentage points (equivalent to 970.0 thousand workers) below the average during 1998--99, a period with a particularly tight labor market.
