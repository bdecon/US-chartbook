In November 2023, 80.7 percent of 25 to 54 years olds were employed (see {\color{blue!90!cyan}\textbf{---}}), the highest level since September 2023. Over the past year, the age 25 to 54 employment rate increased one percentage point. The November 2023 rate is 0.7 percentage point (equivalent to 950,000 workers) below the average rate of 81.4 during the tight labor market of 1999--2000.
