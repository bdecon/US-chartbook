As of 2021 Q4, the Federal Reserve \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/z1/20191212/html/b101.htm}{report} \textbf{owners' equity} is 69.2 percent of residential real estate (see {\color{blue!80!violet}\textbf{---}}). Over the past three years, the owners' equity share increased by a total of five percentage points. Over the past year, the share increased by a total of 2.3 percentage points. The current share is substantially above the 1989 average of 67.9 percent.
