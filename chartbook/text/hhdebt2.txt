The ratio of household and nonprofit debt to disposable personal income has fallen to 99.9 percent in 2023 Q2 from the housing bubble peak of 137.2 percent in 2007. Over the past four years, household and nonprofit debt has increased 24.2 percent while disposable personal income increased 25.3 percent. As a result, the debt-to-income ratio has fallen by 1.1 percentage points.
