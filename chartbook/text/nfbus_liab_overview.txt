Corporate liabilities total \$27.3 trillion in the third quarter of 2023, equivalent to 98.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{red!60!orange}\textbf{---}}). Of this, corporate debt is equivalent to 49.6 percent of GDP (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, corporate liabilities are 109.7 percent of GDP and corporate debt is 49.8 percent.

Noncorporate business sector liabilities are equivalent to 41.0 percent of GDP in 2023 Q2 and 39.5 percent in 2019 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}). Noncorporate business debt is 27.0 percent of GDP in the latest data and 27.6 percent in 2019 (see {\color{blue!70!black!60!white}\textbf{---}}).
