Corporate liabilities total \$29.1 trillion in the fourth quarter of 2023, equivalent to 104.2 percent of GDP (see {\color{red!60!orange}\textbf{---}}). Of this, corporate debt is equivalent to 48.6 percent of GDP (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, corporate liabilities are 109.7 percent of GDP and corporate debt is 49.8 percent.

Noncorporate business sector liabilities are equivalent to 40.6 percent of GDP in 2023 Q2 and 39.5 percent in 2019 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}). Noncorporate business debt is 26.8 percent of GDP in the latest data and 27.6 percent in 2019 (see {\color{blue!70!black!60!white}\textbf{---}}).
