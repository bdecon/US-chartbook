Government consumption expeditures and gross investment, which provide services and infrastructure, subtracted 0.27 percentage point from real GDP growth in 2021 Q2, substantially below the average contribution of 0.05 percentage point over the past year, and far below the average of 0.24 percentage point since 1989. In 2021 Q2, federal defense spending and investment (see\cbox{blue!60!black}) subtracted 0.03 percentage point, nondefense federal government spending and investment (see\cbox{green!85!black}) subtracted 0.33 percentage point, and state and local government (see\cbox{purple!70!magenta}) contributed 0.09 percentage point.
