Government gross investment, less depreciation, is the government's net investment in the economy. Government investment includes infrastructure, buildings, equipment, intellectual property, and other capital goods. In the latest data, covering 2020 Q4, government net investment is \$191.2 billion. Government net investment is equivalent to 0.9 percent of GDP in 2020 Q4 (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}), compared to 0.8 percent in 2019 Q4, and 0.7 percent in 2018 Q4.
