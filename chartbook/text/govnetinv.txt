Government gross investment, less depreciation, is the government's net investment in the tangible assets that make the economy more productive. Government investment includes infrastructure, buildings, equipment, intellectual property, and other capital goods. In the latest data, covering 2024 Q1, government net investment is \$277.8 billion. Government net investment represents 0.98 percent of GDP in 2024 Q1 (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}), compared to 0.70 percent in 2023 Q1, and 0.60 percent in 2022 Q1.
