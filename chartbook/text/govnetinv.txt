Government gross investment, less depreciation, is the government's net investment in the tangible assets that make the economy more productive. Government investment includes infrastructure, buildings, equipment, intellectual property, and other capital goods. In the latest data, covering 2023 Q3, government net investment is \$235.0 billion. Government net investment represents 0.85 percent of GDP in 2023 Q3 (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}), compared to 0.58 percent in 2022 Q3, and 0.67 percent in 2021 Q3.
