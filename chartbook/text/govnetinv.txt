Government gross investment, less depreciation, is the government's net investment in the economy. Government investment includes infrastructure, buildings, equipment, intellectual property, and other capital goods. In the latest data, covering 2021 Q3, government net investment is \$156.7 billion. Government net investment is equivalent to 0.7 percent of GDP in 2021 Q3 (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}), compared to 0.8 percent in 2020 Q3, and 0.7 percent in 2019 Q3.
