Government gross investment, less depreciation, is the government's net investment in the tangible assets that make the economy more productive. Government investment includes infrastructure, buildings, equipment, intellectual property, and other capital goods. In the latest data, covering 2024 Q2, government net investment is \$297.9 billion. Government net investment represents 1.04 percent of GDP in 2024 Q2 (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}), compared to 0.79 percent in 2023 Q2, and 0.55 percent in 2022 Q2.
