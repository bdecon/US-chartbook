Public administration has the highest union membership rate, at 29.2 percent as of November 2021, followed by education and health with 16.0 percent, and construction and mining with 13.6 percent. The manufacturing industry experienced the largest overall percentage point decrease in union membership rates over the past 30 years, and is currently 16.7 percentage points below its February 1989 rate of 23.5 percent. The lowest union membership rate is in leisure and hospitality (2.7 percent). The union membership rate of the industry was 18.0 percent at its 30-year peak in January 1989.
