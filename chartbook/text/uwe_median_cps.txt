In December 2023, the median full-time worker receives \$1,154 per week, following \$1,115 per week in November and \$1,137 per week in October. The average over these three months is \$1,135 per week, a 5.6 percent increase over the same three months, one year prior (see {\color{violet!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

Median usual weekly earnings increased 8.7 percent over the year ending December 2023 (see\cbox{violet!80!blue}), following increases of 3.3 percent in November and 5.1 percent in October.
