In April 2024, the median full-time worker receives \$1,144 per week, following \$1,150 per week in March and \$1,128 per week in February. The average over these three months is \$1,141 per week, a 2.9 percent increase over the same three months, one year prior (see {\color{violet!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

Median usual weekly earnings increased 3.6 percent over the year ending April 2024 (see\cbox{violet!80!blue}), following increases of 4.8 percent in March and 0.4 percent in February.
