In October 2023, the median full-time worker receives \$1,137 per week, following \$1,149 per week in September and \$1,083 per week in August. The average over these three months is \$1,123 per week, a 3.8 percent increase over the same three months, one year prior (see {\color{violet!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

Median usual weekly earnings increased 5.1 percent over the year ending October 2023 (see\cbox{violet!80!blue}), following increases of 4.5 percent in September and 1.8 percent in August.
