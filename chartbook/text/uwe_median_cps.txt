In October 2023, the median full-time worker receives \$1,137 per week, following \$1,149 per week in September 2023 and \$1,083 per week in August 2023. The average over these three months is \$1,123 per week, a 3.8 percent increase over the same three months, one year prior (see {\color{violet!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

Median usual weekly earnings increased 5.1 percent over the year ending October 2023 (see\cbox{violet!80!blue}), following a 4.5 percent increase in September, and a 1.8 percent increase in August.
