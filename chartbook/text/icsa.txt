The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 3,489,261 actual new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending April 25, 2020, a one-week decrease of 792,400. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 4,736,800 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 202,200 new claims per week.
