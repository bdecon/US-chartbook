The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 1,603,000 actual new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending May 30, 2020, a one-week decrease of 314,600. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 2,014,700 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 192,000 new claims per week.
