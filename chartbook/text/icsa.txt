The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 344,653 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending July 24, 2021, a one-week decrease of 66,600. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 382,500 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 1,369,800 new claims per week.
