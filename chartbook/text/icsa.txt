The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 575,350 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending April 24, 2021, a one-week decrease of 9,500. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 630,400 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 4,687,200 new claims per week.
