The Department of Labor \href{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}{report} 214,873 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending February 19, 2022, a one-week decrease of 24,800. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 235,700 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 817,300 new claims per week.
