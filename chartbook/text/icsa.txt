The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 383,166 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending July 10, 2021, virtually unchanged from the previous week. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 381,700 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 1,444,400 new claims per week.
