The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 1,914,958 actual new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending May 23, 2020, a one-week decrease of 266,700. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 2,327,200 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 195,600 new claims per week.
