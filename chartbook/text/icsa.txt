The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 308,574 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending August 14, 2021, a one-week decrease of 14,500. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 325,400 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 974,200 new claims per week.
