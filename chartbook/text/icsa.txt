The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 2,849,090 actual new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending May 2, 2020, a one-week decrease of 646,600. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 3,897,900 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 204,200 new claims per week.
