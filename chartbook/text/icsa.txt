The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 833,352 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI; see {\color{cyan!85!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending August 29, 2020, a one-week increase of 7,600. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 846,900 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 178,700 new claims per week.
