The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 1,205,871 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI; see {\color{cyan!85!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending July 25, 2020, a one-week decrease of 171,100. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 1,372,700 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 212,700 new claims per week.
