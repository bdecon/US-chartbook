The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 2,174,329 actual new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending May 16, 2020, a one-week decrease of 182,300. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 2,725,500 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 197,200 new claims per week.
