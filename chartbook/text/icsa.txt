The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 748,078 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending February 27, 2021, a one-week increase of 31,500. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 791,900 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 211,300 new claims per week.
