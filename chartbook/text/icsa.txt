The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 738,166 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending October 31, 2020, virtually unchanged from the previous week.
