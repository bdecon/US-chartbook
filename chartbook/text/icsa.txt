The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 256,146 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending December 25, 2021, a one-week increase of 1,100. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 265,200 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 888,400 new claims per week.
