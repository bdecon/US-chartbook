The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 2,898,450 new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending March 21, 2020, a one-week increase of 2,647,000. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 891,700 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 203,500 new claims per week.
