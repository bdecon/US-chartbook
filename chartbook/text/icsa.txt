The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 315,469 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending January 1, 2022, a one-week increase of 57,600. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 273,500 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 876,400 new claims per week.
