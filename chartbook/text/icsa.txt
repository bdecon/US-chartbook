The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 5,823,917 actual new claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending March 28, 2020, a one-week increase of 2,903,800. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 2,299,000 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 194,400 new claims per week.
