The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 922,072 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending January 2, 2021, a one-week increase of 77,400. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 895,400 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 301,400 new claims per week.
