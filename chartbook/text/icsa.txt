The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{reported}} 3,283,000 initial claims for unemployment insurance during the week ending March 21, 2020, a one-week increase of 3,001,000. Over the past three months, initial claims averaged 473,667 per week. During the same three month period three years ago, initial claims averaged 245,417 per week.
