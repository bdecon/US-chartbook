The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 277,632 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending October 9, 2021, a one-week increase of 16,000. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 286,600 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 773,700 new claims per week.
