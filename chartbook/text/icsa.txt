The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 419,446 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending January 8, 2022, a one-week increase of 103,700. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 312,000 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 914,900 new claims per week.
