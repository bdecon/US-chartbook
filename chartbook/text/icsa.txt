The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 816,247 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending January 30, 2021, a one-week decrease of 23,500.
