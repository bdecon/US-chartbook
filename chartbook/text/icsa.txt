The Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 746,496 actual new claims for unemployment insurance (UI) under state programs (see {\color{cyan!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) during the week ending March 13, 2021, a one-week increase of 24,300. Over the past four weeks, new claims have averaged 735,500 per week. During the same four-week period last year, there were an average of 217,000 new claims per week.
