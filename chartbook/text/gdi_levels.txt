The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/gdp/gross-domestic-product}{report} seasonally-adjusted and annualized \href{https://www.bea.gov/help/glossary/gross-domestic-income-gdi}{Gross Domestic Income} (GDI) of \$22,489 billion in 2021 Q1, compared to an inflation-adjusted equivalent of \$22,228 billion in 2019 Q4. Real GDI per capita was \$67,940 in 2021 Q1 and \$67,525 in 2019 Q4.
