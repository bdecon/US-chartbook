The Bureau of Economic Analysis \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/gdp/gross-domestic-product}{report} seasonally-adjusted and annualized \href{https://www.bea.gov/help/glossary/gross-domestic-income-gdi}{Gross Domestic Income} (GDI) of \$19,486 billion in 2020 Q2, compared to an inflation-adjusted equivalent of \$21,684 billion in 2019 Q4. Real GDI per capita was \$59,068 in 2020 Q2 and \$65,870 in 2019 Q4.
