In the the first quarter of 2023, gross private business investment totals \$3,474 billion on a seasonally-adjusted annualized basis, equivalent to 13.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{blue!60!violet}\textbf{---}}). Private business investment in fixed assets totals \$3,534 billion, or 13.3 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!80!white}\textbf{---}}). Private business depreciation totals \$2,936 billion in the quarter, or 11.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}). As a result, net investment is \$539 billion, or 2.0 percent of GDP (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

 In 2019 Q4, prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, private business gross investment was \$2,880 billion. Since 2019 Q4, annualized gross investment increased 20.7 percent. Net investment was \$596 billion in 2019 Q4, and decreased 9.7 percent from 2019 Q4 to 2023 Q1, as gross investment recovered from its pandemic lows.
