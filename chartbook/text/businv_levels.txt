In the the third quarter of 2021, gross private business investment totals \$2,978 billion on a seasonally-adjusted annualized basis, equivalent to 12.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{blue!60!violet}\textbf{---}}). Private business investment in fixed assets totals \$3,073 billion, or 13.3 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!80!white}\textbf{---}}). Private business depreciation totals \$2,524 billion in the quarter, or 10.9 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}). As a result, net investment is \$453 billion, or 2.0 percent of GDP (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

 In 2019 Q4, prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, private business gross investment was \$2,896 billion. Since 2019 Q4, annualized gross investment increased 2.8 percent. Net investment was \$614 billion in 2019 Q4, and decreased 26.2 percent from 2019 Q4 to 2021 Q3, as gross investment recovered from its pandemic lows.
