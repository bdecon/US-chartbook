In the the third quarter of 2020, gross private business investment totals \$2,708 billion on a seasonally-adjusted annualized basis, equivalent to 12.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{blue!60!violet}\textbf{---}}). Private business investment in fixed assets totals \$2,773 billion, or 13.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!80!white}\textbf{---}}). Private business depreciation totals \$2,326 billion in the quarter, or 11.0 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}). As a result, net investment is \$382 billion, or 1.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). 

 In 2019 Q4, prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, private business gross investment was \$2,839 billion. Since 2019 Q4, annualized gross investment decreased 4.6 percent. Net investment was \$569 billion in 2019 Q4, and decreased 32.8 percent from 2019 Q4 to 2020 Q3, as gross investment fell while deprecation was relatively constant.
