In the second quarter of 2024, gross private business investment totals \$3,877 billion on a seasonally-adjusted annualized basis, which is 13.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{blue!60!violet}\textbf{---}}). Private business investment in fixed assets totals \$3,905 billion, or 13.6 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!80!white}\textbf{---}}). Private business depreciation totals \$3,100 billion in the quarter, or 10.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}). As a result, net fixed investment is \$805 billion, or 2.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}).

 In 2019 Q4, prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, private business gross investment was \$2,935 billion. Since 2019 Q4, gross investment increased at an annual rate of 6.4 percent. Net fixed investment was \$711 billion in 2019 Q4, and increased at an annual rate of 2.8 percent from 2019 Q4 to 2024 Q2, as growth of depreciation costs outpaced the increase in gross investment.
