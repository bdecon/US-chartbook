In the third quarter of 2023, gross private business investment totals \$3,732 billion on a seasonally-adjusted annualized basis, which is 13.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{blue!60!violet}\textbf{---}}). Private business investment in fixed assets totals \$3,729 billion, or 13.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!80!white}\textbf{---}}). Private business depreciation totals \$2,983 billion in the quarter, or 10.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}). As a result, net fixed investment is \$746 billion, or 2.7 percent of GDP (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}).

 In 2019 Q4, prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, private business gross investment was \$2,935 billion. Since 2019 Q4, gross investment increased at an annual rate of 6.6 percent. Net fixed investment was \$711 billion in 2019 Q4, and increased at an annual rate of 1.3 percent from 2019 Q4 to 2023 Q3, as growth of depreciation costs outpaced the increase in gross investment.
