The trimmed-mean PCE price index increased 3.5 percent over the year ending January 2022 (see {\color{violet!60!magenta}\textbf{---}}). By excluding top and bottom categories, the trimmed-mean rate was 2.5 percentage points below the all-items PCE rate. In December 2021, the trimmed-mean rate was 3.1 percent, 2.7 percentage points below the all-items rate. From 2017--2019, the average trimmed-mean rate was 1.9 percent, 0.1 percentage point above the all-items rate.
