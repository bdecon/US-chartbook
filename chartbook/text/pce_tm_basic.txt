The trimmed-mean PCE price index increased 3.9 percent over the year ending August 2023 (see {\color{violet!60!magenta}\textbf{---}}). By excluding top and bottom categories, the trimmed-mean rate was 0.4 percentage point above the all-items PCE rate. In July 2023, the \textbf{trimmed-mean inflation rate} was 4.2 percent, 0.8 percentage point above the all-items rate. From 2017--2019, the average trimmed-mean rate was 1.9 percent, 0.1 percentage point above the all-items rate.
