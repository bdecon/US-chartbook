The trimmed-mean PCE price index increased 2.9 percent over the year ending April 2024 (see {\color{violet!60!magenta}\textbf{---}}). By excluding top and bottom categories, the trimmed-mean rate was 0.3 percentage point above the all-items PCE rate. In March 2024, the \textbf{trimmed-mean inflation rate} was 3.0 percent, 0.3 percentage point above the all-items rate. From 2017--2019, the average trimmed-mean rate was 1.9 percent, 0.1 percentage point above the all-items rate.
