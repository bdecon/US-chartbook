In 2023 Q3, gross fixed investment was \$5.8 trillion, depreciation was \$4.6 trillion, and net fixed investment was \$1.2 trillion, equivalent to 4.3 percent of GDP (see {\color{purple}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, net fixed investment was 5.2 percent of GDP.

The financial accounts also tabulate net spending on consumer durable goods, such as autos, furniture, and appliances. Net spending on consumer durables was \$368 billion in 2023 Q3, or 1.3 percent of GDP (see {\color{blue!80!green!95!white}\textbf{---}}). Net consumer durable goods spending was 1.1 percent of GDP in 2019.
