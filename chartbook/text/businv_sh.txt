Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Investment in structures was \$562 billion in 2020 Q3, equivalent to 2.7 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment was \$1,206 billion or 5.7 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment was \$1,005 billion or 4.7 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
