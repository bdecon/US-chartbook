Business gross investment in fixed assets is grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Annualized investment in structures is \$833 billion in 2023 Q2, equivalent to 3.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment is \$1,390 billion or 5.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment is \$1,486 billion or 5.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
