Business fixed investment encompasses structures, equipment, and intellectual property, such as software and R\&D. Annualized investment in structures is \$876 billion in 2024 Q2, representing 3.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment is \$1,448 billion or 5.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment is \$1,580 billion, which is 5.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
