Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Investment in structures was \$552 billion in 2021 Q1, equivalent to 2.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment was \$1,315 billion or 6.0 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment was \$1,094 billion or 5.0 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
