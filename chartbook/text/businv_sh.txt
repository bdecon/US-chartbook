Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Investment in structures was \$723 billion in 2023 Q1, equivalent to 2.7 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment was \$1,359 billion or 5.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment was \$1,450 billion or 5.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
