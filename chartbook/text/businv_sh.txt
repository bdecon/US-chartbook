Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Investment in structures was \$631 billion in 2022 Q2, equivalent to 2.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment was \$1,300 billion or 5.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment was \$1,361 billion or 5.4 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
