Business fixed investment encompasses structures, equipment, and intellectual property, such as software and R\&D. Annualized investment in structures is \$842 billion in 2023 Q3, representing three percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment is \$1,386 billion or five percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment is \$1,502 billion, which is 5.4 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
