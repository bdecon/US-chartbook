Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Investment in structures was \$557 billion in 2020 Q3, equivalent to 2.6 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment was \$1,202 billion or 5.7 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment was \$1,028 billion or 4.9 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
