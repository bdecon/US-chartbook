Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Annualized investment in structures is \$733 billion in 2023 Q1, equivalent to 2.8 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment is \$1,353 billion or 5.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment is \$1,447 billion or 5.5 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
