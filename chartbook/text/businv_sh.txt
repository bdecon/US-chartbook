Business investments in fixed assets are grouped into three categories: structures, equipment, and intellectual property (for example software and R\&D). Investment in structures was \$692 billion in 2022 Q4, equivalent to 2.6 percent of GDP (see {\color{yellow!50!orange}\textbf{---}}). Equipment investment was \$1,360 billion or 5.2 percent of GDP (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and intellectual property investment was \$1,415 billion or 5.4 percent of GDP (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}).
