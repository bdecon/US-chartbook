In 2024 Q1, nonfarm business labor productivity increased at an annual rate of 0.3 percent (see\cbox{teal}), as the result of an increase of 1.3 percent in real output and an increase of one percent in hours worked. In the prior quarter, 2023 Q4, labor productivity increased at an annual rate of 3.5 percent, as real output increased 3.8 percent and hours of work increased 0.2 percent. Productivity has increased at an annual rate of 1.8 percent over the past five years, slightly below the 1989-onward rate of 1.9 percent.
