In 2020 Q1, federal government expenditures total \$4.9 trillion, equivalent to 22.7 percent of GDP, and receipts total \$3.8 trillion, or 17.4 percent of GDP. The federal deficit was therefore \$1.1 trillion or 5.3 percent of GDP. Over the past three years, the ratio of expenditures to GDP increased by a total of 0.94 percentage point, and the ratio of receipts to GDP has decreased by a total of 0.89 percentage point, causing the deficit to widen by 1.8 percent of GDP.
