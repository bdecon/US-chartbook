Nonstore sales were 1.7 percent of after-tax income in January 1992 and 6.9 percent in June 2023, a shift that is equivalent to \$1,049 billion per year. Since 1992, sales as a share of after-tax income has decreased in food and beverage stores (-2.9 percentage points), clothing and accessories stores (-0.9 percentage points), and general merchandise stores (-0.7 percentage points).
