Nonstore sales were 1.7 percent of after-tax income in January 1992 and 7.0 percent in August 2022, a shift that is equivalent to \$988 billion per year. Since 1992, sales as a share of after-tax income has decreased in food and beverage stores (-2.7 percentage points), clothing and accessories stores (-0.8 percentage points), and general merchandise stores (-0.6 percentage points).
