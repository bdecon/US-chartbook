On July 22, 2024, the \href{https://www.eia.gov/dnav/pet/hist/RWTCD.htm}{spot price} for a barrel of west Texas intermediate (WTI) \textbf{crude oil} is \$81.25 (see {\color{red!80!purple}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, this measure of oil prices increased 6.8 percent. Over the past four years, the price increased 99.6 percent. The WTI price is currently \$53 below its peak monthly average price of \$134 per barrel in June 2008.
