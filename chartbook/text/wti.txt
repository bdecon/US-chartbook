As of April 1, 2020, a barrel of west Texas intermediate (WTI) \textbf{crude oil} sells for \$20.28. Over the past year, this measure of oil prices has decreased by 68.2 percent. Over the past three years, the price decreased by 60.3 percent. Currently, the WTI price is \$113.60 per barrel below its June 2008 average.
