On October 3, 2023, the \href{https://www.eia.gov/dnav/pet/hist/RCLC1D.htm}{futures price} for a barrel of west Texas intermediate (WTI) \textbf{crude oil} is \$89.23 (see {\color{red!80!purple}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, this measure of oil prices increased 2.5 percent. Over the past four years, the price increased 65.2 percent. The WTI price is currently \$45 below its peak monthly average price of \$134 per barrel in June 2008.
