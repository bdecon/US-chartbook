On November 22, 2022, the \href{https://www.eia.gov/dnav/pet/hist/RCLC1D.htm}{futures price} for a barrel of west Texas intermediate (WTI) \textbf{crude oil} is \$80.95 (see {\color{red!80!purple}\textbf{---}}).
