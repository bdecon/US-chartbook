As of March 30, 2020, a barrel of west Texas intermediate (WTI) \textbf{crude oil} sells for \$14.1. Over the past year, this measure of oil prices has decreased by 75.8 percent. Over the past three years, the price decreased by 71.4 percent. Currently, the WTI price is \$119.78 per barrel below its June 2008 average.
