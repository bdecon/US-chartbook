In the second quarter of 2023, private fixed investment, which does not include inventory investment, totals \$4.6 trillion, equivalent to 17.2 percent of GDP. Non-residential (business) fixed investment totals \$3.6 trillion, or 13.4 percent of GDP, while residential fixed investment totals \$1.0 trillion (3.8 percent of GDP).

During the quarter, private fixed investment contributed 1.07 percentage points to real GDP growth. Non-residential fixed investment contributed 0.80 percentage point, while residential fixed investment subtracted 0.14 percentage point. Private inventories increased by \$7.9 billion in Q2.
