In the first quarter of 2023, private fixed investment, which does not include inventory investment, totals \$4.6 trillion, equivalent to 17.2 percent of GDP. Non-residential (business) fixed investment totals \$3.5 trillion, or 13.3 percent of GDP, while residential fixed investment totals \$1.0 trillion (3.9 percent of GDP).

During the quarter, private fixed investment subtracted 0.07 percentage point from real GDP growth. Non-residential fixed investment contributed 0.10 percentage point, while residential fixed investment subtracted 0.17 percentage point. Prinvate inventories increased by \$6.8 billion in Q1.
