In the week of May 25, 2020, the M2 measure of money averaged \$18.0 trillion, equivalent to 83.5 percent of GDP. Institution money market accounts, which are not included in M2, can be combined with M2 to create a slightly-broader-than-M2 measure of the money stock. These funds averaged \$3.3 trillion in the same week, equivalent to 15.2 percent of GDP.
