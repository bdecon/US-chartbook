In the week of August 17, 2020, the M2 measure of money averaged \$18.4 trillion, equivalent to 94.5 percent of GDP. Institution money market accounts, which are not included in M2, can be combined with M2 to create a slightly-broader-than-M2 measure of the money stock. These funds averaged \$3.1 trillion in the same week, equivalent to 15.8 percent of GDP.
