In the week of November 25, 2019, the M2 measure of money averaged \$15.3 trillion, equivalent to 71.0 percent of GDP. Institution money market accounts, which are not included in M2, can be combined with M2 to create a slightly-broader-than-M2 measure of the money stock. These funds averaged \$2.2 trillion in the same week, equivalent to 10.4 percent of GDP.
