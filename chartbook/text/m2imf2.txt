In the week of January 11, 2021, the M2 measure of money averaged \$19.6 trillion, equivalent to 92.4 percent of GDP. Institution money market accounts, which are not included in M2, can be combined with M2 to create a slightly-broader-than-M2 measure of the money stock. These funds averaged \$2.8 trillion in the same week, equivalent to 13.4 percent of GDP.
