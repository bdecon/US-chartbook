In the week of June 29, 2020, the M2 measure of money averaged \$18.4 trillion, equivalent to 85.3 percent of GDP. Institution money market accounts, which are not included in M2, can be combined with M2 to create a slightly-broader-than-M2 measure of the money stock. These funds averaged \$3.2 trillion in the same week, equivalent to 14.8 percent of GDP.
