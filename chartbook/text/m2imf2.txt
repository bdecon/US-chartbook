In the week of April 13, 2020, the M2 measure of money averaged \$17.1 trillion, equivalent to 78.6 percent of GDP. Institution money market accounts, which are not included in M2, can be combined with M2 to create a slightly-broader-than-M2 measure of the money stock. These funds averaged \$3.0 trillion in the same week, equivalent to 13.8 percent of GDP.
