In May 2020, there were 21.2 million government jobs, equivalent to 8.1 percent of the age 16+ population (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}). The previous year, in May 2019, there were 22.6 million government jobs, equivalent to 8.7 percent of the age 16 or older population. Since February 2020, the US has lost 1.6 million total government jobs.
