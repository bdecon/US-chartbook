The Bank for International Settlements (BIS) \href{https://www.bis.org/statistics/eer.htm}{calculates} \textbf{real effective exchange rates} for many countries, on a monthly basis. As of November 2022, the US dollar real effective exchange rate has increased 30.9 percent since 2010. In 2019, the index average was 117.1. Over the past three months, the index average value was 132.4.
