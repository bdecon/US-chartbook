The Bank for International Settlements (BIS) \href{https://www.bis.org/statistics/eer.htm}{calculates} \textbf{real effective exchange rates} for many countries, on a monthly basis. As of October 2023, the US dollar real effective exchange rate has increased 10.8 percent since 2010. In 2019, the index average was 98.6. Over the past three months, the index average value was 109.2.
