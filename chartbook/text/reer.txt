The Bank for International Settlements (BIS) \href{https://www.bis.org/statistics/eer.htm}{calculates} \textbf{real effective exchange rates} for many countries, on a monthly basis. As of June 2024, the US dollar real effective exchange rate has increased 11.0 percent since 2020. In 2019, the index average was 98.6. Over the past three months, the index average value was 110.1.
