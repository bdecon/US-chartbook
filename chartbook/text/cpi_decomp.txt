As of February 2021, core goods contributed 0.29 percentage point to the overall non-seasonally-adjusted CPI inflation rate of 2.01 percent, while core services excluding shelter contributed 0.30 percentage point. Shelter contributed 0.62 percentage point, and food and energy contributed 0.81 percentage point. One year prior, in February 2020, the corresponding CPI inflation rate was 2.73 percent; core goods did not contribute, core services excluding shelter contributed 0.86 percentage point, shelter contributed 1.35 percentage points, and food and energy contributed 0.53 percentage point.
