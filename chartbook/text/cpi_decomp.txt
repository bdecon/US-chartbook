In September 2022, core goods contributed 1.2 percentage points to the one-year non-seasonally-adjusted CPI inflation rate of 8.2 percent (see\cbox{blue!85!black}), while core services excluding shelter contributed 1.7 percentage points (see\cbox{green!60!black}).  One year prior, in September 2021, the corresponding CPI inflation rate was 5.4 percent; core goods contributed 1.3 percentage points, core services excluding shelter contributed 0.7 percentage point, shelter contributed 1.1 percentage points, and food and energy added 2.4 percentage points.
