As of March 2021, core goods contributed 0.36 percentage point to the overall non-seasonally-adjusted CPI inflation rate of 3.13 percent, while core services excluding shelter contributed 0.47 percentage point. Shelter contributed 0.72 percentage point, and food and energy contributed 1.69 percentage points. One year prior, in March 2020, the corresponding CPI inflation rate was 1.81 percent; core goods subtracted 0.04 percentage point, core services excluding shelter contributed 0.77 percentage point, shelter contributed 1.23 percentage points, and food and energy subtracted 0.20 percentage point.
