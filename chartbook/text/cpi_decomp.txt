As of April 2021, core goods contributed 0.94 percentage point to the overall non-seasonally-adjusted CPI inflation rate of 4.94 percent, while core services excluding shelter contributed 0.91 percentage point. One year prior, in April 2020, the corresponding CPI inflation rate was 0.39 percent; core goods subtracted 0.18 percentage point, core services excluding shelter contributed 0.47 percentage point, shelter contributed 1.08 percentage points, and food and energy subtracted 1.10 percentage points.
