In August 2022, core goods contributed 1.3 percentage points to the one-year non-seasonally-adjusted CPI inflation rate of 8.3 percent (see\cbox{blue!85!black}), while core services excluding shelter contributed 1.4 percentage points (see\cbox{green!60!black}).  One year prior, in August 2021, the corresponding CPI inflation rate was 5.2 percent; core goods contributed 1.4 percentage points, core services excluding shelter contributed 0.7 percentage point, shelter contributed one percentage point, and food and energy added 2.3 percentage points.
