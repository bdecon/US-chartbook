As of June 2021, core goods contributed 1.84 percentage points to the overall non-seasonally-adjusted CPI inflation rate of 6.44 percent, while core services excluding shelter contributed 1.14 percentage points. Shelter contributed 1.10 percentage points, and food and energy contributed 2.41 percentage points. One year prior, in June 2020, the corresponding CPI inflation rate was 0.77 percent; core goods subtracted 0.23 percentage point, core services excluding shelter contributed 0.40 percentage point, shelter contributed 0.98 percentage point, and food and energy subtracted 0.44 percentage point.
