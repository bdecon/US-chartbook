For the week ending February 25, 2023, the Department of Labor \href{{https://www.dol.gov/ui/data.pdf}}{{report}} 1,975,193 \textbf{continued claims} for unemployment insurance (insured unemployed) under state programs (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-week increase of 83,400. One year prior, during the week of February 26, 2022, there were an average of 1,912,200 insured unemployed.
