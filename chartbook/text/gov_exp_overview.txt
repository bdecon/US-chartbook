As of 2023 Q3, government current expenditures total \$9.3 trillion, which is 33.6 percent of GDP (see {\color{black!80!white}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in 2022 Q3, government current expenditures total 33.6 percent of GDP. Government spending peaked during the COVID-19 pandemic, averaging 40.9 percent of GDP from 2020 to 2021. Since 1989, government spending averages 33.3 percent of GDP.
