In the second quarter of 2019, total public debt was \$22.0 trllion, equivalent to 103.2 percent of GDP. Of this, \$7.4 trillion, or 33.8 percent of the total, is held by private domestic investors. An additional \$6.6 trillion, or 30.1 percent of the total, is held by foreign investors. The remainder is held by the Federal Reserve and various government agencies and trusts, such as the Social Security Trust Fund.
