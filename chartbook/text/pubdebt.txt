Breaking down federal debt by holder, \$14.8 trillion, or 42.8 percent of the total, is held by private domestic investors (see\cbox{green!60!black}). An additional \$8.1 trillion, or 23.4 percent of the total, is held by foreign investors (see\cbox{orange!70!white}). The remainder is held by the Federal Reserve (see\cbox{blue}) and various government agencies and trusts (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}), such as the Social Security Trust Fund.
