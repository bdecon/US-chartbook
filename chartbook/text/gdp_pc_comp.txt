Much of the increase in GDP over the past 30 years comes from consumer spending. Consumer spending (see {\color{yellow!45!orange}\textbf{---}}) is equivalent to \$54,628 per person in 2023 Q2, a price-adjusted increase of \$24,505 since 1989.

Gross private domestic investment (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}) is equivalent to \$13,777 per person in 2023 Q2. Government spending and investment (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}) totals \$13,988 per person. The trade deficit, equivalent to \$2,402 per person, is subtracted, to capture only domestic production (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
