In the third quarter of 2023, median usual earnings of full-time wage and salary workers are \$1,118 per week, compared to \$1,070 per week in 2022 Q3, a nominal one-year increase of 4.5 percent (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}). In 2023 Q2, the median full-time worker receives \$1,100 per week, a one-year increase of 5.7 percent.
