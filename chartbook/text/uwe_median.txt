In the fourth quarter of 2022, median usual earnings of full-time wage and salary workers are \$1,085 per week, compared to \$1,010 per week in 2021 Q4, a nominal one-year increase of 7.4 percent (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}). In 2022 Q3, the median full-time worker receives \$1,070 per week, a one-year increase of 6.9 percent.
