In the first quarter of 2024, median usual earnings of full-time wage and salary workers are \$1,139 per week, compared to \$1,100 per week in 2023 Q1, a nominal one-year increase of 3.5 percent (see {\color{cyan!60!white}\textbf{---}}). In 2023 Q4, the median full-time worker receives \$1,145 per week, a one-year increase of 5.5 percent.
