The US resident population is 333.0 million in September 2022, from the latest population estimates, released on December 21, 2021 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}). The 2017-based projections of the future US resident population show a 2025 population of 344.2 million people (see \begin{tikzpicture} \draw[magenta!80!white] (0pt,5pt) -- (10pt,5pt); \fill[magenta!80!white] (1ex,1ex) circle (0.5ex);\end{tikzpicture}). The resident population under age 65 was estimated to be 274.2 million in 2019 (see {\color{green!70!blue}\textbf{---}}) and is projected to be 279.0 million in 2025 (see \begin{tikzpicture}[baseline=-2.5pt] \draw[cyan!80!white] (-5pt,0pt) -- (5pt,0pt); \color{cyan!80!white}{\pgfuseplotmark{triangle}};\end{tikzpicture}).
