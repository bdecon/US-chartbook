During the 12 months ending October 2022, the US generated 4,208 billion kilowatt hours of electricity. Of this, 1,672 billion kilowatt hours were generated using natural gas (see\cbox{blue!40!cyan!60!white}), 817 billion kilowatt hours were generated from coal (see\cbox{brown}), 774 billion from nuclear (see\cbox{{red!80!blue!70!white}}), and 907 billion from renewable sources (see\cbox{{green!75!blue}}).
