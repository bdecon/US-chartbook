During the 12 months ending June 2023, the US generated 4,161 billion kilowatt hours of electricity. Of this, 1,752 billion kilowatt hours were generated using natural gas (see\cbox{blue!40!cyan!60!white}), 717 billion kilowatt hours were generated from coal (see\cbox{brown}), 769 billion from nuclear (see\cbox{{red!80!blue!70!white}}), and 888 billion from renewable sources (see\cbox{{green!75!blue}}).
