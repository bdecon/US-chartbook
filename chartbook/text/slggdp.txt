Combined state and local government expenditures total \$3.2 trillion, or 14.0 percent of GDP, in 2021 Q2. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2021 Q2, however, in 2021 Q1, combined state and local government receipts total \$3.1 trillion, or 14.1 percent of GDP. In 2021 Q1, the combined state and local government deficit was \$12 billion or 0.1 percent of GDP.
