Combined state and local government expenditures total \$3.9 trillion, or 13.7 percent of GDP, in 2024 Q2. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2024 Q2, however, in 2024 Q1, combined state and local government receipts total \$3.7 trillion, or 13.1 percent of GDP. In 2024 Q1, the combined state and local government deficit was \$174 billion or 0.6 percent of GDP.
