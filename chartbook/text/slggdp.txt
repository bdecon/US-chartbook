Combined state and local government expenditures total \$3.0 trillion, or 14.2 percent of GDP, in 2020 Q3. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2020 Q3, however, in 2020 Q2, combined state and local government receipts total \$3.5 trillion, or 17.7 percent of GDP. In 2020 Q2, the combined state and local government surplus was \$470 billion or 2.4 percent of GDP.
