Combined state and local government expenditures total \$3.7 trillion, or 13.8 percent of GDP, in 2023 Q1. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2023 Q1, however, in 2022 Q4, combined state and local government receipts total \$3.5 trillion, or 13.6 percent of GDP. In 2022 Q4, the combined state and local government deficit was \$141 billion or 0.5 percent of GDP.
