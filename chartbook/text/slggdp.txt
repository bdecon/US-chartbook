Combined state and local government expenditures total \$3.7 trillion, or 14.1 percent of GDP, in 2022 Q4. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2022 Q4, however, in 2022 Q3, combined state and local government receipts total \$3.5 trillion, or 13.7 percent of GDP. In 2022 Q3, the combined state and local government deficit was \$80 billion or 0.3 percent of GDP.
