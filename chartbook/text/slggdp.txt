Combined state and local government expenditures total \$3.3 trillion, or 14.1 percent of GDP, in 2021 Q3. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2021 Q3, however, in 2021 Q2, combined state and local government receipts total \$4.0 trillion, or 17.7 percent of GDP. In 2021 Q2, the combined state and local government surplus was \$829 billion or 3.6 percent of GDP.
