BLS \href{https://www.bls.gov/webapps/legacy/cpswktab5.htm}{report} first decile usual earnings for full-time workers of \$587 per week in 2023 Q3 and \$560 per week in 2022 Q3, a nominal one-year increase of 4.8 percent (see {\color{blue!65!black}\textbf{---}}). Over the year ending 2023 Q2, first decile usual weekly earnings increased 6.2 percent.

The more-volatile CPS-based monthly measure shows first decile usual earnings of \$601 per week in December 2023, \$576 per week in November 2023, and \$590 per week in October 2023. The three-month average is \$589 per week; first decile earnings increased 3.6 percent over the same months, one year prior (see {\color{lime!65!green!90!black}\textbf{---}}). By month, over the year ending December 2023, first decile earnings increased 4.3 percent, following increases of one percent in November and 5.6 percent in October.
