BLS \href{https://www.bls.gov/webapps/legacy/cpswktab5.htm}{report} first decile usual earnings for full-time workers of \$596 per week in 2024 Q2 and \$581 per week in 2023 Q2, a nominal one-year increase of 2.6 percent (see {\color{blue!65!black}\textbf{---}}). Over the year ending 2024 Q1, first decile usual weekly earnings increased 3.5 percent.

The more-volatile CPS-based monthly measure shows first decile usual earnings of \$601 per week in April 2024, \$597 per week in March 2024, and \$592 per week in February 2024. The three-month average is \$597 per week; first decile earnings increased 3.8 percent over the same months, one year prior (see {\color{lime!65!green!90!black}\textbf{---}}). By month, over the year ending April 2024, first decile earnings increased 3.5 percent, following increases of 3.2 percent in March and 4.9 percent in February.
