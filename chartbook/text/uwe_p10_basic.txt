BLS \href{https://www.bls.gov/webapps/legacy/cpswktab5.htm}{report} first decile usual earnings for full-time workers of \$581 per week in 2023 Q2 and \$547 per week in 2022 Q2, a nominal one-year increase of 6.2 percent (see {\color{blue!65!black}\textbf{---}}). Over the year ending 2023 Q1, first decile usual weekly earnings increased 8.1 percent.

The more-volatile CPS-based monthly measure shows first decile usual earnings of \$588 per week in June 2023, \$582 per week in May 2023, and \$580 per week in April 2023. The three-month average is \$583 per week; first decile earnings increased seven percent over the same months, one-year prior (see {\color{lime!65!green!90!black}\textbf{---}}). By month, over the year ending June 2023, first decile earnings increased 6.5 percent, following 5.5 percent in May 2023, and 8.9 percent in April 2023.
