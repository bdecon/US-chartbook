As of September 2020, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 1.4 percent, compared to 1.3 percent in August 2020, and 1.4 percent in September 2019. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 1.5 percent in September 2020, 1.4 percent in August 2020, and 1.7 percent in September 2019.
