As of November 2020, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 1.1 percent, compared to 1.2 percent in October 2020, and 1.4 percent in November 2019. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 1.4 percent in November 2020, 1.4 percent in October 2020, and 1.6 percent in November 2019.
