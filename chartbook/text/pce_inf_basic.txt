As of January 2021, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 1.5 percent, compared to 1.3 percent in December 2020, and 1.9 percent in January 2020. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 1.5 percent in January 2021, 1.4 percent in December 2020, and 1.8 percent in January 2020.
