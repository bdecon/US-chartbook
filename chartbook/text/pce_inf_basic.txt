As of April 2021, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 3.6 percent, compared to 2.4 percent in March 2021, and 0.5 percent in April 2020. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 3.1 percent in April 2021, 1.9 percent in March 2021, and 0.9 percent in April 2020.
