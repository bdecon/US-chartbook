As of December 2020, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 1.3 percent, compared to 1.1 percent in November 2020, and 1.6 percent in December 2019. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 1.5 percent in December 2020, 1.4 percent in November 2020, and 1.6 percent in December 2019.
