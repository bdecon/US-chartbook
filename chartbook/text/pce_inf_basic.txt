As of June 2021, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 4.0 percent, compared to 4.0 percent in May 2021, and 0.9 percent in June 2020. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 3.5 percent in June 2021, 3.4 percent in May 2021, and 1.1 percent in June 2020.
