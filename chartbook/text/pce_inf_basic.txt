As of August 2020, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 1.4 percent, compared to 1.1 percent in July 2020, and 1.5 percent in August 2019. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 1.6 percent in August 2020, 1.4 percent in July 2020, and 1.9 percent in August 2019.
