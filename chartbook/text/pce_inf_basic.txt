As of March 2021, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 2.3 percent, compared to 1.5 percent in February 2021, and 1.3 percent in March 2020. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 1.8 percent in March 2021, 1.4 percent in February 2021, and 1.7 percent in March 2020.
