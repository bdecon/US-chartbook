As of October 2021, PCE inflation, measured as the one-year percent change in the overall index, is 5.0 percent, compared to 4.4 percent in September 2021, and 1.2 percent in October 2020. Core PCE inflation, which excludes food and energy, was 4.1 percent in October 2021, 3.7 percent in September 2021, and 1.4 percent in October 2020.
