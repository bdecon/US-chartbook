As of March 2022, surveyed consumers expect an average inflation rate of 3.0 percent over the next five years, (see {\color{violet!60!magenta}\textbf{---}}), compared to an expected rate of 2.8 percent in March 2021. Consumers had expected inflation to average 2.4 percent over the past five years, while actual inflation over the period was 2.6 percent.
