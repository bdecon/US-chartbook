For production and nonsupervisory workers, the highest average hourly earnings in September 2023 are in the utilities industry (\$44.86), followed by the information industry (\$39.63), and the construction industry (\$34.54). The lowest wage industries in the latest data, by average hourly earnings, are leisure and \\[-0.6ex] hospitality (\$18.89) and retail trade (\$20.69).

Over the past year, nine of the 12 industry groups have wage growth above the increase in prices indicated by the consumer price index (see {\color{orange!50!yellow}\textbf{---}}). The transportation \\[-0.6ex] and warehousing industry had the fastest nominal growth rate, at 5.9 percent, followed by 5.8 percent in utilities and 5.5 percent in construction.
