For production and nonsupervisory workers, the highest average hourly earnings in November 2023 are in the utilities industry (\$45.03), followed by the information industry (\$39.97), and the construction industry (\$34.96). The lowest wage industries in the latest data, by average hourly earnings, are leisure and hospitality (\$19.25) and retail trade (\$20.65).

Over the past year, nine of the 12 industry groups have wage growth above the increase in prices indicated by the consumer price index (see {\color{orange!50!yellow}\textbf{---}}). The transportation and warehousing industry had the fastest nominal growth rate, at 6.9 percent, followed by 6.4 percent in financial activities and 5.9 percent in construction.
