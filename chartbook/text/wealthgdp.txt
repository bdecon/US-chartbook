The ratio of US total wealth, excluding public lands, to GDP increased to 5.63 in 2021 Q2 from 3.81 in 1989 Q1. The market value of corporate equities (see\cbox{{magenta!50!violet}}) increased to a 2.8 multiple of GDP in 2021 Q2 from 0.56 in 1989 Q1. The market value of residential real estate (see\cbox{{green!80!blue}}) increased to 1.71 times GDP from 1.33 in 1989. The other category (see\cbox{{cyan!35!white}}), which includes tangible assets other than residential real estate less US financial obligations to the rest of the world, decreased to 1.12 from 1.92 in 1989.
