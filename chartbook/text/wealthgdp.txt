The ratio of US total wealth, excluding public lands, to GDP increased to 5.33 in 2020 Q4 from 3.65 in 1989 Q1. The market value of corporate equities (see\cbox{{magenta!50!violet}}) increased to a 2.51 multiple of GDP in 2020 Q4 from 0.56 in 1989 Q1. The market value of residential real estate (see\cbox{{green!80!blue}}) increased to 1.67 times GDP from 1.33 in 1989. The other category (see\cbox{{cyan!35!white}}), which includes tangible assets other than residential real estate less US financial obligations to the rest of the world, decreased to 1.15 from 1.76 in 1989.
