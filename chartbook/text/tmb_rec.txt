The United States Treasury \href{https://fiscal.treasury.gov/reports-statements/mts/current.html}{report} federal government receipts and outlays in the Monthly Treasury Statement. Over the 12 months ending April 2023, \textbf{Federal government receipts} total \$4.6 trillion, of which \$2.3 trillion are from individual income taxes (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}). The remaining receipts (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}) are largely social insurance contributions (\$1.6 trillion) and corporate income taxes (\$0.4 trillion).
