Over the 12 months ending June 2024, \textbf{federal government receipts} total \$4.8 trillion, compared to \$4.5 trillion one year prior, in June 2023.

Over the past 12 months, 50 percent of receipts, totalling \$2.4 trillion, are from individual income taxes (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}). The remaining receipts (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}) are largely social insurance contributions (\$1.7 trillion) and corporate income taxes (\$0.5 trillion).
