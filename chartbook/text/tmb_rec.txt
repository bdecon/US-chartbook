Over the 12 months ending August 2023, \textbf{federal government receipts} total \$4.5 trillion, compared to \$4.9 trillion one year prior, in August 2022.

In the latest 12 months of data, 49 percent of receipts, which total \$2.2 trillion are from individual income taxes (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}). The remaining receipts (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}) are largely social insurance contributions (\$1.6 trillion) and corporate income taxes (\$0.4 trillion).
