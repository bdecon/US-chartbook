In the second quarter of 2023, nominal GDP increased at an annual rate of 4.1 percent, following an increase of 6.1 percent in 2023 Q1, and an increase of 6.6 percent in 2022 Q4. From 1989 to 2019 Q4, nominal GDP increased at an annual rate of 4.6 percent. Since 2019 Q4, the annualized growth rate is 6.2 percent.
