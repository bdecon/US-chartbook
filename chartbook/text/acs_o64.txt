The age 65 and older population is disproportionately concentrated in Florida. The commuter zone with the highest share of its population over 64 is Port Angeles, WA (34.8 percent), followed by Sarasota, FL (34.6 percent), and Ocala, FL (31.3 percent). The commuter zones with lowest local over-64 population share were Provo, UT (8.3 percent), Laredo, TX (10.1 percent), and Odessa, TX (10.8 percent).
