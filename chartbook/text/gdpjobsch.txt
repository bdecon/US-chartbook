In 2021 Q2, population growth contributed 0.25 percentage point to annualized GDP growth, and, for comparison, added 0.56 percentage point in 2019 Q4. Changes in the employed share of the population contributed 2.92 percentage points in the latest quarter, and added 1.64 percentage points in the fourth quarter of 2019. Changes in average hours worked added 0.83 percentage point to GDP growth in the latest quarter and added 0.36 percentage point in 2019 Q4. Lastly, productivity contributed 2.72 percentage points to GDP growth in 2021 Q2, compared to a reduction of 0.67 percentage point in 2019 Q4.
