In 2023 Q3, population growth contributed 0.58 percentage point to annualized GDP growth, and, for comparison, added 0.58 percentage point in 2019 Q4. Changes in the employed share of the population subtracted 0.24 percentage point in the latest quarter, and added 1.63 percentage points in the fourth quarter of 2019. Changes in average hours worked did not add to GDP growth in the latest quarter and added 0.35 percentage point in 2019 Q4. Lastly, productivity contributed 4.53 percentage points to GDP growth in 2023 Q3, compared to virtually no addition in 2019 Q4.
