In the the fourth quarter of 2020, holding gains contributed 7.8 percentage points to the 10.1 percent change in net worth. Income invested at the 1989-onward average rate of 10.2 percent would have contributed 1.5 percentage points; an additional 1.3 percentage points were added as household net investment was 18.7 percent of disposable person income in 2020 Q4. Other volume changes subtracted 0.4 percentage points. Over the past three years, nominal one-year growth of net worth averaged 6.3 percent. Holding gains contributed 4.4 percentage points on average; net investment of income contributed 2.8 percentage points; and other volume changes did not contribute significantly.
