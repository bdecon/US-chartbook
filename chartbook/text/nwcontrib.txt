In the the second quarter of 2021, holding gains contributed 16.7 percentage points to the 19.6 percent change in net worth. Income invested at the 1989-onward average rate of 10.1 percent would have contributed 1.5 percentage points; an additional 0.6 percentage points were added as household net investment was 14.2 percent of disposable person income in 2021 Q2. Other volume changes contributed 0.7 percentage points. Over the past three years, nominal one-year growth of net worth averaged 8.6 percent. Holding gains contributed 6.3 percentage points on average; net investment of income contributed 2.2 percentage points; and other volume changes contributed 0.3 percentage points.
