As of April 2023, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, five increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, seven decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Since February 2020, paper production increased by 11.3 percent, chemical production decreased by 7.4 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 5.5 percent, and electric \& gas utilities production decreased by 3.8 percent. 

Since February 2020, eight of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Mining capacity increased by 11.9 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 11.0 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products increased by 9.2 percent.
