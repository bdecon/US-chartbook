As of April 2024, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, five increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, and seven decreased production (see\cbox{black!20}).

Since February 2020, aerospace and miscellaneous transportation equipment production increased by 12.8 percent, paper production decreased by 12.0 percent, chemical production increased by 8.2 percent, and production of computer and electronic products increased by 7.3 percent. 

Since February 2020, eight of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!80!blue}). Production capacity for computer and electronic products increased by 16.8 percent, electric and gas utilities capacity increased by 12.8 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 12.4 percent.
