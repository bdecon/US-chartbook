As of October 2022, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, two increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, ten decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Since February 2020, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 26.3 percent, paper production increased by 9.2 percent, machinery production decreased by 8.3 percent, and chemical production decreased by 7.5 percent. 

Since February 2020, eight of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 12.5 percent, mining capacity increased by 8.6 percent, and electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 6.8 percent.
