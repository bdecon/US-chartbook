Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, all 12 increased \textbf{production} over the past year, none decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending May 2021, motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 140.8 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production increased by 27.1 percent, production of petroleum \& coal products increased by 23.9 percent, and machinery production increased by 22.2 percent. 

Over the year ending May 2021, five of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 2.8 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 2.7 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products decreased by 1.7 percent.
