As of September 2021, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, seven increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, five decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Since February 2020, motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 15.9 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 15.2 percent, mining production increased by 12.1 percent, and production of computer \& electronic products decreased by 10.1 percent. 

Since February 2020, five of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, five decreased capacity, and two were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 4.8 percent, electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 4.4 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products increased by 1.8 percent.
