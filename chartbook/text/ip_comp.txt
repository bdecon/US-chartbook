Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, three increased \textbf{production} over the past three years, nine decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the three years ending July 2020, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 19.6 percent, production of petroleum \& coal products decreased by 14.6 percent, production of computer \& electronic products increased by 13.6 percent, and paper production decreased by 11.0 percent.\\ 

Over the three years ending July 2020, nine of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, three decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the three-year period, mining capacity increased by 13.4 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 10.9 percent, and production capacity for plastics \& rubber products increased by 8.3 percent.
