Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, two increased \textbf{production} over the past year, ten decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending July 2020, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 20.5 percent, mining production decreased by 17.0 percent, production of petroleum \& coal products decreased by 14.2 percent, and machinery production decreased by 12.0 percent.\\ 

Over the year ending July 2020, eight of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 4.1 percent, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 2.9 percent, and food, beverage, \& tobacco capacity increased by 1.1 percent.
