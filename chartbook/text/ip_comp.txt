Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, 11 increased \textbf{production} over the past year, one decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending April 2021, motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 439.7 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production increased by 44.3 percent, machinery production increased by 33.1 percent, and production of plastics \& rubber products increased by 25.8 percent. 

Over the year ending April 2021, four of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 3.1 percent, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 3.1 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 2.6 percent.
