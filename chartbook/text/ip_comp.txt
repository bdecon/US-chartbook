Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, two increased \textbf{production} over the past year, ten decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending October 2020, production of petroleum \& coal products decreased by 14.8 percent, mining production decreased by 14.4 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 12.5 percent, and production of fabricated metal products decreased by 7.5 percent. 

Over the year ending October 2020, four of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 3.4 percent, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 3.1 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 1.5 percent.
