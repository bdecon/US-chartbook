As of July 2021, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, eight increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, four decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the period since February 2020, production of computer \& electronic products decreased by 9.1 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 9.1 percent, mining production increased by 8.4 percent, and chemical production decreased by 5.7 percent. 

Since February 2020, six of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, five decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the period, electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 4.0 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 3.9 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products increased by 2.0 percent.
