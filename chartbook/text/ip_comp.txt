Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, three increased \textbf{production} over the past year, eight decreased production, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending November 2020, production of petroleum \& coal products decreased by 14.0 percent, mining production decreased by 12.5 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 10.3 percent, and electric \& gas utilities production decreased by 8.9 percent. 

Over the year ending November 2020, four of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 3.2 percent, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 3.2 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 2.2 percent.
