As of August 2022, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, four increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, seven decreased production, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Since February 2020, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 24.1 percent, chemical production decreased by 8.1 percent, production of computer \& electronic products decreased by 6.1 percent, and machinery production decreased by 5.1 percent. 

Since February 2020, eight of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 11.3 percent, mining capacity increased by 8.9 percent, and electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 6.4 percent.
