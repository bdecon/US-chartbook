As of June 2024, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, six increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, and six decreased production (see\cbox{black!20}).

Since February 2020, aerospace and miscellaneous transportation equipment production increased by 14.5 percent, paper production decreased by 12.0 percent, chemical production increased by 10.3 percent, and production of computer and electronic products increased by 8.2 percent. 

Since February 2020, six of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, six decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!80!blue}). Production capacity for plastics and rubber products increased by 13.3 percent, electric and gas utilities capacity increased by 11.6 percent, and production capacity for computer and electronic products increased by 11.0 percent.
