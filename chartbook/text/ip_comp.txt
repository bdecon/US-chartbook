As of May 2021, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, seven increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, five decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the period since February 2020, mining production increased by 10.2 percent, motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 6.9 percent, production of computer \& electronic products decreased by 6.6 percent, and paper production increased by 5.8 percent. 

Since February 2020, seven of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, five decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the period, electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 3.6 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 3.1 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products increased by 2.1 percent.
