As of December 2023, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, six increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, five decreased production, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{black!20}).

Since February 2020, aerospace and miscellaneous transportation equipment production increased by 13.8 percent, paper production decreased by 13.7 percent, production of computer and electronic products increased by 7.8 percent, and chemical production increased by 6.2 percent. 

Since February 2020, eight of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!80!blue}). Production capacity for computer and electronic products increased by 14.9 percent, mining capacity decreased by 12.4 percent, and electric and gas utilities capacity increased by 11.5 percent.
