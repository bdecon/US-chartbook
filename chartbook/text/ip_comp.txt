As of June 2023, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, seven increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, five decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Since February 2020, paper production increased by 13.1 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 9.6 percent, chemical production decreased by 7.4 percent, and production of petroleum \& coal products increased by 3.6 percent. 

Since February 2020, seven of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Mining capacity increased by 12.0 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 11.9 percent, and electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 9.7 percent.
