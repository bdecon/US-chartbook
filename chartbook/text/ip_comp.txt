As of August 2021, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, seven increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, five decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the period since February 2020, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 12.7 percent, production of computer \& electronic products decreased by 11.3 percent, mining production increased by 9.4 percent, and motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 5.6 percent. 

Since February 2020, five of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, five decreased capacity, and two were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the period, production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 4.4 percent, electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 4.2 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products increased by 1.9 percent.
