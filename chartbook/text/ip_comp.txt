As of July 2023, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, six increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, six decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Since February 2020, paper production increased by 15.2 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 9.6 percent, chemical production decreased by 7.1 percent, and motor vehicles \& parts production decreased by 6.2 percent. 

Since February 2020, six of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, four decreased capacity, and two were unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 12.4 percent, mining capacity increased by 12.1 percent, and electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 10.0 percent.
