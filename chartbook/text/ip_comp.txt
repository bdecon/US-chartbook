As of June 2021, of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, seven increased \textbf{production} since February 2020, five decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the period since February 2020, motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 13.8 percent, mining production increased by 8.6 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 7.8 percent, and production of computer \& electronic products decreased by 7.4 percent. 

Since February 2020, six of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, five decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the period, electric \& gas utilities capacity decreased by 3.8 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products decreased by 3.5 percent, and production capacity for petroleum \& coal products increased by 2.0 percent.
