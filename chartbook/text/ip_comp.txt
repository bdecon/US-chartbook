Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, three increased \textbf{production} over the past year, nine decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending February 2021, mining production decreased by 15.3 percent, production of petroleum \& coal products decreased by 12.5 percent, electric \& gas utilities production increased by 10.1 percent, and motor vehicles \& parts production decreased by 8.6 percent. 

Over the year ending February 2021, four of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 3.1 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 3.0 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 2.7 percent.
