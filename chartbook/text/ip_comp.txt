Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, eight increased \textbf{production} over the past year, four decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending March 2021, motor vehicles \& parts production increased by 29.7 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production increased by 13.2 percent, mining production decreased by 8.8 percent, and machinery production increased by 5.5 percent. 

Over the year ending March 2021, four of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 3.1 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 3.0 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 2.7 percent.
