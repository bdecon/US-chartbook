Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, four increased \textbf{production} over the past year, eight decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Over the year ending December 2020, production of petroleum \& coal products decreased by 13.3 percent, mining production decreased by 12.3 percent, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation equipment production decreased by 8.3 percent, and production of fabricated metal products decreased by 5.4 percent. 

Over the year ending December 2020, four of the 12 industries increased \textbf{capacity}, seven decreased capacity, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{cyan!90!blue}). Over the one-year period, electric \& gas utilities capacity increased by 3.2 percent, production capacity for computer \& electronic products increased by 3.0 percent, and mining capacity decreased by 2.8 percent.
