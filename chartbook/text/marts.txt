According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales totalled \$403.9 billion in April 2020, equivalent to roughly 22.5 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales decreased by 21.6 percent, without adjusting for prices (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased by 21.6 percent over the same period (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), and total \$78.4 billion, or roughly 4.4 percent of GDP.
