According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales total \$620.2 billion in May 2021, equivalent to 33.7 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales increased by 28.1 percent, without adjusting for prices (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased by 7.9 percent over the same period (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), and total \$87.9 billion, or 4.8 percent of GDP.
