According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales total \$649.8 billion in January 2022, equivalent to 32.5 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales increased 13.0 percent, without adjusting for inflation (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased 8.4 percent over the same period, and total \$94.3 billion, or 4.7 percent of GDP.
