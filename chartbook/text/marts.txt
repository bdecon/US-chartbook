According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales total \$639.8 billion in November 2021, equivalent to 33.1 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales increased by 18.2 percent, without adjusting for prices (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased by 12.1 percent over the same period (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), and total \$92.2 billion, or 4.8 percent of GDP.
