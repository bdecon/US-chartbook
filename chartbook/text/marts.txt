According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales total \$540.9 billion in December 2020, equivalent to 30.7 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales increased by 2.9 percent, without adjusting for prices (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased by 19.2 percent over the same period (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), and total \$80.6 billion, or 4.6 percent of GDP.
