According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales total \$485.5 billion in May 2020, equivalent to 27.1 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales decreased by 6.1 percent, without adjusting for prices (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased by 30.8 percent over the same period (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), and total \$86.4 billion, or 4.8 percent of GDP.
