According to the \href{https://www.census.gov/retail/index.html}{Census Bureau}, retail and food service sales total \$561.7 billion in February 2021, equivalent to 31.4 percent of GDP on an annualized basis. Over the past year, retail and food service sales increased by 6.3 percent, without adjusting for prices (see {\color{green!90!blue}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, which include online retailers, have increased by 25.9 percent over the same period (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}), and total \$86.7 billion, or 4.8 percent of GDP.
