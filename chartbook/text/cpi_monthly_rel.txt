Turning to one-month growth, the core CPI, which excludes food and energy, increased 0.3 percent in August 2023, or 3.4 percent annualized, substantially below the one-year core CPI inflation rate of 4.4 percent. The core CPI increased 0.2 percent in both July 2023 and in June 2023.

In August, housing prices increased 0.3 percent, (3.7 percent annualized). Over the past three months, housing prices increased at an average annualized rate of 3.9 percent, slightly below the 12-month rate of 4.4 percent. Food prices increased 0.2 percent in August, or 2.9 percent, annualized, compared to a three-month average of 2.4 percent.

Transportation prices increased at an annualized rate of 36.8 percent in August, and increased at an average annualized rate of 12.5 percent over the past three months. Energy prices increased at an annualized rate of 91.9 percent in August, and increased at an average annualized rate of 33.6 percent over the past three months.
