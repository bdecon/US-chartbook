Turning to one-month growth, the core CPI, which excludes food and energy, increased 0.3 percent in April 2024, or 3.6 percent annualized, in line with the one-year core CPI inflation rate of 3.6 percent. The core CPI increased 0.4 percent in both March 2024 and in February 2024.

In April, housing prices increased 0.2 percent, (2.7 percent annualized). Over the past three months, housing prices increased at an average annualized rate of 4.1 percent, substantially above the 12-month rate of 3.6 percent. Food prices was virtually unchanged in April, or 0.2 percent, annualized, compared to a three-month average of 0.6 percent.

Transportation prices increased at an annualized rate of 8.5 percent in April, and increased at an average annualized rate of 12.1 percent over the past three months. Energy prices increased at an annualized rate of 14.4 percent in April, and increased at an average annualized rate of 19.9 percent over the past three months.
