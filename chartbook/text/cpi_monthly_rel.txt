Turning to one-month growth, the core CPI, which excludes food and energy, increased 0.1 percent in June 2024, or 0.8 percent annualized, far below the one-year core CPI inflation rate of 3.3 percent. The core CPI increased 0.2 percent in May 2024, and increased 0.3 percent in April 2024.

In June, housing prices increased 0.2 percent, (2.2 percent annualized). Over the past three months, housing prices increased at an average annualized rate of 2.8 percent, slightly below the 12-month rate of 3.3 percent. Food prices increased 0.2 percent in June, or three percent, annualized, compared to a three-month average of 1.6 percent.

Transportation prices decreased at an annualized rate of 14.3 percent in June, and decreased at an average annualized rate of 5.9 percent over the past three months. Energy prices decreased at an annualized rate of 21.9 percent in June, and decreased at an average annualized rate of 9.8 percent over the past three months.
