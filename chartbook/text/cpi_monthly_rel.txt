Turning to one-month growth, the core CPI, which excludes food and energy, increased 0.3 percent in December 2023, or 3.8 percent annualized, slightly below the one-year core CPI inflation rate of 3.9 percent. The core CPI increased 0.3 percent in November 2023, and increased 0.2 percent in October 2023.

In December, housing prices increased 0.4 percent, (4.6 percent annualized). Over the past three months, housing prices increased at an average annualized rate of 4.4 percent, slightly above the 12-month rate of 3.9 percent. Food prices increased 0.2 percent in December, or 2.6 percent, annualized, compared to a three-month average of 3.0 percent.

Transportation prices increased at an annualized rate of 2.5 percent in December, and decreased at an average annualized rate of 5.2 percent over the past three months. Energy prices increased at an annualized rate of 5.1 percent in December, and decreased at an average annualized rate of 15.0 percent over the past three months.
