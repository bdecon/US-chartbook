Turning to one-month growth, the core CPI, which excludes food and energy, increased 0.2 percent in October 2023, or 2.8 percent annualized, substantially below the one-year core CPI inflation rate of 4.0 percent. The core CPI increased 0.3 percent in both September 2023 and in August 2023.

In October, housing prices increased 0.3 percent, (3.4 percent annualized). Over the past three months, housing prices increased at an average annualized rate of 4.8 percent, substantially above the 12-month rate of 4.0 percent. Food prices increased 0.3 percent in October, or 3.6 percent, annualized, compared to a three-month average of 3.1 percent.

Transportation prices decreased at an annualized rate of 10.7 percent in October, and increased at an average annualized rate of ten percent over the past three months. Energy prices decreased at an annualized rate of 25.8 percent in October, and increased at an average annualized rate of 28.7 percent over the past three months.
