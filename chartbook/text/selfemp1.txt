As of December 2022, there are 9.8 million \textbf{unincorporated self-employed}, equivalent to 6.0 percent of the labor force (see {\color{purple}\textbf{---}}). Over the past year, the unincorporated self-employed made up an average of 6.0 percent of the labor force, compared to an average of 5.8 percent in 2019. From 1989 to 1994, the category made up an average of 8.0 percent of the labor force.
