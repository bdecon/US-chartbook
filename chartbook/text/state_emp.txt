In November 2020, 33 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 45 in April 2020. In November 2019, 16 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and 12 states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In November 2020, four states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in November 2020 are Nebraska (67.2\%), South Dakota (65.7\%), and Utah (65.6\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (50.3\%), New Mexico (52.3\%), and Mississippi (52.5\%).
