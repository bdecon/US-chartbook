In September 2020, 36 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 45 in April 2020. In September 2019, 17 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and 11 states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In September 2020, three states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in September 2020 are Nebraska (66.5\%), Utah (65.5\%), and South Dakota (65.3\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Hawaii (47.5\%), West Virginia (49.7\%), and Kentucky (52.0\%).
