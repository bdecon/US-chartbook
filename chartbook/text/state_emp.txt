In April 2024, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 19 states. One year prior, in April 2023, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 22 states. The rate is above 65 percent in eight states, in the latest month, and in eight states in April 2023.

The states with the highest employment rates in April 2024 are the District of Columbia (69.3\%), North Dakota (67.5\%), and Nebraska (67.1\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (52.7\%), Mississippi (52.9\%), and Kentucky (55.1\%).
