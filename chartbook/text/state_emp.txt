In August 2021, 29 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 44 in April 2020. In August 2020, 34 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and four states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In August 2021, six states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in August 2021 are South Dakota (67.4\%), North Dakota (67.1\%), and Nebraska (67.1\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.3\%), West Virginia (53.0\%), and New Mexico (53.5\%).
