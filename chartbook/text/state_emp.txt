In September 2022, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 21 states. One year prior, in September 2021, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 27 states. The rate is above 65 percent in eight states, in the latest month, and in seven states in September 2021.

The states with the highest employment rates in September 2022 are the District of Columbia (68.3\%), Nebraska (68.1\%), and North Dakota (67.8\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.9\%), West Virginia (53.2\%), and New Mexico (53.7\%).
