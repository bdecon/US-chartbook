In November 2023, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 17 states. One year prior, in November 2022, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 23 states. The rate is above 65 percent in eight states, in the latest month, and in eight states in November 2022.

The states with the highest employment rates in November 2023 are the District of Columbia (68.6\%), Utah (68.0\%), and Nebraska (67.5\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.2\%), West Virginia (52.8\%), and Kentucky (54.7\%).
