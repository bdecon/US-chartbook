In March 2021, 36 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 44 in April 2020. In March 2020, 23 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and nine states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In March 2021, three states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in March 2021 are Nebraska (66.2\%), South Dakota (66.1\%), and Utah (65.3\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (51.1\%), Mississippi (51.9\%), and New Mexico (52.5\%).
