In October 2021, 23 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 44 in April 2020. In October 2020, 33 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and five states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In October 2021, eight states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in October 2021 are Nebraska (67.6\%), Utah (67.6\%), and South Dakota (67.3\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.9\%), West Virginia (53.5\%), and New Mexico (54.1\%).
