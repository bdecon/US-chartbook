In December 2022, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 24 states. One year prior, in December 2021, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 23 states. The rate is above 65 percent in eight states, in the latest month, and in seven states in December 2021.

The states with the highest employment rates in December 2022 are Nebraska (68.0\%), the District of Columbia (67.8\%), and Utah (67.6\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.2\%), West Virginia (52.3\%), and New Mexico (53.9\%).
