In May 2021, 30 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 44 in April 2020. In May 2020, 42 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and one states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In May 2021, five states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in May 2021 are South Dakota (67.0\%), Nebraska (67.0\%), and North Dakota (66.7\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (52.2\%), Mississippi (52.5\%), and New Mexico (52.7\%).
