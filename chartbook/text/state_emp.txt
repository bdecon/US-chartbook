In June 2024, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 19 states. One year prior, in June 2023, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 19 states. The rate is above 65 percent in seven states, in the latest month, and in nine states in June 2023.

The states with the highest employment rates in June 2024 are the District of Columbia (68.2\%), North Dakota (68.2\%), and South Dakota (67.1\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (53.0\%), West Virginia (53.4\%), and New Mexico (55.1\%).
