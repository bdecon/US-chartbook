In March 2023, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 24 states. One year prior, in March 2022, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 22 states. The rate is above 65 percent in eight states, in the latest month, and in eight states in March 2022.

The states with the highest employment rates in March 2023 are Nebraska (68.2\%), Utah (68.0\%), and North Dakota (67.5\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (52.2\%), Mississippi (52.9\%), and New Mexico (54.9\%).
