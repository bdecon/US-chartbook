In December 2020, 35 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 45 in April 2020. In December 2019, 17 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and 11 states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In December 2020, four states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in December 2020 are Nebraska (67.0\%), Utah (65.8\%), and District of Columbia (65.6\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (50.0\%), New Mexico (52.0\%), and Mississippi (52.1\%).
