In July 2023, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 21 states. One year prior, in July 2022, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 23 states. The rate is above 65 percent in 10 states, in the latest month, and in nine states in July 2022.

The states with the highest employment rates in July 2023 are North Dakota (69.2\%), Utah (68.3\%), and the District of Columbia (68.2\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.3\%), West Virginia (53.3\%), and New Mexico (54.8\%).
