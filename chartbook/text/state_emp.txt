In August 2022, 23 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 43 in April 2020. In August 2021, 29 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and six states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. The employment rate was above 65 percent in nine states. 

The states with the highest employment rates in August 2022 are the District of Columbia (68.4\%), Nebraska (68.3\%), and North Dakota (68.3\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (52.8\%), Mississippi (52.9\%), and New Mexico (53.7\%).
