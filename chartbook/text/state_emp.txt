In December 2021, 23 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 43 in April 2020. In December 2020, 36 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and three states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In December 2021, seven states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in December 2021 are Nebraska (68.4\%), District of Columbia (67.5\%), and Utah (66.6\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (52.8\%), Mississippi (52.8\%), and New Mexico (54.2\%).
