In July 2021, 28 states had an employment rate above below 60 percent, compared to 44 in April 2020. In July 2020, 35 states had an employment rate below 60 percent and three states had an unemployment rate above 65 percent. In July 2021, seven states have an employment rate above 65 percent. 

The states with the highest employment rates in July 2021 are South Dakota (68.1\%), Nebraska (67.8\%), and North Dakota (67.6\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are Mississippi (52.8\%), New Mexico (53.1\%), and West Virginia (53.2\%).
