In December 2022, the age 16 and older employment rate is below 60 percent in 24 states. One year prior, in December 2021, the employment rate was below 60 percent in 25 states. The rate is above 65 percent in eight states, in the latest month, and in eight states in December 2021.

The states with the highest employment rates in December 2022 are Nebraska (67.7\%), Utah (67.3\%), and the District of Columbia (67.2\%). The states with the lowest employment rates are West Virginia (52.3\%), Mississippi (52.4\%), and South Carolina (53.8\%).
