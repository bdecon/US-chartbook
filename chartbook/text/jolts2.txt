In June 2020, there were 5.9 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 6.7 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 4.8 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.9 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 2.6 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}).
