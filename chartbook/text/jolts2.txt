In May 2020, there were 5.4 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 6.5 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 4.1 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.8 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 2.1 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in June 2019, there were 1.8 million layoffs, and 7.2 million job openings.
