In May 2021, there were 9.2 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 5.9 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.3 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.4 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 3.6 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in May 2020, there were 2.1 million layoffs, and 5.4 million job openings.
