In November 2023, there were 8.8 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!90!black}\textbf{---}}) and 5.5 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month, there were 5.3 million nonfarm separations, including 1.5 million layoffs (see {\color{orange!75!yellow}\textbf{---}}), 3.5 million quits (see {\color{green!65!black}\textbf{---}}), and 342,000 other separations.
