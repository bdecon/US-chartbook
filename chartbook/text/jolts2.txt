In August 2020, there were 6.5 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 5.9 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 4.6 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.5 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 2.8 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in September 2019, there were 2.0 million layoffs, and 7.0 million job openings.
