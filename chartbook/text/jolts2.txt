In February 2021, there were 7.4 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 5.7 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.5 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.8 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 3.4 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in March 2020, there were 13.0 million layoffs, and 5.8 million job openings.
