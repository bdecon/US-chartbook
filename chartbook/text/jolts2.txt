In November 2020, there were 6.5 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 6.0 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.4 million nonfarm separations, of which 2.0 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 3.2 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in December 2019, there were 1.9 million layoffs, and 6.6 million job openings.
