In July 2021, there were 10.9 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 6.7 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.8 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.5 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 4.0 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in July 2020, there were 1.8 million layoffs, and 6.7 million job openings.
