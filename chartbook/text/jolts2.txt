In April 2020, there were 5.0 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 3.5 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 9.9 million nonfarm separations, of which 7.7 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 1.8 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}).
