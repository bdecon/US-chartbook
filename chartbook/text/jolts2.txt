In April 2021, there were 9.3 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 6.1 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.8 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.4 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 4.0 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in April 2020, there were 9.3 million layoffs, and 4.6 million job openings.
