In March 2020, there were 6.2 million total nonfarm job openings and 5.2 million hires completed. In the same month there were 14.5 million nonfarm separations, of which 2.8 million were voluntary. In comparison, there are 7.1 million unemployed persons in March 2020. The ratio of job openings to unemployed persons was 0.9 in the latest month, compared to 1.2 in the same month one year prior, and 0.8 in March 2017.
