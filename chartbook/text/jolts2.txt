In April 2020, there were 5.0 million total nonfarm job openings and 3.5 million hires completed. In the same month there were 9.9 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.8 million were voluntary. In comparison, there are 23.1 million unemployed persons in April 2020. The ratio of job openings to unemployed persons was 0.2 in the latest month, compared to 1.2 in the same month one year prior, and 0.9 in April 2017.
