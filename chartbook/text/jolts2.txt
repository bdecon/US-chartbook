In June 2021, there were 10.1 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 6.7 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.6 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.3 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 3.9 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}).
