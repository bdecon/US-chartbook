In January 2021, there were 6.9 million total nonfarm job openings (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}) and 5.3 million hires completed (see {\color{cyan}\textbf{---}}). In the same month there were 5.3 million nonfarm separations, of which 1.7 million were layoffs (see {\color{orange!70!yellow}\textbf{---}}) and 3.3 million were voluntary (see {\color{red!85!violet}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in February 2020, there were 2.0 million layoffs, and 7.0 million job openings.
