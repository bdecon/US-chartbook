The US added 199,000 jobs in December 2021, compared to 249,000 added in November 2021, and an average of 365,000 added over the past three months. US payrolls shed a combined 22.4 million jobs in March and April 2020 and have since recovered 18.8 million jobs (84.0 percent).
 
To maintain a steady employment rate with population growth, the US needs to add around 150,000 jobs per month. During the 12 months prior to the COVID-related job losses the US was adding an average of 202,000 jobs per month.
