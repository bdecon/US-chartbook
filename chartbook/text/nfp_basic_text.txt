The US added 943,000 jobs in July 2021, compared to 938,000 added in June 2021, and an average of 832,000 added over the past three months. US payrolls shed a combined 22.4 million jobs in March and April 2020 and have since recovered 16.7 million jobs (74.5 percent).
 
To maintain a steady employment rate with population growth, the US needs to add around 140,000 jobs per month. During the 12 months prior to the COVID-related job losses the US was adding an average of 202,000 jobs per month.
