The US added 194,000 jobs in September 2021, compared to 366,000 added in August 2021, and an average of 550,000 added over the past three months. US payrolls shed a combined 22.4 million jobs in March and April 2020 and have since recovered 17.4 million jobs (77.8 percent).
 
To maintain a steady employment rate with population growth, the US needs to add around 140,000 jobs per month. During the 12 months prior to the COVID-related job losses the US was adding an average of 202,000 jobs per month.
