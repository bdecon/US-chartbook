Consumer spending is also \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/consumer-spending/main}{reported} on a monthly basis. In October 2023, consumer spending totals \$56,172 per capita, on an annualized basis. Inflation- and population-adjusted consumer spending increased 1.7 percent over the year ending October 2023 (see {\color{green!70!olive!60!black}\textbf{---}}), substantially above the previous year rate (an increase of one percent over the year ending October 2022).
