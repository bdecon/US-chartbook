Consumer spending is also \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/consumer-spending/main}{reported} on a monthly basis. In November 2022, consumer spending totals \$53,451 per capita, on an annualized basis. Inflation- and population-adjusted consumer spending increased 1.7 percent over the year ending November 2022 (see {\color{green!70!olive!60!black}\textbf{---}}), far below the previous year rate (an increase of 7.2 percent over the year ending November 2021).
