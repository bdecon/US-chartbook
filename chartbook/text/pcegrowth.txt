Consumer spending is also \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/consumer-spending/main}{reported} on a monthly basis. In September 2022, consumer spending totals \$52,822 per capita, on an annualized basis. Inflation- and population-adjusted consumer spending increased 1.6 percent over the year ending September 2022 (see {\color{green!70!olive!60!black}\textbf{---}}), far below the previous year rate (an increase of 6.4 percent over the year ending September 2021).
