Consumer spending is also \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/consumer-spending/main}{reported} on a monthly basis. In June 2024, consumer spending totals \$57,725 per capita, on an annualized basis. Inflation- and population-adjusted consumer spending increased 2.1 percent over the year ending June 2024 (see {\color{green!70!olive!60!black}\textbf{---}}), substantially above the previous year rate (an increase of 1.6 percent over the year ending June 2023).
