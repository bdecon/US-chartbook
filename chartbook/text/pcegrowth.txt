Consumer spending is also \href{https://www.bea.gov/data/consumer-spending/main}{reported} on a monthly basis. In February 2023, consumer spending totals \$54,164 per capita, on an annualized basis. Inflation- and population-adjusted consumer spending increased two percent over the year ending February 2023 (see {\color{green!70!olive!60!black}\textbf{---}}), far below the previous year rate (an increase of 6.3 percent over the year ending February 2022).
