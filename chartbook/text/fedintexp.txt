The Office of Management and Budget \href{https://www.whitehouse.gov/omb/historical-tables/}{report} \textbf{federal interest outlays} of \$475 billion in fiscal year 2022, compared to \$352 billion in fiscal year 2021. Put into the context of the size of the economy, federal interest outlays in fiscal year 2021 were equivalent to 1.55 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}), following 1.64 percent of GDP in FY2020 and 1.77 percent in FY2019, and compared to an average of 2.9 percent in the 1990s, when interest rates were substantially higher.
