The Office of Management and Budget \href{https://www.whitehouse.gov/omb/historical-tables/}{report} \textbf{federal interest outlays} of \$659 billion in fiscal year 2023 (the year beginning October 1, 2022), compared to \$475 billion in fiscal year 2022.

Put into the context of the size of the economy, federal interest outlays in fiscal year 2022 were equivalent to 1.88 percent of GDP (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}), following 1.54 percent of GDP in FY2021 and 1.62 percent in FY2020, and compared to an average of 2.9 percent in the 1990s, when interest rates were substantially higher.
