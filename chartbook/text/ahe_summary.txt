Over the year ending September 2023, nominal average hourly earnings increased 4.3 percent for production and non-supervisory workers (see {\color{violet!80!blue}\textbf{---}}), following an increase of 4.5 percent in August 2023. Comparing the latest three months to the previous three months, nominal earnings increased at an annual rate of four percent.

Real average hourly earnings increased 0.6 percent in September 2023 (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}), following an increase of 0.8 percent in August 2023. Real wages increased at an annual rate of 0.4 percent over the latest three months, compared to the previous three months.
