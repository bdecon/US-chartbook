The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In August 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 75.7, compared to 76.6 in July 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In August 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 65.5, compared to 68.3 in July 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
