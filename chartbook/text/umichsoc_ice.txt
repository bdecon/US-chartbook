The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In October 2022, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 65.6, compared to 59.7 in September 2022, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In October 2022, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 56.2, compared to 58.0 in September 2022, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
