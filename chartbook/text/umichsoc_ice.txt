The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In February 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 70.7, compared to 68.4 in January 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In February 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 64.7, compared to 62.7 in January 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
