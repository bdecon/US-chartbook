The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In June 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 69.0, compared to 64.9 in May 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In June 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 61.5, compared to 55.4 in May 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
