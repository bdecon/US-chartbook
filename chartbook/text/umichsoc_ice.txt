The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In November 2022, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 58.8, compared to 65.6 in October 2022, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In November 2022, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 55.6, compared to 56.2 in October 2022, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
