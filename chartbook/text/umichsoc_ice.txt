The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In March 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 66.3, compared to 70.7 in February 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In March 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 59.2, compared to 64.7 in February 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
