The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In May 2024, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 69.6, compared to 79.0 in April 2024, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In May 2024, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 68.8, compared to 76.0 in April 2024, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
