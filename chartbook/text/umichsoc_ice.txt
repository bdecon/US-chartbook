The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In September 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 71.4, compared to 75.7 in August 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In September 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 66.0, compared to 65.5 in August 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
