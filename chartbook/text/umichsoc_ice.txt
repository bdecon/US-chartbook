The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In April 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 68.2, compared to 66.3 in March 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In April 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 60.5, compared to 59.2 in March 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
