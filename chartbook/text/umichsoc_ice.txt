The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In December 2022, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 59.4, compared to 58.8 in November 2022, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In December 2022, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 59.9, compared to 55.6 in November 2022, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
