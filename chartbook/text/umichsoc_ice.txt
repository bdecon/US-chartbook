The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In June 2024, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 65.9, compared to 69.6 in May 2024, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In June 2024, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 69.6, compared to 68.8 in May 2024, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
