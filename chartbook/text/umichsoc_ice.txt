The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In November 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 68.3, compared to 70.6 in October 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In November 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 56.8, compared to 59.3 in October 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
