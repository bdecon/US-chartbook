The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In September 2022, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 59.7, compared to 58.6 in August 2022, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In September 2022, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 58.0, compared to 58.0 in August 2022, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
