The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In December 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 73.3, compared to 68.3 in November 2023, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In December 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 67.4, compared to 56.8 in November 2023, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
