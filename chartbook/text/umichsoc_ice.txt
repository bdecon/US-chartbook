The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In January 2023, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 68.4, compared to 59.4 in December 2022, and 110.8 in 2019 (see {\color{blue!50!cyan}\textbf{---}}).

In January 2023, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 62.7, compared to 59.9 in December 2022, and 86.5 in 2019 (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}).
