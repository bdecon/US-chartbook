In April 2024, housing prices contributed two percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 3.4 percent, far below the category's April 2023 contribution of 3.3 percentage points. Transportation prices added 0.6 percentage point to April 2024 inflation, substantially above the year-prior virtually no contribution. Food prices increased the inflation rate by 0.3 percentage point in the latest data, compared to one percentage point in April 2023.

Medical care prices increased the inflation rate by 0.2 percentage point in April 2024, slightly above the year-prior virtually no contribution. Energy prices make up 6.7 percent of the CPI basket and contributed 0.2 percentage point to overall inflation in the latest data, substantially above a subtraction of 0.4 percentage point one year prior.
