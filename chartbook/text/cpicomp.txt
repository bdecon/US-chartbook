In June 2024, housing prices contributed two percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 3.0 percent, substantially below the category's June 2023 contribution of 2.8 percentage points. Food prices added 0.3 percentage point to June 2024 inflation, substantially below the year-prior contribution of 0.8 percentage point. Medical care prices increased the inflation rate by 0.3 percentage point in the latest data, compared to 0.0 percentage point in June 2023.

Transportation prices increased the inflation rate by 0.2 percentage point in June 2024, far above the year-prior subtraction of 0.8 percentage point. Energy prices make up 6.7 percent of the CPI basket and did not contribute to overall inflation in the latest data, far above a subtraction of 1.2 percentage points one year prior.
