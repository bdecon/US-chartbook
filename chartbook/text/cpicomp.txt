In April 2021, medical care added 0.13 percentage point to overall CPI inflation, slightly below the category's April 2020 contribution of 0.43 percentage point. Housing contributed 1.10 percentage points to inflation in April 2021 and added 0.92 percentage point in April 2020. Food away from home contributed 0.24 percentage point to inflation, and added 0.17 percentage point one year prior.

Communication contributed 0.09 percentage point to inflation in April 2021, in line with the contribution of 0.04 percentage point during the same month one year prior. Energy added 1.75 percentage points in April 2021, far above the reduction of 1.02 percentage points in April 2020.
