In August 2022, housing prices contributed 3.3 percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 8.3 percent, far above the category's August 2021 contribution of 1.5 percentage points. Transportation prices added 2.5 percentage points to August 2022 inflation, substantially below the year-prior contribution of 3.2 percentage points. The energy category makes up 7.3 percent of the CPI basket, but accounts for 23.8 percent of August 2022 inflation. Energy prices increased the inflation rate by two percentage points in the latest data, compared to 1.8 percentage points in August 2021.

Food prices increased the inflation rate by 1.6 percentage points in August 2022, far above the year-prior contribution of 0.5 percentage point. Medical care prices make up 8.5 percent of the CPI basket and contributed 0.4 percentage point to overall inflation in the latest data, substantially above a contribution of 0.0 percentage point one year prior.
