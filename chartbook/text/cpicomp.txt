In June 2023, housing prices contributed 2.8 percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 3.0 percent, substantially below the category's June 2022 contribution of 3.2 percentage points. The energy category makes up 6.9 percent of the CPI basket, but accounts for -39.2 percent of June 2023 inflation. Energy prices subtracted 1.2 percentage points from June 2023 inflation, far below the year-prior contribution of 3.6 percentage points. Transportation prices reduced the inflation rate by 0.9 percentage point in the latest data, compared to a contribution of 3.7 percentage points in June 2022.

Food prices increased the inflation rate by 0.8 percentage point in June 2023, substantially below the year-prior contribution of 1.4 percentage points. Medical care prices make up 8.1 percent of the CPI basket and did not contribute to overall inflation in the latest data, substantially below a contribution of 0.4 percentage point one year prior.
