In January 2023, housing prices contributed 3.7 percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 6.4 percent, far above the category's January 2022 contribution of 2.5 percentage points. Food prices added 1.4 percentage points to January 2023 inflation, substantially above the year-prior contribution of 0.9 percentage point. Transportation prices increased the inflation rate by 0.6 percentage point in the latest data, compared to 3.6 percentage points in January 2022.

Energy prices increased the inflation rate by 0.6 percentage point in January 2023, far below the year-prior contribution of 1.9 percentage points. Medical care prices make up 8.1 percent of the CPI basket and contributed 0.2 percentage point to overall inflation in the latest data, in line with a contribution of 0.2 percentage point one year prior.
