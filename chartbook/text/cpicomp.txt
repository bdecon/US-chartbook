In October 2023, housing prices contributed 2.3 percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 3.2 percent, far below the category's October 2022 contribution of 3.5 percentage points. Food prices added 0.4 percentage point to October 2023 inflation, far below the year-prior contribution of 1.5 percentage points. Energy prices reduced the inflation rate by 0.3 percentage point in the latest data, compared to a contribution of 1.3 percentage points in October 2022.

Recreation prices increased the inflation rate by 0.2 percentage point in October 2023, in line with the year-prior contribution of 0.2 percentage point. Transportation prices make up 16.7 percent of the CPI basket and contributed 0.1 percentage point to overall inflation in the latest data, far below a contribution of two percentage points one year prior.
