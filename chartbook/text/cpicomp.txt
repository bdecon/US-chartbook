In August 2023, housing prices contributed 2.5 percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 3.7 percent, substantially below the category's August 2022 contribution of 3.4 percentage points. Food prices added 0.6 percentage point to August 2023 inflation, substantially below the year-prior contribution of 1.5 percentage points. Energy prices reduced the inflation rate by 0.3 percentage point in the latest data, compared to a contribution of 1.8 percentage points in August 2022.

Transportation prices increased the inflation rate by 0.2 percentage point in August 2023, far below the year-prior contribution of 2.4 percentage points. Medical care prices make up 8.1 percent of the CPI basket and did not contribute to overall inflation in the latest data, substantially below a contribution of 0.4 percentage point one year prior.
