In December 2023, housing prices contributed 2.2 percentage points to the CPI one-year inflation rate of 3.4 percent, far below the category's December 2022 contribution of 3.6 percentage points. Transportation prices added 0.5 percentage point to December 2023 inflation, slightly below the year-prior contribution of 0.6 percentage point. Food prices increased the inflation rate by 0.4 percentage point in the latest data, compared to 1.4 percentage points in December 2022.

Recreation prices increased the inflation rate by 0.1 percentage point in December 2023, slightly below the year-prior contribution of 0.3 percentage point. Medical care prices make up 8.1 percent of the CPI basket and did not contribute to overall inflation in the latest data, slightly below a contribution of 0.3 percentage point one year prior.
