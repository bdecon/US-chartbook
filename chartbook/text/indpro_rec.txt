Looking more closely at recent industrial production growth, the latest one-year growth rate, covering January 2023, is in line with the five-year average, and in line with the December 2022 growth rate. 

By market group, the latest low growth is the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is the result of an increase in energy materials and business equipment and non-industrial supplies that is partially offset by a decrease in non-energy materials. By industry group, the latest low growth is also the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is largely the result of an increase in mining that is partially offset by a decrease in electric and gas utilities.
