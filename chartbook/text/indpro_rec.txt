Looking more closely at recent industrial production growth, the latest one-year growth rate, covering March 2023, is in line with the five-year average, and slightly below the February growth rate. 

By market group, the latest low growth is the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is largely the result of an increase in energy materials that is partially offset by a decrease in non-energy materials. By industry group, the latest low growth is also the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is the result of an increase in mining and electric and gas utilities that is partially offset by a decrease in nondurable manufacturing and durable manufacturing.
