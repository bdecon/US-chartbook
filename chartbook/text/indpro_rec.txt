Looking more closely at recent industrial production growth, the latest one-year growth rate, covering August 2023, is slightly below the five-year average, and in line with the July growth rate. 

By market group, the latest low growth is largely the result of an increase in energy materials, and partially offset by a decrease in other categories. By industry group, the latest low growth is the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is the result of an increase in mining and durable manufacturing that is offset by a decrease in nondurable manufacturing.
