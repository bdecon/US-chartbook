Looking more closely at recent industrial production growth, the latest one-year growth rate, covering September 2023, is slightly below the five-year average, and in line with the August growth rate. 

By market group, the latest low growth is the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is largely the result of an increase in energy materials that is offset by a decrease in business equipment and non-industrial supplies and non-energy materials. By industry group, the latest low growth is also the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is the result of an increase in mining and electric and gas utilities that is offset by a decrease in nondurable manufacturing.
