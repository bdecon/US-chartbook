Looking more closely at recent industrial production growth, the latest one-year growth rate, covering July 2023, is slightly below the five-year average, and slightly above the June growth rate. 

By market group, the latest small decrease is the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is the result of a decrease in business equipment and non-industrial supplies and non-energy materials that is offset by an increase in energy materials. By industry group, the latest small decrease is largely the result of a decrease in nondurable manufacturing, and partially offset by an increase in other categories.
