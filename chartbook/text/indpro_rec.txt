Looking more closely at recent industrial production growth, the latest one-year growth rate, covering April 2023, is slightly below the five-year average, and in line with the March growth rate. 

By market group, the latest low growth is the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is largely the result of an increase in energy materials that is offset by a decrease in non-energy materials and business equipment and non-industrial supplies. By industry group, the latest low growth is also the result of conflicting changes in subcategories. The overall effect is largely the result of an increase in mining that is offset by a decrease in nondurable manufacturing and durable manufacturing.
