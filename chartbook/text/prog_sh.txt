The number of Social Security beneficiaries has increased from 53.4 million in 2010, or 17.2 percent of the population, to 67.7 million in April 2024, equivalent to 20.1 percent of the population. 

Medicare enrollment has grown from 15.3 percent of the population in 2010 to 19.9 percent. Total Medicare enrollment is 67.0 million in January 2024. Medicaid, which was expanded in 2014, and the Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP), increased enrollment from 17.4 percent of the population in 2010 to 25.0 percent in January 2024. The total enrollment for Medicaid and CHIP is 84.0 million people.
