Over the longer-term, US labor productivity growth averages two percent per year. The trailing 20-year average growth rate is 1.6 percent in 2023 Q3 (see {\color{orange!80!yellow}\textbf{---}}). During the 1990s and early 2000s, labor productivity growth was above its long-term average. In contrast, from 2010 to 2017, productivity growth was below average.
