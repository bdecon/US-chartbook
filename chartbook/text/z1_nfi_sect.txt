Levels of net fixed investment vary by sector and over time. In 2023 Q1, household sector net fixed investment, excluding consumer durables, was equivalent to 1.0 percent of GDP, compared to 1.3 percent in 2019 (see\cbox{cyan!38!white}). From 2003 to 2006, during the housing bubble, household net fixed investment averaged 3.5 percent of GDP. Business sector net fixed investment is equivalent to 2.0 percent of GDP in 2023 Q1, and 2.8 percent in 2019 (see\cbox{magenta!90!white}). Government net fixed investment is equivalent to 0.6 percent of GDP in 2023 Q1 and 0.8 percent in 2019 (see\cbox{violet!85!black}).
