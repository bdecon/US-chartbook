In November 2022, the M2 money stock totals \$21.3 trillion. Put into the context of overall economic activity, M2 is equivalent to 83.7 percent of GDP in the third quarter of 2022. During the 1990s, the ratio of money to economic activity was falling (see {\color{magenta}\textbf{---}}). Following the great recession, the money supply has expanded relative to activity. Since 1989, the ratio has increased by a total of 48.1 percent.
