As of July 25, 2024, one troy ounce of \textbf{gold} \href{https://www.lbma.org.uk/prices-and-data/precious-metal-prices#/table}{sells} for \$2,371.25 (see {\color{orange!40!yellow}\textbf{---}}), compared to an average of \$1,949.57 one year prior.
