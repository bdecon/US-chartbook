As of November 24, 2023, one troy ounce of \textbf{gold} \href{https://www.lbma.org.uk/prices-and-data/precious-metal-prices#/table}{sells} for \$1,995.20 (see {\color{orange!40!yellow}\textbf{---}}), compared to an average of \$1,725.40 one year prior. Following the great recession, the monthly average price of gold reached \$1,780.65 per ounce, in September 2011. In April 2023, the average monthly price reached \$1,998.84 per ounce.
