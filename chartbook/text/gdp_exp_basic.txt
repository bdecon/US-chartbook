In the fourth quarter of 2021, consumer spending (see\cbox{yellow!80!orange}) contributed 2.25 percentage points to overall real GDP growth. Private domestic investment (see\cbox{blue!70!black}) contributed 5.15 percentage points to real GDP growth, government spending and investment (see\cbox{cyan!50!white}) subtracted 0.51 percentage point, and net exports (see\cbox{green!60!black}) did not contribute.
