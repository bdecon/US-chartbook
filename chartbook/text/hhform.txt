From 2019 Q4 to 2022 Q3, the average annual \textbf{household formation rate} was 1.4 percent, while annual population growth averaged 0.3 percent. Changes in the number of owner-occupied households contributed 1.3 percentage points on an average basis (see \cbox{teal!68!green!95!black}), and changes in rented households contributed 0.1 percentage point (see \cbox{cyan!65!white}). Over the year ending 2022 Q3, the household formation rate averaged 1.3 percent, of which owner-occupied households contributed one percentage point, and rented households contributed 0.3 percentage point.
