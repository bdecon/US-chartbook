From 2019 Q4 to 2023 Q3, the average annual \textbf{household formation rate} was 1.4 percent, while annual population growth averaged 0.4 percent. Changes in the number of owner-occupied households contributed 1.3 percentage points on an average basis (see\cbox{teal!68!green!95!black}), and changes in rented households contributed 0.1 percentage point (see\cbox{cyan!65!white}). Over the year ending 2023 Q3, the household formation rate averaged 1.5 percent, of which owner-occupied households contributed 1.3 percentage points, and rented households contributed 0.3 percentage point.
