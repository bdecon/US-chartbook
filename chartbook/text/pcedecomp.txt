Real per capita consumer spending increased at an average rate of 1.7 percent over the four quarters ending 2023 Q1. Changes to disposable income added 3.1 percentage points, changes to saving subtracted 0.7 percentage point, and changes to other outlays subtracted 0.7 percentage point. During 2019, real per capita consumer spending increased at an average rate of 1.5 percent. Increased income contributed 3.3 percentage points, and a slight increase in saving subtracted 1.7 percentage points.
