In the fourth quarter of 2023, the market value of government assets, excluding land, is \$29.0 trillion, equivalent to 103.8 percent of GDP. Of this, state and local government nonfinancial assets, such as buildings and equipment, are equivalent to 56.9 percent of GDP (see\cbox{cyan!36!white}), and state and local government financial assets, such as insurance trust funds, are equivalent to 17.0 percent of GDP (see\cbox{blue!75!cyan}).

The market value of federal government nonfinancial assets is equivalent to 16.5 percent of GDP in 2023 Q4 (see\cbox{violet!60!white}). Federal government financial assets are valued at 13.5 percent of GDP (see\cbox{blue!28!violet!90!black}).
