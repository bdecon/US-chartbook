In the fourth quarter of 2020, public/government assets excluding land are valued at \$24.6 trillion, equivalent to 114.2 percent of GDP. Of this, state and local government nonfinancial assets, such as buildings and equipment, are equivalent to 57.7 percent of GDP (see\cbox{cyan!36!white}), and state and local government financial assets, such as insurance trust funds, are equivalent to 18.0 percent of GDP (see\cbox{blue!75!cyan}). 

Federal government nonfinancial assets are equivalent to 17.2 percent of GDP in 2020 Q4 (see\cbox{violet!60!white}). Federal government financial assets have a market value equivalent to 21.3 percent of GDP (see\cbox{blue!28!violet!90!black}).
