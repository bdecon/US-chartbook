In the second quarter of 2021, public/government assets excluding land are valued at \$24.8 trillion, equivalent to 109.2 percent of GDP. Of this, state and local government nonfinancial assets, such as buildings and equipment, are equivalent to 57.2 percent of GDP (see\cbox{cyan!36!white}), and state and local government financial assets, such as insurance trust funds, are equivalent to 18.6 percent of GDP (see\cbox{blue!75!cyan}). 

Federal government nonfinancial assets are equivalent to 17.0 percent of GDP in 2021 Q2 (see\cbox{violet!60!white}). Federal government financial assets have a market value equivalent to 16.4 percent of GDP (see\cbox{blue!28!violet!90!black}).
