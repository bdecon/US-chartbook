Replication of the wage growth tracker using the bd CPS shows matched-observation hourly wage growth of 6.2 percent in February 2022 (see {\color{orange!60!white}\textbf{---}}), and average wage growth of 6.1 percent over the three months ending February 2022 (see {\color{blue!75!cyan}\textbf{---}}). One year prior, in February 2021, three-month moving average wage growth was 3.3 percent. Matched observation weekly wage growth, which is affected by changes in hours worked, increased 6.7 percent over the year ending February 2022 (see right chart).
