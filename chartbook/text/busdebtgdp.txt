As of 2020 Q3, nonfinancial business debt--the debt security and loan liabilities of nonfinancial businesses--both corporate and non-corporate--totals \$17,539 billion, with \$10,903 billion (62.2\%) held by corporate businesses. Over the past three years, nonfinancial business debt has increased faster than overall economic activity. As a share of GDP, nonfinancial business debt increased by 10.1 percentage points to 82.8 percent in 2020 Q3 from 72.7 percent in 2017 Q3.
