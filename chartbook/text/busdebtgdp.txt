As of 2021 Q3, nonfinancial business debt--the debt security and loan liabilities of nonfinancial businesses--both corporate and non-corporate--totals \$18,147 billion, with \$11,388 billion (62.8\%) held by corporate businesses. Over the past three years, nonfinancial business debt has increased faster than overall economic activity. As a share of GDP, nonfinancial business debt increased by 4.1 percentage points to 78.2 percent in 2021 Q3 from 74.1 percent in 2018 Q3.
