As of 2021 Q1, nonfinancial business debt--the debt security and loan liabilities of nonfinancial businesses--both corporate and non-corporate--totals \$17,965 billion, with \$11,236 billion (62.5\%) held by corporate businesses. Over the past three years, nonfinancial business debt has increased faster than overall economic activity. As a share of GDP, nonfinancial business debt increased by 9.0 percentage points to 81.4 percent in 2021 Q1 from 72.5 percent in 2018 Q1.
