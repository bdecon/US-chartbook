The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/news.release/ximpim.nr0.htm}{report} changes in the prices of imports and exports. Over the year ending August 2023, \textbf{US import prices} fell three percent (see {\color{cyan!58!white}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of 4.6 percent in July and 6.1 percent in June. Excluding fuels, US import prices decreased 0.8 percent in August 2023 and fell 0.9 percent in July. In 2019, US import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.3 percent. Excluding fuels, import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.1 percent in 2019.

\textbf{Prices of US exports} fell 5.5 percent over the year ending August 2023 (see {\color{blue!90!black}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of eight percent in July, and 11.8 percent in June. In 2019, export prices decreased at an average rate of 0.8 percent.
