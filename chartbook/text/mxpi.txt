The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/news.release/ximpim.nr0.htm}{report} changes in the prices of imports and exports. Over the year ending June 2024, \textbf{US import prices} grew 1.6 percent (see {\color{cyan!58!white}\textbf{---}}), following increases of 1.4 percent in May and 1.1 percent in April. Excluding fuels, US import prices increased one percent in June 2024 and grew 0.5 percent in May. In 2019, US import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.3 percent. Excluding fuels, import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.1 percent in 2019.

\textbf{Prices of US exports} grew 0.7 percent over the year ending June 2024 (see {\color{blue!90!black}\textbf{---}}), following increases of 0.5 percent in May, and decreases of 0.8 percent in April. In 2019, export prices decreased at an average rate of 0.8 percent.
