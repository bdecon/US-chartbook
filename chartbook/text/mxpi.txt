The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/news.release/ximpim.nr0.htm}{report} changes in the prices of imports and exports. Over the year ending April 2024, \textbf{US import prices} grew 1.1 percent (see {\color{cyan!58!white}\textbf{---}}), following increases of 0.4 percent in March and decreases of 0.9 percent in February. Excluding fuels, US import prices increased 0.9 percent in April 2024 and were unchanged in March. In 2019, US import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.3 percent. Excluding fuels, import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.1 percent in 2019.

\textbf{Prices of US exports} fell one percent over the year ending April 2024 (see {\color{blue!90!black}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of 1.6 percent in March, and 2.1 percent in February. In 2019, export prices decreased at an average rate of 0.8 percent.
