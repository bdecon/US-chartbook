The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/news.release/ximpim.nr0.htm}{report} changes in the prices of imports and exports. Over the year ending October 2023, \textbf{US import prices} fell two percent (see {\color{cyan!58!white}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of 1.5 percent in September and 3.0 percent in August. Excluding fuels, US import prices decreased 0.9 percent in October 2023 and fell 0.8 percent in September. In 2019, US import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.3 percent. Excluding fuels, import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.1 percent in 2019.

\textbf{Prices of US exports} fell 4.9 percent over the year ending October 2023 (see {\color{blue!90!black}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of 4.3 percent in September, and 5.7 percent in August. In 2019, export prices decreased at an average rate of 0.8 percent.
