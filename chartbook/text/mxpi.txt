The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/news.release/ximpim.nr0.htm}{report} changes in the prices of imports and exports. Over the year ending December 2023, \textbf{US import prices} fell 1.6 percent (see {\color{cyan!58!white}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of 1.5 percent in November and 1.8 percent in October. Excluding fuels, US import prices decreased 0.8 percent in December 2023 and fell 0.4 percent in November. In 2019, US import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.3 percent. Excluding fuels, import prices decreased at an average rate of 1.1 percent in 2019.

\textbf{Prices of US exports} fell 3.2 percent over the year ending December 2023 (see {\color{blue!90!black}\textbf{---}}), following decreases of 5.2 percent in November, and 4.7 percent in October. In 2019, export prices decreased at an average rate of 0.8 percent.
