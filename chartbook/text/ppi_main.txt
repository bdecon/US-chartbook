The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/ppi/}{report} prices producers receive for the various goods and services they produce. The producer price index for all commodities (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) increased by 19.0 percent over the year ending May 2021, far above the 12-month growth rate of -6.5 percent in May 2020. Over the past three years, producer prices increased by 1.2 percent per year, on average.
