The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/ppi/}{report} prices producers receive for the various goods and services they produce. The producer price index for all commodities (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) increased by 22.2 percent over the year ending October 2021, far above the 12-month growth rate of -1.1 percent in October 2020. Over the past three years, producer prices increased by 3.3 percent per year, on average.
