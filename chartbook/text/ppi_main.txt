The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/ppi/}{report} prices producers receive for the various goods and services they produce. The producer price index for all commodities (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) increased by 19.8 percent over the year ending July 2021, far above the 12-month growth rate of -3.8 percent in July 2020. Over the past three years, producer prices increased by 2.0 percent per year, on average.
