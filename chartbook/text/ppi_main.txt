The Bureau of Labor Statistics \href{https://www.bls.gov/ppi/}{report} prices producers receive for the various goods and services they produce. The producer price index for all commodities (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}) increased 19.3 percent over the year ending January 2022, far above the 12-month growth rate of 2.8 percent in January 2021. Over the past three years, producer prices increased by 4.9 percent per year, on average.
