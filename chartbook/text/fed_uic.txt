Over the week ending January 1, 2022, there were 900 initial UI \href{https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/DataDashboard.asp}{claims} under the Pandemic Unemployment Assistance (PUA) program (see {\color{blue!50!purple!80!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to 900 during the prior week, and an average of 1,300 initial claims per week over the past four weeks. 

Federal program continuing claims total 191,042 in December 25, 2021 (see {\color{green!50!blue}\textbf{---}}). These include both claims under the PUA program and claims under the Pandemic Emergency Unemployment Compensation (PEUC) program. Combining federal program claims with state program claims indicates there are a total of 2.2 million insured unemployed persons during the week ending December 25, 2021, compared to 2.2 million one month prior, during the week ending December 4, 2021.
