Over the week ending September 26, 2020, there were 650,100 initial UI claims under the Pandemic Unemployment Assistance (PUA) program (see {\color{blue!50!purple!80!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to 615,600 during the prior week, and an average of 702,300 initial claims per week over the past four weeks. \\ 

Federal program continuing claims total 13,634,579 in September 19, 2020 (see {\color{green!50!blue}\textbf{---}}). These include both claims under the PUA program and claims under the Pandemic Emergency Unemployment Compensation (PEUC) program. Combining federal program claims with state program claims indicates there are a total of 25.0 million insured unemployed persons during the week ending September 19, 2020, compared to 29.7 million one month prior, during the week ending August 29, 2020.
