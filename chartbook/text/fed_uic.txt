Over the week ending August 14, 2021, there were 109,400 initial UI \href{https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/DataDashboard.asp}{claims} under the Pandemic Unemployment Assistance (PUA) program (see {\color{blue!50!purple!80!black}\textbf{---}}), compared to 103,800 during the prior week, and an average of 100,200 initial claims per week over the past four weeks. 

Federal program continuing claims total 8,664,156 in August 7, 2021 (see {\color{green!50!blue}\textbf{---}}). These include both claims under the PUA program and claims under the Pandemic Emergency Unemployment Compensation (PEUC) program. Combining federal program claims with state program claims indicates there are a total of 11.4 million insured unemployed persons during the week ending August 7, 2021, compared to 12.8 million one month prior, during the week ending July 17, 2021.
