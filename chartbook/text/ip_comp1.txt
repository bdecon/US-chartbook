Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, nine increased production over the past three years, three decreased production, and none were unchanged. Mining production increased by 28.2 percent in total over the three years ending October 2019. Computer \& electronic product production increased by 14.8 percent, and machinery production increased by 10.7 percent. In contrast, motor vehicles \& parts production decreased by 9.6 percent over the same period.
