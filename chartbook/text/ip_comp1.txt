Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, four increased \textbf{production} over the past three years, seven decreased production, and one was unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Mining production increased by 21.1 percent in total over the three years ending March 2020. Computer \& electronic product production increased by 15.4 percent, and food, beverage, \& tobacco production increased by 4.0 percent. In contrast, motor vehicles \& parts production decreased by 23.6 percent over the same period.
