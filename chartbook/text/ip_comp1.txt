Of a subset of 12 industries that contribute the majority of industrial production, nine increased \textbf{production} over the past three years, three decreased production, and none were unchanged (see\cbox{green!60!lime}). Mining production increased by 29.9 percent in total over the three years ending January 2020. Computer \& electronic product production increased by 16.2 percent, and machinery production increased by 6.5 percent. In contrast, aerospace \& miscellaneous transportation eq. production decreased by 4.6 percent over the same period.
