In the first quarter of 2024, real GDI increased at an annual rate of 1.3 percent, following increases of 3.6 percent in 2023 Q4 and 1.9 percent in 2023 Q3. In the latest quarter, labor income contributed 1.26 percentage points to annualized growth, profit subtracted 0.13 percentage point, changes in indirect tax revenue and subsidies added 0.02 point, and 0.17 point went to depreciation growth.
