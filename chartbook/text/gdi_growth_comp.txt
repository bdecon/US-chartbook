In the first quarter of 2021, gross domestic income increased at an annual rate of 6.8 percent, following an increase of 19.4 percent in 2020 Q4 and an increase of 24.1 percent in 2020 Q3. In the latest quarter, labor income contributed 2.21 percentage points to overall growth, following a contribution of 7.28 percentage points in 2020 Q4. Profit income subtracted 0.02 percentage point in the first quarter of 2021 and 1.32 percentage points in 2020 Q4. Changes in indirect tax revenue and surpluses contributed 4.27 percentage points to aggregate income growth in the latest quarter and contributed 12.78 percentage points in 2020 Q4.
