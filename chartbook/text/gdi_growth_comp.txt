In the third quarter of 2023, real GDI increased at an annual rate of 1.5 percent, following increases of 0.5 percent in both Q2 and Q1. In the latest quarter, labor income contributed 1.11 percentage points to annualized growth, profit added 0.28 percentage point, changes in indirect tax revenue and subsidies subtracted 0.12 point, and 0.26 point went to depreciation growth.
