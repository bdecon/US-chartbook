In the third quarter of 2020, gross domestic income increased at an annual rate of 25.8 percent, following a decrease of 32.6 percent in 2020 Q2 and a decrease of 2.5 percent in 2020 Q1. In the latest quarter, labor income contributed 10.85 percentage points to overall growth, following a subtraction of 10.73 percentage points in 2020 Q2. Profit income contributed 15.61 percentage points in the third quarter of 2020 and subtracted 4.23 percentage points in 2020 Q2. Changes in indirect tax revenue and surpluses subtracted 0.80 percentage point from aggregate income growth in the latest quarter and subtracted 18.33 percentage points in 2020 Q2.
