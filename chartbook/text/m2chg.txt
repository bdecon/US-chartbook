The M2 money stock was virtually unchanged in April 2024, over the previous month, following an increase of 1.1 percent in March, and a decrease of 0.4 percent in February. Over the past 12 months, the money stock increased 0.5 percent (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). The M2 money stock has increased 43.4 percent, in total, over the past five years.
