For those age 16 to 64 with disabilities, the employment rate is 37.8 percent in April 2024 (see {\color{blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-year increase of 2.1 percentage points, and an 11.5 percentage point increase since 2020.

In 2013, during the sluggish recovery from the great recession, the employment rate for those age 16 to 64 with a disability averaged 26.8 percent.
