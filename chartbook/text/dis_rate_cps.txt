For those age 16 to 64 with disabilities, the employment rate is 37.9 percent in December 2023 (see {\color{blue}\textbf{---}}), a one-year increase of 0.9 percentage point, and a 7.3 percentage point increase since 2019.

In 2013, during the sluggish recovery from the great recession, the employment rate for those age 16 to 64 with a disability averaged 26.8 percent.
