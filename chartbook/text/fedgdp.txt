Federal government expenditures total \$6.0 trillion, or 28.1 percent of GDP, in 2020 Q4. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2020 Q4, however, in 2020 Q3, federal government receipts total \$3.7 trillion, or 17.4 percent of GDP. In 2020 Q3, the federal government deficit was \$3,517 billion or 16.6 percent of GDP.
