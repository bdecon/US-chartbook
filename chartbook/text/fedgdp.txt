Federal government expenditures total \$6.4 trillion, or 24.3 percent of GDP, in 2023 Q1. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2023 Q1, however, in 2022 Q4, federal government receipts total \$5.0 trillion, or 19.3 percent of GDP. In 2022 Q4, the federal government deficit was \$1,186 billion or 4.5 percent of GDP.
