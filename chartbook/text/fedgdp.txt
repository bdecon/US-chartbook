Federal government expenditures total \$6.6 trillion, or 28.3 percent of GDP, in 2021 Q3. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2021 Q3, however, in 2021 Q2, federal government receipts total \$4.1 trillion, or 18.2 percent of GDP. In 2021 Q2, the federal government deficit was \$3,344 billion or 14.7 percent of GDP.
