Federal government expenditures total \$6.2 trillion, or 23.6 percent of GDP, in 2022 Q4. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2022 Q4, however, in 2022 Q3, federal government receipts total \$5.0 trillion, or 19.5 percent of GDP. In 2022 Q3, the federal government deficit was \$1,038 billion or 4.0 percent of GDP.
