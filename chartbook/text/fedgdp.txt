Federal government expenditures total \$6.7 trillion, or 23.3 percent of GDP, in 2024 Q2. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2024 Q2, however, in 2024 Q1, federal government receipts total \$5.0 trillion, or 17.8 percent of GDP.
