Federal government expenditures total \$7.5 trillion, or 32.9 percent of GDP, in 2021 Q2. BEA has not yet released receipts data for 2021 Q2, however, in 2021 Q1, federal government receipts total \$3.9 trillion, or 17.6 percent of GDP. In 2021 Q1, the federal government deficit was \$4,182 billion or 19.0 percent of GDP.
