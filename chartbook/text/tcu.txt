In April 2023, the US is utilizing 79.7 percent of total industrial capacity (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), and 78.3 percent of manufacturing capacity (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, the total capacity utilization rate averaged 78.6 percent, and the manufacturing capacity utilization rate averaged 77.1 percent. Total capacity utilization has fallen by 5.5 percentage points since January 1989.
