In September 2023, the US is utilizing 79.7 percent of total industrial capacity (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), and 77.8 percent of manufacturing capacity (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, the total capacity utilization rate averaged 78.6 percent, and the manufacturing capacity utilization rate averaged 77.1 percent. Total capacity utilization has increased by 1.1 percentage points since 2019, and decreased by four percentage points since 1989.
