In December 2022, the US is utilizing 78.8 percent of total industrial capacity (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), and 77.5 percent of manufacturing capacity (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, the total capacity utilization rate averaged 78.3 percent, and the manufacturing capacity utilization rate averaged 76.9 percent. Total capacity utilization has fallen by 6.5 percentage points since January 1989.
