In June 2024, the US is utilizing 78.8 percent of total industrial capacity (see {\color{blue!80!black}\textbf{---}}), and 77.9 percent of manufacturing capacity (see {\color{blue!40!cyan}\textbf{---}}). In 2019, the total capacity utilization rate averaged 78.6 percent, and the manufacturing capacity utilization rate averaged 77.2 percent. Total capacity utilization has increased by 0.1 percentage point since 2019, and decreased by 4.9 percentage points since 1989.
