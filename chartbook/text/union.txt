Over the 12 months ending November 2019, the share of jobs held by union and employee association members averaged 10.3 percent. In levels, there were 14.6 million union jobs, and 127.0 million nonunion jobs, on average over the period. This union membership rate averaged 10.5 percent during the 12 months ending November 2018, and 10.8 percent during the 12 months ending November 2017. Union jobs decreased by 146,000 from November 2018 to November 2019, while nonunion jobs increased by 1,868,000.
