Over the 12 months ending October 2019, the share of jobs held by union and employee association members averaged 10.3 percent. In levels, there were 14.6 million union jobs, and 126.9 million nonunion jobs, on average over the period. This union membership rate averaged 10.6 percent during the 12 months ending October 2018, and 10.8 percent during the 12 months ending October 2017. Union jobs decreased by 168,000 from October 2018 to October 2019, while nonunion jobs increased by 1,954,000.
