Over the 12 months ending November 2021, the share of jobs held by union and employee association members averaged 10.3 percent. In levels, there were 14.0 million union jobs, and 122.0 million nonunion jobs, on average over the period. This union membership rate averaged 10.7 percent during the 12 months ending November 2020, and 10.3 percent during the 12 months ending November 2019. Union jobs decreased by 262,000 from November 2020 to November 2021, while nonunion jobs increased by 3,370,000.
