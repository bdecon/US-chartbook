Over the 12 months ending January 2020, the share of jobs held by union and employee association members averaged 10.3 percent. In levels, there were 14.6 million union jobs, and 127.4 million nonunion jobs, on average over the period. This union membership rate averaged 10.5 percent during the 12 months ending January 2019, and 10.7 percent during the 12 months ending January 2018. Union jobs decreased by 178,000 from January 2019 to January 2020, while nonunion jobs increased by 1,767,000.
