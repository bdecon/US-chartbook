Over the 12 months ending February 2022, the share of jobs held by union and employee association members averaged 10.2 percent. In levels, there were 14.0 million union jobs, and 123.5 million nonunion jobs, on average over the period. This union membership rate averaged 10.8 percent during the 12 months ending February 2021, and 10.3 percent during the 12 months ending February 2020. Union jobs decreased by 116,000 from February 2021 to February 2022, while nonunion jobs increased by 6,741,000.
