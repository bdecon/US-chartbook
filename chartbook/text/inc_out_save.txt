In the national accounts, disposable personal income, or \textbf{after-tax income}, totals \$20.5 trillion, on an annualized basis, in October 2023, equivalent to \$60,898 per person (see {\color{blue!75!black}\textbf{---}}). Personal consumption expenditures, or \textbf{consumer spending}, totals \$18.9 trillion in October 2023, or \$56,172 per person (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). \textbf{Personal saving}, calculated as after-tax income minus consumer spending and other outlays such as interest payments, totals \$0.77 trillion, or \$2,289 per person (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}).
