In the national accounts, disposable personal income, or \textbf{after-tax income}, totals \$20.9 trillion, on an annualized basis, in June 2024, equivalent to \$62,142 per person (see {\color{blue!75!black}\textbf{---}}). Personal consumption expenditures, or \textbf{consumer spending}, totals \$19.4 trillion in June 2024, or \$57,725 per person (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). \textbf{Personal saving}, calculated as after-tax income minus consumer spending and other outlays such as interest payments, totals \$0.70 trillion, or \$2,087 per person (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}).
