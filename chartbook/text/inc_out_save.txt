Disposable personal income, or \textbf{after-tax income}, totals \$18.9 trillion, on an annualized basis, in November 2022, equivalent to \$56,675 per person (see {\color{blue!75!black}\textbf{---}}). Personal consumption expenditures, or \textbf{consumer spending}, totals \$17.8 trillion in November 2022, or \$53,451 per person (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). \textbf{Saving}, calculated as after-tax income minus consumer spending, totals \$0.5 trillion, or \$1,383 per person (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}).
