Disposable personal income, or \textbf{after-tax income}, totals \$19.9 trillion, on an annualized basis, in May 2023, equivalent to \$59,262 per person (see {\color{blue!75!black}\textbf{---}}). Personal consumption expenditures, or \textbf{consumer spending}, totals \$18.3 trillion in May 2023, or \$54,524 per person (see {\color{orange}\textbf{---}}). \textbf{Saving}, calculated as after-tax income minus consumer spending, totals \$0.9 trillion, or \$2,717 per person (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}).
