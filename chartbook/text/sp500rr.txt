According to historical stock market \href{http://www.econ.yale.edu/~shiller/data.htm}{data} from Robert Shiller, the \textbf{inflation-adjusted trailing twenty-year annual rate of return} of the S\&P 500 was 6.8 percent as of December 2021 (see {\color{green!80!blue}\textbf{---}}). Ultra-long-term real returns are currently low relative to the average trailing twenty-year real annual return of 10.1 percent during 1995--2005. The trailing ten-year real return was 13.7 percent, as of December 2021, and 10.7 percent during 1995--2005 (see {\color{blue}\textbf{---}}).
