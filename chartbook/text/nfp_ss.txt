Over the four years ending November 2023, nonfarm payrolls increased by a total of 5,366,000. By sector, combined government payrolls grew by 234,000 (see\cbox{green!85!yellow!75!black}), and private payrolls increased by a total of 5,132,000 over the four-year period. Private goods-producing industries added 599,000 jobs (see\cbox{blue!80!black}), and private service-providing industries added 4.5 million jobs (see\cbox{cyan!80!white}).
