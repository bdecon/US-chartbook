Over the three years ending December 2022, nonfarm payrolls increased by a total of 1,925,000. By sector, combined government payrolls fell by 292,000 (see \cbox{green!85!yellow!75!black}), and private payrolls increased by a total of 2,217,000 over the three-year period. Private goods-producing industries added 316,000 jobs (see \cbox{blue!80!black}), and private service-providing industries added 1.9 million jobs (see \cbox{cyan!80!white}).
