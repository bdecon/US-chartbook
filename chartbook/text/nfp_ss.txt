Over the three years ending January 2023, nonfarm payrolls increased by a total of 4,966,000. By sector, combined government payrolls fell by 116,000 (see \cbox{green!85!yellow!75!black}), and private payrolls increased by a total of 5,082,000 over the three-year period. Private goods-producing industries added 521,000 jobs (see \cbox{blue!80!black}), and private service-providing industries added 4.6 million jobs (see \cbox{cyan!80!white}).
