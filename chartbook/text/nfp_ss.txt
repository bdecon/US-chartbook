Over the three years ending October 2022, nonfarm payrolls increased by a total of 1,869,000. By sector, combined government payrolls fell by 347,000 (see \cbox{green!85!yellow!75!black}), and private payrolls increased by a total of 2,216,000 over the three-year period. Private goods-producing industries added 227,000 jobs (see \cbox{blue!80!black}), and private service-providing industries added two million jobs (see \cbox{cyan!80!white}).
