Over the four years ending June 2023, nonfarm payrolls increased by a total of 5,249,000. By sector, combined government payrolls grew by 98,000 (see \cbox{green!85!yellow!75!black}), and private payrolls increased by a total of 5,151,000 over the four-year period. Private goods-producing industries added 492,000 jobs (see \cbox{blue!80!black}), and private service-providing industries added 4.7 million jobs (see \cbox{cyan!80!white}).
