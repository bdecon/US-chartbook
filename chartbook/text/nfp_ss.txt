Over the five years ending June 2024, nonfarm payrolls increased by a total of 7,678,000. By sector, combined government payrolls grew by 726,000 (see\cbox{green!85!yellow!75!black}), and private payrolls increased by a total of 6,952,000 over the five-year period. Private goods-producing industries added 740,000 jobs (see\cbox{blue!80!black}), and private service-providing industries added 6.2 million jobs (see\cbox{cyan!80!white}).
