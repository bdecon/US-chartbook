In May 2023, the ratio of total business inventories to sales was 1.40, compared to 1.40 in April 2023, 1.33 in May 2022, and 1.43 in February 2020.

The inflation-adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.48 times sales in May 2023, following a ratio of 1.49 in April 2023, and 1.47 one year prior, in May 2022. In 2019, real monthly inventories were 1.50 times real monthly sales, on average.
