In May 2020, the ratio of inventories to sales was 1.51, compared to 1.67 in April 2020, and 1.40 in May 2019. The inflation adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.52 times sales in May 2020, compared to 1.66 in May 2019. From 2011 to 2014, real monthly inventories were 1.38 times real monthly sales, on average.
