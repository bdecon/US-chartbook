In May 2024, the ratio of total business inventories to sales was 1.37, compared to 1.37 in April 2024, 1.38 in May 2023, and 1.43 in February 2020.

The inflation-adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.56 times sales in May 2024, following a ratio of 1.56 in April 2024, and 1.56 one year prior, in May 2023. In 2019, real monthly inventories were 1.55 times real monthly sales, on average.
