In October 2022, the ratio of total business inventories to sales was 1.33, compared to 1.33 in September 2022, 1.25 in October 2021, and 1.42 in February 2020.

The inflation-adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.46 times sales in October 2022, following a ratio of 1.33 in September 2022, and 1.38 one year prior, in October 2021. In 2019, real monthly inventories were 1.50 times real monthly sales, on average.
