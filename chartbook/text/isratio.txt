In February 2021, the ratio of inventories to sales was 1.30, compared to 1.27 in January 2021, and 1.38 in February 2020. The inflation adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.32 times sales in January 2021, compared to 1.37 in January 2020. From 2011 to 2014, real monthly inventories were 1.38 times real monthly sales, on average.
