In November 2020, the ratio of inventories to sales was 1.32, compared to 1.31 in October 2020, and 1.39 in November 2019. The inflation adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.36 times sales in October 2020, compared to 1.36 in October 2019. From 2011 to 2014, real monthly inventories were 1.38 times real monthly sales, on average.
