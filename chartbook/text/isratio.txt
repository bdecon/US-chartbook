In April 2021, the ratio of inventories to sales was 1.25, compared to 1.26 in March 2021, and 1.73 in April 2020. The inflation adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.30 times sales in April 2021, compared to 1.30 in April 2020. From 2011 to 2014, real monthly inventories were 1.38 times real monthly sales, on average.
