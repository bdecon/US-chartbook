In April 2020, the ratio of inventories to sales was 1.67, compared to 1.45 in March 2020, and 1.39 in April 2019. The inflation adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.67 times sales in April 2020, compared to 1.50 in April 2019. From 2011 to 2014, real monthly inventories were 1.38 times real monthly sales, on average.
