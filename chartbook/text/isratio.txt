In December 2022, the ratio of total business inventories to sales was 1.37, compared to 1.35 in November 2022, 1.29 in December 2021, and 1.42 in February 2020.

The inflation-adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.47 times sales in December 2022, following a ratio of 1.35 in November 2022, and 1.40 one year prior, in December 2021. In 2019, real monthly inventories were 1.50 times real monthly sales, on average.
