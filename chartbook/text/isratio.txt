In July 2023, the ratio of total business inventories to sales was 1.39, compared to 1.40 in June 2023, 1.35 in July 2022, and 1.43 in February 2020.

The inflation-adjusted version from BEA shows inventories at 1.55 times sales in July 2023, following a ratio of 1.56 in June 2023, and 1.55 one year prior, in July 2022. In 2019, real monthly inventories were 1.55 times real monthly sales, on average.
