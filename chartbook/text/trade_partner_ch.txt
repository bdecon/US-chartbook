Over the year ending May 2024, nominal total trade increased among 17 of the top 25 trading partners. The largest one-year increase in total trade was with Mexico. Monthly trade with Mexico grew by 4.3 billion, or 6.3 percent. The largest one-year decrease is with Switzerland, with monthly trade falling by 1.8 billion, which is a drop of 21.6 percent. Total trade with all countries grew 4.1 percent over the year.
