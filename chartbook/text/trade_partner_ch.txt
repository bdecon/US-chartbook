Over the year ending October 2023, nominal total trade increased among 10 of the top 25 trading partners. The largest one-year increase in total trade was with Mexico. Monthly trade with Mexico grew by 4.4 billion, or 6.5 percent. The largest one-year decrease is with China, with monthly trade falling by 2.8 billion, which is a drop of 4.6 percent. Total trade with all countries fell 1.5 percent over the year.
