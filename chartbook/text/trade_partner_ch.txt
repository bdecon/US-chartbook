Over the year ending September 2023, nominal total trade increased among only 7 of the top 25 trading partners. The largest one-year increase in total trade was with Hong Kong. Monthly trade with Hong Kong grew by 1.2 billion, or 45.2 percent. The largest one-year decrease is with China, with monthly trade falling by nine billion, which is a drop of 14.7 percent. Total trade with all countries fell 4.2 percent over the year.
