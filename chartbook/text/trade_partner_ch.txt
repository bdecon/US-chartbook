Over the year ending November 2023, nominal total trade increased among 12 of the top 25 trading partners. The largest one-year increase in total trade was with Mexico. Monthly trade with Mexico grew by 3.3 billion, or 5.2 percent. The largest one-year decrease is with China, with monthly trade falling by 3.3 billion, which is a drop of 6.2 percent. Total trade with all countries fell 1.3 percent over the year.
