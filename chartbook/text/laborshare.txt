As of the second quarter of 2020, labor receives 55.9 percent of gross domestic income, and the labor share increased by a total of 2.5 percentage points over the past year. The labor share is 4.3 percentage points above its 30-year low of 51.6 percent in 2014 Q3, but 1.6 percentage points below the 30-year high of 57.4 percent in 1992 Q3.
