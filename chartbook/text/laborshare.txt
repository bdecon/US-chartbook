As of the second quarter of 2023, labor receives 65.2 percent of NDI. Labor's share increased 2.1 percentage points over the past year. For context, one percent of annual NDI is equivalent to \$217 billion, or \$1,390 per worker. 

The labor share is 3.9 percentage points above its 30-year low of 61.3 percent in 2014 Q3, and 2.5 percentage points below the 30-year high of 67.7 percent in 2020 Q2.
