As of the third quarter of 2019, labor receives 53.5 percent of gross domestic income, and the labor share increased by a total of 0.4 percentage points over the past year. The labor share is 2.0 percentage points above its 30-year low of 51.6 percent in 2014 Q3, but 3.9 percentage points below the 30-year high of 57.4 percent in 1992 Q3.
