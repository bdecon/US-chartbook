As of the fourth quarter of 2022, labor receives 64.4 percent of net domestic income. Labor's share increased 0.7 percentage point over the past year. The labor share is 3.1 percentage points above its 30-year low of 61.3 percent in 2014 Q3, and 3.3 percentage points below the 30-year high of 67.7 percent in 2020 Q2.
