As of the fourth quarter of 2020, labor receives 64.9 percent of net domestic income, and the labor share increased by a total of 1.4 percentage points over the past year. The labor share is 3.7 percentage points above its 30-year low of 61.2 percent in 2014 Q3, but 3.6 percentage points below the 30-year high of 68.5 percent in 2020 Q2.
