The Federal Reserve industrial production index \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{measures} the real output of the industrial sector, which includes manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities.

\textbf{Industrial production} increased 0.2 percent over the year ending April 2023, following an increase of 0.1 percent in March. The manufacturing-only index decreased 0.9 percent over the year ending April, and subtracted 0.7 percentage point from the growth of the total index. Mining contributed 0.8 percentage point, and utilities did not contribute.

By market group, finished consumer goods did not contribute to one-year industrial production growth in April. Business equipment did not contribute, nonindustrial supplies subtracted 0.3 percentage point, and materials contributed 0.6 percentage point.
