A monthly index produced by the Federal Reserve \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{shows} industrial production increased by 9.8 percent over the year ending June 2021, following an increase of 16.1 percent over the year ending May 2021. One-year growth in manufacturing production was 9.8 percent in June 2021, and manufacturing contributed 7.3 percentage points to the overall change in industrial production. Over the same period, mining contributed 2.5 percentage points to the overall change, and electric and gas utilities contributed 0.2 percentage point. 

By market group, production of consumer goods contributed 1.5 percentage points to one-year industrial production growth in June 2021. Production of business equipment contributed 2.1 percentage points, production of nonindustrial supplies contributed 1.5 percentage points, and production of materials contributed 4.8 percentage points.
