A monthly index produced by the Federal Reserve \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{shows} industrial production increased by 4.1 percent over the year ending January 2022, following an increase of 3.8 percent over the year ending December 2021. One-year growth in manufacturing production was 2.5 percent in January 2022, and manufacturing contributed 1.9 percentage points to the overall change in industrial production. Over the same period, mining contributed 1.2 percentage points to the overall change, and electric and gas utilities contributed 1.1 percentage points. 

By market group, production of consumer goods contributed 0.5 percentage point to one-year industrial production growth in January 2022. Production of business equipment contributed 0.8 percentage point, production of nonindustrial supplies contributed 0.6 percentage point, and production of materials contributed 2.3 percentage points.
