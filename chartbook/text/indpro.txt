The Federal Reserve industrial production index \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{measures} the real output of the industrial sector, which includes manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities.

\textbf{Industrial production} increased 1.6 percent over the year ending June 2024, following an increase of 0.3 percent in May. The manufacturing-only index increased 1.1 percent over the year ending June, and contributed 0.8 percentage point to the growth of the total index. Mining did not contribute, and utilities contributed 0.9 percentage point.

By market group, finished consumer goods contributed 0.8 percentage point to one-year industrial production growth in June. Business equipment did not contribute, nonindustrial supplies contributed 0.2 percentage point, and materials contributed 0.7 percentage point.
