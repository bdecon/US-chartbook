A monthly index produced by the Federal Reserve \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{shows} industrial production decreased by 8.2 percent over the year ending July 2020, following a decrease of 11.0 percent over the year ending June 2020. One-year growth in manufacturing production was -7.7 percent in July 2020, and manufacturing subtracted 5.8 percentage points from the overall change in industrial production. Over the same period, mining subtracted 2.2 percentage points from the overall change, and electric and gas utilities contributed 0.1 percentage point.\\ 

By market group, production of consumer goods subtracted 0.7 percentage point from one-year industrial production growth in July 2020. Production of business equipment subtracted 1.7 percentage points, production of nonindustrial supplies subtracted 1.3 percentage points, and production of materials subtracted 4.4 percentage points.
