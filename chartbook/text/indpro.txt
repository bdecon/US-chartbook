A monthly index produced by the Federal Reserve \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{shows} industrial production increased by 5.9 percent over the year ending August 2021, following an increase of 6.6 percent over the year ending July 2021. One-year growth in manufacturing production was 5.9 percent in August 2021, and manufacturing contributed 4.4 percentage points to the overall change in industrial production. Over the same period, mining contributed 1.4 percentage points to the overall change, and electric and gas utilities contributed 0.2 percentage point. 

By market group, production of consumer goods contributed 0.5 percentage point to one-year industrial production growth in August 2021. Production of business equipment contributed 1.5 percentage points, production of nonindustrial supplies contributed 0.9 percentage point, and production of materials contributed 3.1 percentage points.
