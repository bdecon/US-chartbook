The Federal Reserve industrial production index \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{measures} the real output of the industrial sector, which includes manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities.

\textbf{Industrial production} increased 3.3 percent over the year ending October 2022, following an increase of five percent in September. The manufacturing-only index increased 2.4 percent over the year ending October, and contributed 1.8 percentage points to the growth of the total index. Mining contributed 1.3 percentage points, and utilities contributed 0.2 percentage point.

By market group, finished consumer goods contributed 0.7 percentage point to one-year industrial production growth in October. Business equipment contributed one percentage point, nonindustrial supplies contributed 0.2 percentage point, and materials contributed 1.4 percentage points.
