The Federal Reserve industrial production index \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{measures} the real output of the industrial sector, which includes manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities.

\textbf{Industrial production} decreased 0.4 percent over the year ending June 2023, following an increase of 0.0 percent in May. The manufacturing-only index decreased 0.3 percent over the year ending June, and subtracted 0.3 percentage point from the growth of the total index. Mining contributed 0.4 percentage point, and utilities subtracted 0.7 percentage point.

By market group, finished consumer goods subtracted 0.2 percentage point from one-year industrial production growth in June. Business equipment did not contribute, nonindustrial supplies subtracted 0.3 percentage point, and materials did not contribute.
