The Federal Reserve industrial production index \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{measures} the real output of the industrial sector, which includes manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities.

\textbf{Industrial production} decreased 0.2 percent over the year ending July 2023, following a decrease of 0.8 percent in June. The manufacturing-only index decreased 0.7 percent over the year ending July, and subtracted 0.5 percentage point from the growth of the total index. Mining contributed 0.3 percentage point, and utilities subtracted 0.1 percentage point.

By market group, finished consumer goods did not contribute to one-year industrial production growth in July. Business equipment did not contribute, nonindustrial supplies subtracted 0.4 percentage point, and materials contributed 0.1 percentage point.
