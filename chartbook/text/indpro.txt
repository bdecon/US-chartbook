The Federal Reserve industrial production index \href{https://www.federalreserve.gov/releases/g17/Current/default.htm}{measures} the real output of the industrial sector, which includes manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities.

\textbf{Industrial production} increased one percent over the year ending December 2023, following a decrease of 0.6 percent in November. The manufacturing-only index increased 1.2 percent over the year ending December, and contributed 0.9 percentage point to the growth of the total index. Mining contributed 0.6 percentage point, and utilities subtracted 0.5 percentage point.

By market group, finished consumer goods subtracted 0.3 percentage point from one-year industrial production growth in December. Business equipment contributed 0.1 percentage point, nonindustrial supplies did not contribute, and materials contributed 1.2 percentage points.
