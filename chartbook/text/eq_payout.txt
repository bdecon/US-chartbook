In the third quarter of 2022, nonfinancial corporation net dividends are equivalent to 3.6 percent of GDP (see \cbox{blue!90!purple}) and net equities issuance is equivalent to 3.0 percent of GDP (see \cbox{magenta}). In 2019, net dividends were 2.9 percent of GDP and net issuance was 1.7 percent. From 1990 to 2015, net dividends averaged 2.6 percent of GDP and net issuance averaged 1.2 percent.
