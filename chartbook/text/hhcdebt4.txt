The FRBNY data show mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totals \$12.49 trillion in the third quarter of 2023, equivalent to 61.5 percent of disposable personal income. Student loans total \$1,599 billion, or 7.9 percent of income; auto loans total \$1,595 billion (7.9 percent of income); and credit card debt is \$1,079 billion (5.3 percent of income).
