According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$10,223 billion in 2020 Q3, equivalent to 57.6 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,546 billion, or 8.7 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,360 billion (7.7 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$807 billion (4.5 percent of DPI).
