According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$10,392 billion in 2020 Q4, equivalent to 59.9 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,555 billion, or 9.0 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,374 billion (7.9 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$819 billion (4.7 percent of DPI).
