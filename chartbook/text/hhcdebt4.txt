The FRBNY data show mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totals \$12.26 trillion in the fourth quarter of 2022, equivalent to 64.1 percent of disposable personal income. Student loans total \$1,595 billion, or 8.3 percent of income; auto loans total \$1,552 billion (8.1 percent of income); and credit card debt is \$986 billion (5.2 percent of income).
