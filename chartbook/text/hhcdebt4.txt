According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$10,764 billion in 2021 Q2, equivalent to 59.6 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,570 billion, or 8.7 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,415 billion (7.8 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$787 billion (4.4 percent of DPI).
