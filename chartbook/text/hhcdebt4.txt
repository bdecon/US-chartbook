The FRBNY data show mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totals \$12.38 trillion in the first quarter of 2023, equivalent to 62.8 percent of disposable personal income. Student loans total \$1,604 billion, or 8.1 percent of income; auto loans total \$1,562 billion (7.9 percent of income); and credit card debt is \$986 billion (5.0 percent of income).
