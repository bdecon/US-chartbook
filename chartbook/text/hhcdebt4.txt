The FRBNY data show mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totals \$11.71 trillion in the second quarter of 2022, equivalent to 63.5 percent of disposable personal income. Student loans total \$1,589 billion, or 8.6 percent of income; auto loans total \$1,502 billion (8.2 percent of income); and credit card debt is \$887 billion (4.8 percent of income).
