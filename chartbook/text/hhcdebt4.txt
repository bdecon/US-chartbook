The FRBNY data show mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totals \$12.82 trillion in the first quarter of 2024, equivalent to 61.9 percent of disposable personal income. Student loans total \$1,595 billion, or 7.7 percent of income; auto loans total \$1,616 billion (7.8 percent of income); and credit card debt is \$1,115 billion (5.4 percent of income).
