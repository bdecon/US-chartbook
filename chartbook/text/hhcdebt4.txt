According to the FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, was \$11.71 trillion in the second quarter of 2022, equivalent to 63.5 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,589 billion, or 8.6 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,502 billion (8.2 percent of DPI); and credit card debt was \$887 billion (4.8 percent of DPI).
