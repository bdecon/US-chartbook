The FRBNY data show mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totals \$12.35 trillion in the second quarter of 2023, equivalent to 62.1 percent of disposable personal income. Student loans total \$1,569 billion, or 7.9 percent of income; auto loans total \$1,582 billion (8.0 percent of income); and credit card debt is \$1,031 billion (5.2 percent of income).
