According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$10,989 billion in 2021 Q3, equivalent to 60.4 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,584 billion, or 8.7 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,443 billion (7.9 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$804 billion (4.4 percent of DPI).
