According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$10,099 billion in 2020 Q1, equivalent to 60.3 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,535 billion, or 9.2 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,346 billion (8.0 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$893 billion (5.3 percent of DPI).
