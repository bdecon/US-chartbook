According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$10,495 billion in 2021 Q1, equivalent to 53.7 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,584 billion, or 8.1 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,382 billion (7.1 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$770 billion (3.9 percent of DPI).
