According to the same FRBNY data, mortgage debt, including home equity lines of credit, totalled \$9,947 billion in 2019 Q4, equivalent to 59.7 percent of disposable personal income (DPI). Student loans totalled \$1,508 billion, or 9.1 percent of DPI; auto loans totalled \$1,331 billion (8.0 percent of DPI); and credit card debt totalled \$927 billion (5.6 percent of DPI).
