Nonparticipants aged 16 and older total 99.5 million in February 2022, and make up 37.8 percent of the age 16 or older population, compared to 36.6 percent in February 2020. About half of nonparticipants, and 19.1 percent of the population, are retirees in February 2022 (see\cbox{green!80!blue!72!black}), compared to 18.2 percent percent in February 2020 (see\cbox{green!80!blue!60!lime}).

Disability or illness keeps an additional 5.4 percent out of the labor force in February 2022, compared to 5.5 percent in February 2020. Students who are out of the labor force make up 5.7 percent in February 2022 and 5.7 percent in February 2020, while unpaid caregivers are 4.9 percent in February 2022 and 4.7 percent in February 2020.
