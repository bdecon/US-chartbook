In October 2020, two states had an unemployment rate above ten percent, compared to 44 states in April 2020. In October 2019, no states had an unemployment rate above ten percent and only three states had an unemployment rate above five percent. In October 2020, 33 states have an unemployment rate above five percent. 

The states with the highest unemployment rates in October 2020 are Hawaii (14.2\%), Nevada (11.8\%), and Louisiana (9.3\%). The states with the lowest unemployment rates are Vermont (2.4\%), Nebraska (2.7\%), and Iowa (3.0\%).
