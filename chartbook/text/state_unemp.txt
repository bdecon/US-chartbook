In October 2021, no states had an unemployment rate above ten percent, compared to 41 states in April 2020. In October 2020, two states had an unemployment rate above ten percent and only 30 states had an unemployment rate above five percent. In October 2021, 11 states have an unemployment rate above five percent. 

The states with the highest unemployment rates in October 2021 are California (6.1\%), New York (6.0\%), and Hawaii (5.8\%). The states with the lowest unemployment rates are Utah (1.4\%), Nebraska (1.4\%), and Vermont (1.7\%).
