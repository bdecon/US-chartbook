In November 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,639,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since September 2006 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 95,000 (6.2 percent) over the previous month, increased by 129,000 (8.5 percent) over last November, and increased by 394,000 (31.6 percent) total over the past five years.
