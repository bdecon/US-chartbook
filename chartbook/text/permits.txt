In August 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,721,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since April 2021 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 91,000 (5.6 percent) over the previous month, increased by 199,000 (13.1 percent) over last August, and increased by 516,000 (42.8 percent) total over the past five years.
