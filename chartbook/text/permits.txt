In November 2022, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,351,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since June 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 161,000 (-10.6 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 378,000 (-21.9 percent) over last November, and increased by 61,000 (4.7 percent) total over the past five years.
