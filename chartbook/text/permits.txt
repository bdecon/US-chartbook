In December 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,704,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since August 2006 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 69,000 (4.2 percent) over the previous month, increased by 247,000 (17.0 percent) over last December, and increased by 495,000 (40.9 percent) total over the past five years.
