In June 2024, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,454,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 55,000 (3.9 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 39,000 (-2.6 percent) over last June, and increased by 139,000 (10.6 percent) total over the past five years.
