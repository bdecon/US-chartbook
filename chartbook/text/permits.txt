In January 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,339,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 2,000 (0.1 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 502,000 (-27.3 percent) over last January, and increased by 25,000 (1.9 percent) total over the past five years.
