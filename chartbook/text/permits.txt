In December 2022, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,337,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since May 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 14,000 (-1.0 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 559,000 (-29.5 percent) over last December, and increased by 33,000 (2.5 percent) total over the past five years.
