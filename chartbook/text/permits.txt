In July 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,635,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 41,000 (2.6 percent) over the previous month, increased by 93,000 (6.0 percent) over last July, and increased by 445,000 (37.4 percent) total over the past five years.
