In April 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,760,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 5,000 (0.3 percent) over the previous month, increased by 666,000 (60.9 percent) over last April, and increased by 602,000 (52.0 percent) total over the past five years.
