In March 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,413,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 137,000 (-8.8 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 466,000 (-24.8 percent) over last March, and increased by 11,000 (0.8 percent) total over the past five years.
