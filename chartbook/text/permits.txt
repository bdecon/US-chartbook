In June 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,241,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 25,000 (2.1 percent) over the previous month, decreased by 32,000 (-2.5 percent) over last June, and decreased by 137,000 (-9.9 percent) total over the past five years.
