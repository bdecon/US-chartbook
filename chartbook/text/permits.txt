In October 2022, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,512,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since June 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 52,000 (-3.3 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 186,000 (-11.0 percent) over last October, and increased by 162,000 (12.0 percent) total over the past five years.
