In September 2022, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,564,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 22,000 (1.4 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 51,000 (-3.2 percent) over last September, and increased by 298,000 (23.5 percent) total over the past five years.
