In September 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,471,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 70,000 (-4.5 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 117,000 (-7.4 percent) over last September, and increased by 151,000 (11.4 percent) total over the past five years.
