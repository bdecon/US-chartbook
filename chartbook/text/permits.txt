In June 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,594,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since September 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 89,000 (-5.3 percent) over the previous month, increased by 298,000 (23.0 percent) over last June, and increased by 386,000 (32.0 percent) total over the past five years.
