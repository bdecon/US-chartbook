In February 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,452,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 98,000 (-6.3 percent) over the previous month, increased by 165,000 (12.8 percent) over last February, and increased by 321,000 (28.4 percent) total over the past five years.
