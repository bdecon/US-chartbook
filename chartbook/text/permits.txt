In December 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,495,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 28,000 (1.9 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, increased by 86,000 (6.1 percent) over last December, and increased by 175,000 (13.3 percent) total over the past five years.
