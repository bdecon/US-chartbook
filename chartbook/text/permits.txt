In June 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,440,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 56,000 (-3.7 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 261,000 (-15.3 percent) over last June, and increased by 102,000 (7.6 percent) total over the past five years.
