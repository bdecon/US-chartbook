In February 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,550,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since September 2022 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 211,000 (15.8 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 307,000 (-16.5 percent) over last February, and increased by 208,000 (15.5 percent) total over the past five years.
