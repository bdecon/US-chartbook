In October 2022, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,526,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since August 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 38,000 (-2.4 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 172,000 (-10.1 percent) over last October, and increased by 176,000 (13.0 percent) total over the past five years.
