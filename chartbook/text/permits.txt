In February 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,720,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 166,000 (-8.8 percent) over the previous month, increased by 282,000 (19.6 percent) over last February, and increased by 534,000 (45.0 percent) total over the past five years.
