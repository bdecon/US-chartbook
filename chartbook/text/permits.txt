In July 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,495,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since January 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 237,000 (18.8 percent) over the previous month, increased by 129,000 (9.4 percent) over last July, and increased by 356,000 (31.3 percent) total over the past five years.
