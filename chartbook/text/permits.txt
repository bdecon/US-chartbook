In January 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual total of 1,550,000 new residential building permits were issued, the highest level since March 2007. Permits issued increased by 130,000 (9.2 percent) over the previous month, increased by 234,000 (17.8 percent) over last January, and increased by 493,000 (46.6 percent) total over the past five years.
