In September 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,586,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since August 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 135,000 (-7.8 percent) over the previous month, decreased by 3,000 (-0.2 percent) over last September, and increased by 306,000 (23.9 percent) total over the past five years.
