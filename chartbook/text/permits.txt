In October 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,487,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 16,000 (1.1 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 68,000 (-4.4 percent) over last October, and increased by 207,000 (16.2 percent) total over the past five years.
