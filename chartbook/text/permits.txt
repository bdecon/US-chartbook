In July 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,443,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 2,000 (0.1 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 215,000 (-13.0 percent) over last July, and increased by 110,000 (8.3 percent) total over the past five years.
