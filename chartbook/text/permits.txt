In March 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,766,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 46,000 (2.7 percent) over the previous month, increased by 410,000 (30.2 percent) over last March, and increased by 647,000 (57.8 percent) total over the past five years.
