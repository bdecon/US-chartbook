In April 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,416,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 21,000 (-1.5 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 379,000 (-21.1 percent) over last April, and increased by 51,000 (3.7 percent) total over the past five years.
