In October 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,498,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 27,000 (1.8 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 57,000 (-3.7 percent) over last October, and increased by 218,000 (17.0 percent) total over the past five years.
