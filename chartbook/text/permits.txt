In September 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,545,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since March 2007 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 69,000 (4.7 percent) over the previous month, increased by 108,000 (7.5 percent) over last September, and increased by 402,000 (35.2 percent) total over the past five years.
