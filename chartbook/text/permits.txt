In August 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,476,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 7,000 (-0.5 percent) over the previous month, increased by 5,000 (0.3 percent) over last August, and increased by 311,000 (26.7 percent) total over the past five years.
