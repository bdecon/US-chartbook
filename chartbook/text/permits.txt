In June 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,598,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since October 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 85,000 (-5.1 percent) over the previous month, increased by 302,000 (23.3 percent) over last June, and increased by 390,000 (32.3 percent) total over the past five years.
