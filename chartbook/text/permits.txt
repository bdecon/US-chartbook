In November 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,635,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since December 2006 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 91,000 (5.9 percent) over the previous month, increased by 125,000 (8.3 percent) over last November, and increased by 390,000 (31.3 percent) total over the past five years.
