In September 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,553,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since March 2007 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 77,000 (5.2 percent) over the previous month, increased by 116,000 (8.1 percent) over last September, and increased by 410,000 (35.9 percent) total over the past five years.
