In October 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,653,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 67,000 (4.2 percent) over the previous month, increased by 58,000 (3.6 percent) over last October, and increased by 375,000 (29.3 percent) total over the past five years.
