In October 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,545,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued were virtually unchanged over the previous month, increased by 42,000 (2.8 percent) over last October, and increased by 387,000 (33.4 percent) total over the past five years.
