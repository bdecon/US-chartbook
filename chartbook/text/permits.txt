In November 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,712,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 59,000 (3.6 percent) over the previous month, increased by 16,000 (0.9 percent) over last November, and increased by 476,000 (38.5 percent) total over the past five years.
