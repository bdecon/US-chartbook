In April 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,074,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since January 2015 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 282,000 (-20.8 percent) over the previous month, decreased by 256,000 (-19.2 percent) over last April, and decreased by 87,000 (-7.5 percent) total over the past five years.
