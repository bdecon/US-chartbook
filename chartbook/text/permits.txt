In May 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,220,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 154,000 (14.4 percent) over the previous month, decreased by 118,000 (-8.8 percent) over last May, and decreased by 55,000 (-4.3 percent) total over the past five years.
