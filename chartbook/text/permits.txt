In March 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,430,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 120,000 (-7.7 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 449,000 (-23.9 percent) over last March, and increased by 28,000 (2.0 percent) total over the past five years.
