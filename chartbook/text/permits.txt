In May 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,681,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since October 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 52,000 (-3.0 percent) over the previous month, increased by 435,000 (34.9 percent) over last May, and increased by 485,000 (40.6 percent) total over the past five years.
