In September 2021, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,589,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the lowest level since September 2020 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 132,000 (-7.7 percent) over the previous month, were virtually unchanged over last September, and increased by 309,000 (24.1 percent) total over the past five years.
