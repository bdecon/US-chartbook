In December 2020, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,709,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since August 2006 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 74,000 (4.5 percent) over the previous month, increased by 252,000 (17.3 percent) over last December, and increased by 500,000 (41.4 percent) total over the past five years.
