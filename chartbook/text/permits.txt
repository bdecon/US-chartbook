In August 2023, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,541,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits, the highest level since October 2022 (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued increased by 98,000 (6.8 percent) (annualized) over the previous month, decreased by 45,000 (-2.8 percent) over last August, and increased by 248,000 (19.2 percent) total over the past five years.
