In February 2022, a seasonally-adjusted annual rate of 1,859,000 new residential housing units were authorized by building permits (see {\color{blue!70!green}\textbf{---}}). Permits issued decreased by 36,000 (-1.9 percent) over the previous month, increased by 133,000 (7.7 percent) over last February, and increased by 619,000 (49.9 percent) total over the past five years.
