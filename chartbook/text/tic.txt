Over the year ending January 2023, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$746 billion of US treasury bonds, equivalent to 2.8 percent of US GDP (see \cbox{black!25!white}). Over the same period, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$294 billion of US agency bonds, (see \cbox{orange!30!yellow!90!white}), and a net buyer of \$163 billion of US corporate bonds, (see \cbox{blue!70!white}).
