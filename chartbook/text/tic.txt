Over the year ending May 2024, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$512 billion of US treasury bonds, equivalent to 1.8 percent of US GDP (see\cbox{black!25!white}). Over the same period, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$90 billion of US agency bonds, (see\cbox{orange!30!yellow!90!white}), and a net buyer of \$329 billion of US corporate bonds, (see\cbox{blue!70!white}).
