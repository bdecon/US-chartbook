Over the year ending December 2022, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$768 billion of US treasury bonds, equivalent to 2.9 percent of US GDP (see \cbox{black!25!white}). Over the same period, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$310 billion of US agency bonds, (see \cbox{orange!30!yellow!90!white}), and a net buyer of \$167 billion of US corporate bonds, (see \cbox{blue!70!white}).
