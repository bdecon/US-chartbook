Over the year ending March 2024, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$526 billion of US treasury bonds, equivalent to 1.9 percent of US GDP (see\cbox{black!25!white}). Over the same period, the rest of the world was a net buyer of \$135 billion of US agency bonds, (see\cbox{orange!30!yellow!90!white}), and a net buyer of \$351 billion of US corporate bonds, (see\cbox{blue!70!white}).
