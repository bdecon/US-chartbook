In the second quarter of 2024, annualized US \textbf{gross fixed investment}, both public and private, totals \$6.1 trillion, or 21.4 percent of GDP (see {\color{black}\textbf{---}}). Gross fixed investment is equivalent to 21.2 percent of GDP one year prior, in 2023 Q2, and averages 21.4 percent of GDP in 2019.
