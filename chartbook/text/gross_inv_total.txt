In the third quarter of 2023, annualized US \textbf{gross fixed investment}, both public and private, totals \$5.8 trillion, or 21.1 percent of GDP (see {\color{black}\textbf{---}}). Gross fixed investment is equivalent to 21.3 percent of GDP one year prior, in 2022 Q3, and averages 21.4 percent of GDP in 2019.
