Changes in retail and food services sales can indicate shifts in consumer behavior, though these figures are also affected by changes in inflation rates. Retail and food services \textbf{sales growth} was 6.5 percent over the year ending November 2022. The three-month moving average growth rate was 7.7 percent (see {\color{green!60!black}\textbf{---}}). Nonstore sales, for example from online retailers, have increased at a faster rate than other sales, since 1992. Over the past three months, one-year nonstore sales growth averaged 10.0 percent (see {\color{blue!70!black}\textbf{---}}).
