Consolidated state and local government expenditures total \$3.0 trillion, or 14.0 percent of GDP, in 2020 Q1, and receipts total \$2.8 trillion, equivalent to 12.9 percent of GDP. The combined state and local government deficit was \$229 billion or 1.06 percent of GDP. Over the past three years, the expenditures to GDP ratio decreased by a total of 0.30 percentage point at the consolidated state and local level, and the ratio of receipts to GDP has decreased by a total of 0.02 percentage point, causing the deficit to shrink by 0.28 percent of GDP.
