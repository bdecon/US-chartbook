According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, US import prices (see {\color{cyan!85!yellow}\textbf{---}}) decreased by 6.8 percent over the 12 months ending April 2020, the lowest level since December 2015. Import prices decreased by 4.2 percent over the 12 months ending the month prior (March 2020). The latest value is far below the past-three-year average growth rate of 0.8 percent.
