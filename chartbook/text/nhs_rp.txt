The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In October 2022, the median new home sold for \$493,000 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$544,000. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has increased 7.1 percent over the past year, and increased 32.0 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 77.3 percent.
