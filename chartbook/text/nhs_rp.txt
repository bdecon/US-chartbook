The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In September 2022, the median new home sold for \$470,600 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$517,700. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has increased 5.3 percent over the past year, and increased 29.0 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 69.9 percent.
