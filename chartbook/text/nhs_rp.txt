The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In November 2022, the median new home sold for \$471,200 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$543,600. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has increased 2.2 percent over the past year, and increased 24.1 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 69.6 percent.
