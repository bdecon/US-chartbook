The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In February 2023, the median new home sold for \$438,200 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$498,700. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased 3.3 percent over the past year, and increased 13.6 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 56.1 percent.
