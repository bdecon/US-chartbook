The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In June 2024, the median new home sold for \$417,300 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$487,200. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased three percent over the past year, and increased 5.2 percent over the past four years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 42.3 percent.
