The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In March 2023, the median new home sold for \$449,800 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$562,400. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased 1.7 percent over the past year, and increased 17.2 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 59.7 percent.
