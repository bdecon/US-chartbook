The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In January 2023, the median new home sold for \$427,500 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$474,400. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased 6.7 percent over the past year, and increased 12.1 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 53.1 percent.
