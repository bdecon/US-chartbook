The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In August 2023, the median new home sold for \$430,300 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$514,000. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased 5.7 percent over the past year, and increased ten percent over the past four years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 50.2 percent.
