The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In May 2023, the median new home sold for \$416,300 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$487,300. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased 11.2 percent over the past year, and increased 10.7 percent over the past three years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 46.7 percent.
