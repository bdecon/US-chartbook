The Census Bureau also tracks the \textbf{sales price} of new single-family homes. In July 2023, the median new home sold for \$436,700 (see {\color{violet}\textbf{---}}), and the average sales price was \$513,000. The inflation-adjusted median sales price has decreased 11.5 percent over the past year, and increased 18.9 percent over the past four years. Since 1989, the inflation-adjusted median new home sales price increased 53.1 percent.
