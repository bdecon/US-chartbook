As of December 8, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 19.9 percent. The information technology sector returned 52.0 percent, the communication services sector returned 49.0 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 36.2 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 10.9 percent.
