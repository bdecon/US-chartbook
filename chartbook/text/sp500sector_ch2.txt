As of November 10, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 15.0 percent. The information technology sector returned 47.0 percent, the communication services sector returned 45.5 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 27.4 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 14.7 percent.
