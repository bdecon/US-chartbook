As of October 27, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 7.2 percent. The communication services sector returned 33.6 percent, the information technology sector returned 31.4 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 17.7 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 16.8 percent.
