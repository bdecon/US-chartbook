As of November 24, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 18.7 percent. The communication services sector returned 50.7 percent, the information technology sector returned 50.4 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 32.7 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 11.7 percent.
