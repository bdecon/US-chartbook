As of October 11, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 14.0 percent. The communication services sector returned 46.9 percent, the information technology sector returned 39.9 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 27.6 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 15.7 percent.
