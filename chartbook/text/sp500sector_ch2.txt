As of November 3, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 13.5 percent. The communication services sector returned 42.4 percent, the information technology sector returned 40.4 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 26.2 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 12.5 percent.
