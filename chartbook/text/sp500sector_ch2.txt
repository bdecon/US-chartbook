As of November 20, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 18.1 percent. The information technology sector returned 50.9 percent, the communication services sector returned 49.9 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 31.9 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 12.4 percent.
