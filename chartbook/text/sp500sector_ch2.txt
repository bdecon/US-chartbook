As of November 1, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 10.1 percent. The communication services sector returned 38.2 percent, the information technology sector returned 35.4 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 20.7 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 14.9 percent.
