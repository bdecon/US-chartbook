As of October 31, 2023, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 9.2 percent. The communication services sector returned 36.6 percent, the information technology sector returned 33.7 percent, and the consumer discretionary sector gained 20.0 percent. The largest loss is in the utilities sector, with a total decrease of 15.5 percent.
