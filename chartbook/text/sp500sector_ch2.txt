As of June 11, 2024, the \textbf{year-to-date total return} for the S\&P 500 is 12.1 percent. The communication services sector returned 22.7 percent, and the information technology sector returned 22.6 percent. The consumer discretionary sector gained 41.0 percent. The largest loss is in the real estate sector, with a total decrease of 5.1 percent.
