US imports of goods and services total \$239.0 billion in August 2020, compared to \$199.7 billion in May, and slightly below the year ago value, \$261.3 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$171.9 billion in August 2020, \$141.8 billion in May, and \$210.5 billion in August 2019. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$67.1 billion in August 2020, \$57.9 billion in May, and \$50.8 billion in August 2019. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
