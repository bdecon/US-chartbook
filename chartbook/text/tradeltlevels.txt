US imports of goods and services total \$240.2 billion in September 2020, compared to \$199.7 billion in May, and slightly below the year ago value, \$257.0 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$176.4 billion in September 2020, \$141.8 billion in May, and \$209.2 billion in September 2019. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$63.9 billion in September 2020, \$57.9 billion in May, and \$47.8 billion in September 2019. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
