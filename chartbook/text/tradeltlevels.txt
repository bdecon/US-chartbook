US imports of goods and services total \$287.0 billion in August 2021, compared to \$201.0 billion in May 2020, and substantially above the year ago value, \$238.0 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$213.7 billion in August 2021, \$146.1 billion in May 2020, and \$174.3 billion in August 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$73.3 billion in August 2021, \$54.9 billion in May 2020, and \$63.7 billion in August 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
