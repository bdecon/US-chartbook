US imports of goods and services total \$282.9 billion in July 2021, compared to \$201.0 billion in May 2020, and far above the year ago value, \$231.7 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$212.8 billion in July 2021, \$146.1 billion in May 2020, and \$170.9 billion in July 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$70.1 billion in July 2021, \$54.9 billion in May 2020, and \$60.7 billion in July 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
