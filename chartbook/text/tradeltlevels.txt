US imports of goods and services total \$258.3 billion in February 2021, compared to \$200.0 billion in May 2020, and slightly above the year ago value, \$246.1 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$187.2 billion in February 2021, \$143.7 billion in May 2020, and \$208.1 billion in February 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$71.1 billion in February 2021, \$56.3 billion in May 2020, and \$38.0 billion in February 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
