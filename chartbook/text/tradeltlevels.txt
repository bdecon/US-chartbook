US imports of goods and services total \$288.5 billion in September 2021, compared to \$201.0 billion in May 2020, and substantially above the year ago value, \$240.7 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$207.6 billion in September 2021, \$146.1 billion in May 2020, and \$178.1 billion in September 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$80.9 billion in September 2021, \$54.9 billion in May 2020, and \$62.6 billion in September 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
