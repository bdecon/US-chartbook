US imports of goods and services total \$304.4 billion in November 2021, compared to \$201.0 billion in May 2020, and far above the year ago value, \$252.5 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$224.2 billion in November 2021, \$146.1 billion in May 2020, and \$185.2 billion in November 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$80.2 billion in November 2021, \$54.9 billion in May 2020, and \$67.3 billion in November 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
