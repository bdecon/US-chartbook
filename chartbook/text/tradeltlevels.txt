In the latest month, exports are equivalent to \$771 per capita or \$1,605 per worker. 

Spending on imports exceeds payments received for exports, resulting in a trade deficit. In October, the trade deficit was \$64.3 billion, following \$61.2 billion in September (see {\color{red}\textbf{---}}).
