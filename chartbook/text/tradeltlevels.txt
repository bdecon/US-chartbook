US imports of goods and services total \$274.5 billion in March 2021, compared to \$200.0 billion in May 2020, and substantially above the year ago value, \$232.4 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$200.0 billion in March 2021, \$143.7 billion in May 2020, and \$185.1 billion in March 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$74.4 billion in March 2021, \$56.3 billion in May 2020, and \$47.2 billion in March 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
