US imports of goods and services total \$290.7 billion in October 2021, compared to \$201.0 billion in May 2020, and substantially above the year ago value, \$246.4 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$223.6 billion in October 2021, \$146.1 billion in May 2020, and \$182.7 billion in October 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$67.1 billion in October 2021, \$54.9 billion in May 2020, and \$63.7 billion in October 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
