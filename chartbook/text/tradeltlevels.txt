US imports of goods and services total \$273.9 billion in April 2021, compared to \$201.0 billion in May 2020, and far above the year ago value, \$203.0 billion. Exports of goods and services are \$205.0 billion in April 2021, \$146.1 billion in May 2020, and \$150.1 billion in April 2020. The trade balance, exports minus imports, shows a trade deficit of \$68.9 billion in April 2021, \$54.9 billion in May 2020, and \$53.0 billion in April 2020. These values are seasonally adjusted but not adjusted for changes in prices or population.
